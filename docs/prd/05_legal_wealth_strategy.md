# Wealth Protection & Legal Strategy (Alam.Duit.co.id)

## 1. Entity Architecture
- **Individual Layer:** Operational daily income (< 500M/year) to utilize 0% tax for MSMEs.
- **PT Perorangan Layer:** Formal contracts and depreciating assets (cars) to avoid progressive personal taxes.
- **Foundation (Yayasan) Layer:** Holding SHM (Freehold) property assets to separate them from business risks and inheritance disputes.

## 2. Asset Diversification
- **Physical Gold:** P2P acquisition to stay under digital tracking thresholds.
- **Digital Assets (Crypto):** Using stablecoins (USDT/USDC) in cold wallets for global diversification and privacy.
- **Collectibles:** Luxury watches/art as mobile wealth stores.

## 3. Family Office Integration
- **Holding Company (PT Holding):** Acts as the central "vault" for shares and dividends.
- **Dividend Strategy:** Utilizing UU Cipta Kerja for 0% tax on dividends reinvested in Indonesia.
- **Succession Planning:** Using multi-class shares (Voting vs. Economic) to protect wealth from incompetent heirs.
- **Tax-Efficient Distribution:** Using corporate payroll (Commisioner roles) and direct grants for family members.
