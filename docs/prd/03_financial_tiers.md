# Financial Tiers & Content Modules

## Tier 0: Survival (Defense)
- **Focus:** Debt management, Pinjol negotiation, and basic budgeting.
- **Tools:** Debt Exit Script, Barebones Calculator.

## Tier 1: The Hustler (Income Boosting)
- **Target:** Fresh grads, UMR workers.
- **Topics:** High-income skills, side hustles, zero-risk business models.
- **Tools:** Pricing tools, TikTok scripts for farmers.

## Tier 2: The Scaler (Optimization)
- **Target:** Managers, Small business owners.
- **Topics:** Systematization (SOP), Delegation, Revenue scaling, Bankability.
- **Tools:** KPI Trackers, Hiring vs Outsourcing calculators.

## Tier 3: The Asset Builder (Wealth Creation)
- **Target:** Successful entrepreneurs, Executives.
- **Topics:** Commercial property, Franchising, Dividend investing.
- **Tools:** Cap Rate calculators, Franchise BEP estimators.

## Tier 4: The Legacy Maker (Wealth Protection)
- **Target:** UHNWI.
- **Topics:** Holding companies, Family Offices, Wealth protection, Succession planning.
- **Tools:** Inheritance tax estimators, Asset vulnerability scorecards.
