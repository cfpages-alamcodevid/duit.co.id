# Duit.co.id Content Repurposing Strategy

## 1. Core Philosophy

**"One Piece of Content, Ten Platforms"**

Every article written for Duit.co.id should be repurposed across multiple platforms to:
- Maximize reach and awareness
- Drive traffic back to the main site
- Build authority across platforms
- Create multiple touchpoints for user discovery
- Optimize content creation effort (write once, distribute everywhere)

---

## 2. Content Hierarchy

### Primary Content (Source Material)
- **Format:** Full Markdown article on Duit.co.id
- **Length:** 1500-3000 words
- **Depth:** Comprehensive, actionable, educational
- **CTA:** "Subscribe, share, or register for more"

### Secondary Content (Repurposed)
- **Format:** Platform-specific adaptations
- **Length:** Varies by platform (15 sec to 10 min)
- **Depth:** Summary, highlights, key takeaways
- **CTA:** "Read full article at duit.co.id/[slug]"

---

## 3. Platform Distribution Matrix

### 3.1 YouTube (Video)

**Content Type:** Article summary video
**Length:** 3-5 minutes
**Format:** Talking head + screen recordings + B-roll

**Video Structure:**
```
0:00-0:05 - Hook (problem statement)
0:05-0:30 - Context (why this matters)
0:30-3:00 - Key points (3-5 main takeaways)
3:00-4:00 - Action steps (what to do next)
4:00-5:00 - CTA ("Read full article at link in description")
```

**Video Elements:**
- **Title:** Same as article title (SEO consistency)
- **Description:** 
  ```
  Learn how to [benefit] with this step-by-step guide.
  
  📖 Read the full article: https://duit.co.id/artikel/[slug]
  
  In this video, we cover:
  - Point 1
  - Point 2
  - Point 3
  
  💡 Want more financial tips? Subscribe to our channel!
  
  #FinancialLiteracy #Indonesia #DuitCoId
  ```
- **Thumbnail:** Quote graphic with key stat/insight
- **Tags:** Article tags + "Indonesia", "financial literacy"
- **End Screen:** Link to related article or subscribe button

**Production Workflow:**
1. Write article (core content)
2. Extract key points (3-5 main ideas)
3. Write video script (conversational tone)
4. Record video (OBS, webcam, or phone)
5. Edit (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or Premiere)
6. Add intro/outro music (royalty-free)
7. Upload with SEO-optimized title/description/tags
8. Add frontmatter `youtube_url` to article

**Tools:**
- Recording: OBS Studio (free), iPhone camera
- Editing: CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve (free)
- Thumbnails: Canva
- Music: Epidemic Sound or YouTube Audio Library

### 3.2 Twitter/X (Thread)

**Content Type:** Article breakdown thread
**Length:** 8-12 tweets
**Format:** Text + images + quote tweets

**Thread Structure:**
```
Tweet 1: Hook + promise
"Most people lose money because they don't know [topic].
Here's how to [benefit] in 2026 (step-by-step): 🧵"

Tweet 2-9: Key points (1 per tweet)
Include: Stats, examples, actionable tips
Use: Numbered lists, bullet points, short sentences

Tweet 10: Summary + CTA
"TL;DR: [3-sentence summary]

Full article with examples: https://duit.co.id/artikel/[slug]

Follow @duitcoid for more financial tips 🇮🇩"
```

**Best Practices:**
- Post during peak hours (7-9 AM WIB, 7-9 PM WIB)
- Use 2-3 relevant hashtags max
- Tag relevant accounts (if applicable)
- Pin thread to profile for 1 week
- Engage with replies within 2 hours

**Visual Elements:**
- Quote graphics for key stats (Canva)
- Screenshots of examples
- Charts/infographics from article

**Scheduling:**
- Thread 1: Day of article publication
- Thread 2: 1 week later (different angle)
- Thread 3: 1 month later (case study update)

---

### 3.3 Instagram (Carousel)

**Content Type:** Visual article summary
**Length:** 7-10 slides
**Format:** Carousel post with designed graphics

**Carousel Structure:**
```
Slide 1: Title + hook (eye-catching design)
Slide 2-3: Problem statement (relatable pain points)
Slide 4-8: Solutions (1 tip per slide, visual + text)
Slide 9: Key takeaway (memorable quote/stat)
Slide 10: CTA ("Link in bio for full guide 👆")
```

**Design Guidelines:**
- Consistent brand colors (Deep Money Green + Aureum Gold)
- Large, readable fonts (mobile-optimized)
- Minimal text per slide (5-7 words per line max)
- Use icons/illustrations (Canva elements)
- Add Duit.co.id logo watermark (bottom corner)

**Caption:**
```
💰 [Hook: Problem statement]

Most people [pain point]. But there's a better way.

Here are [number] ways to [benefit]:
1️⃣ [Tip 1]
2️⃣ [Tip 2]
3️⃣ [Tip 3]
...

📖 Full guide with step-by-step instructions at link in bio!

💬 What's your biggest challenge with [topic]? Comment below!

.
.
.
#FinancialLiteracy #Indonesia #MoneyTips #DuitCoId
```

**Posting Strategy:**
- Post: 12-1 PM WIB or 7-9 PM WIB
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A
- Reels: 15-30 sec tip from article (separate post)
- Engage: Reply to comments within 2 hours

### 3.4 LinkedIn (Article/Post)

**Content Type:** Professional adaptation
**Length:** 500-800 words (post) or 1000-1500 words (article)
**Format:** Long-form text post or LinkedIn Article

**Post Structure:**
```
Headline: [Compelling stat or question]

Opening: Professional context
"This is why [target audience] needs to pay attention to [topic]..."

Body: 3-5 key insights (business/career angle)
- Focus on professional impact
- Use data and research
- Include real-world examples

Conclusion: Actionable advice
"Here's what you can do today:
1. [Action 1]
2. [Action 2]
3. [Action 3]"

CTA: "Full analysis at duit.co.id"
```

**Best Practices:**
- Professional tone (not casual)
- Data-driven arguments
- Tag relevant companies/people (if applicable)
- Use LinkedIn Article feature for long-form
- Post during business hours (8-10 AM WIB)

---

### 3.5 TikTok/Reels (Short Video)

**Content Type:** Quick tip/insight
**Length:** 15-60 seconds
**Format:** Vertical video with captions

**Video Ideas:**
1. **Myth vs Fact:** "3 myths about [topic] that are costing you money"
2. **Quick Tip:** "Do this one thing to [benefit]"
3. **Stat Bomb:** "Did you know [shocking stat]? Here's what to do..."
4. **How-To:** "Step 1, 2, 3 to [result]"

**Structure (15 sec example):**
```
0-3 sec: Hook (text overlay + voice)
"Stop doing [common mistake]!"

3-12 sec: Quick explanation
"Here's what you should do instead: [tip]"

12-15 sec: CTA
"Full guide at duit.co.id (link in bio)"
```

**Production Tips:**
- Film vertically (9:16 aspect ratio)
- Add captions (85% watch without sound)
- Use trending audio (if relevant)
- Fast cuts (change visual every 2-3 seconds)
- End with text overlay of URL

**Posting Strategy:**
- Post 3-5x per week
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Engage with comments (boosts algorithm)
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels

"This week's must-read: [Article title]

Social Proof: 
"This week we hit [X] subscribers!"
"Most shared article: [Title] (shared [X] times)"

CTA:
"Know someone who needs this? Forward this email

**Sending Schedule:**
- Weekly: Friday 5 PM WIB (weekend reading)

**Format:** Question or insight post

**Reddit Post Structure:**

Body:
"I've been researching [topic] and found that [insight].

Here's what the data shows:
- [Stat 1]

**Community Guidelines:**

## 5. Content Repurposing Workflow

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Write Article** (Day 1-2)
   - Research, outline, write, edit
   - Add frontmatter with all metadata
   - Upload featured image
   - Publish on Duit.co.id

2. **Record YouTube Video** (Day 3-4)
   - Write script from article
   - Record video (3-5 min)
   - Edit and upload
   - Add frontmatter `youtube_url`

3. **Create Twitter Thread** (Day 4-5)
   - Extract 8-12 key points
   - Write thread copy
   - Design quote graphics
   - Schedule post

4. **Design Instagram Carousel** (Day 5-6)
   - Create 7-10 slide design
   - Write caption
   - Schedule post
   - Add link to bio

5. **Adapt for LinkedIn** (Day 6-7)
   - Rewrite with professional angle
   - Post as LinkedIn Article or long-form post
   - Engage with comments

6. **Create TikTok/Reel** (Day 7-8)
   - Script 15-60 sec video
   - Record and edit
   - Add captions
   - Cross-post to TikTok + Reels

7. **Include in Newsletter** (Day 9-10)
   - Add to weekly roundup
   - Send to subscribers

8. **Share in Communities** (Day 10-14)
   - Post in Reddit/Facebook groups
   - Answer related questions
   - Engage with responses

### Timeline Summary
```
Week 1: Article + YouTube + Twitter
Week 2: Instagram + LinkedIn + TikTok
Week 3: Newsletter + Community sharing
Week 4: Analytics review + optimization
```

## 7. Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics to Track

**Per Platform:**
- YouTube: Views, subscribers, watch time, click-through to site
- Twitter: Impressions, engagements, link clicks, follows
- Instagram: Reach, saves, shares, profile visits, link clicks
- LinkedIn: Views, reactions, comments, shares, link clicks
- TikTok: Views, likes, shares, follows, link clicks
- Email: Open rate, click rate, unsubscribes, forwards

**Overall:**
- Total referral traffic by platform
- New registrations from social media
- Social shares of articles
- Viral coefficient (new users per existing user)

### Optimization Strategy

**A/B Testing:**
- Test different hook styles
- Test posting times
- Test visual designs
- Test CTA wording

**Content Refresh:**
- Update old articles with new data
- Re-republish as "Updated for 2026"
- Create "Then vs Now" comparison content

**Platform-Specific Learning:**
- Double down on high-performing formats
- Kill underperforming content types
- Iterate based on audience feedback

## 9. Best Practices

### Do:
✅ Maintain consistent branding across platforms
✅ Always include CTA back to Duit.co.id
✅ Adapt tone/style per platform (professional for LinkedIn, casual for TikTok)
✅ Engage with comments within 2 hours
✅ Track all repurposed content URLs in frontmatter
✅ Repurpose top-performing content multiple times
✅ Use platform-native features (polls, stories, reels)

### Don't:
❌ Copy-paste identical content (adapt for each platform)
❌ Ignore platform-specific best practices
❌ Forget to include CTAs
❌ Post without engaging afterward
❌ Skip analytics review
❌ Over-post promotional content (80/20 value/promo ratio)
