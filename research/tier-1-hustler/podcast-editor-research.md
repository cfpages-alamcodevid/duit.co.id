### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement: Nomor Induk Berusaha (NIB)**  
**Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja & KBLI 2025 (podcast masuk kategori 5913 - Produksi Film, Video, dan Program Televisi)**  
**Process: Daftar online via OSS (Online Single Submission)**  
**Cost: Free**  
**Timeline: 1-3 hari**  
**Authority: Kementerian Investasi**  
**Link: oss.go.id**  

**Requirement: NPWP (Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak)**  
**Regulation: UU Ketentunan Perpajakan**  
**Process: Daftar online via djp.go.id, freelancers wajib lapor SPT tahunan**  
**Cost: Free**  
**Timeline: 1 hari**  
**Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak**  
**Link: djp.go.id**  

**Requirement: Kontrak Kerja Freelance**  
**Regulation: UU No. 11/2020 tentang Cipta Kerja (PKWT untuk freelancers)**  
**Process: Buat template kontrak yang mencakup scope, payment, IP rights, confidentiality**  
**Cost: Free (gunakan template online)**  
**Timeline: Per project**  
**Authority: Kemenaker**  
**Link: kemnaker.go.id**  

**Requirement: Perlindungan Data Pribadi**  
**Regulation: UU PDP (2022)**  
**Process: Dapatkan consent untuk data klien, jaga kerahasiaan**  
**Cost: Free**  
**Timeline: Ongoing**  
**Authority: Kemenkominfo**  
**Link: pdp.go.id**
