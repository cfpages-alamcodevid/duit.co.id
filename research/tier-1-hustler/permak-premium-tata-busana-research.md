### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Business registration as individual entrepreneur (badan usaha perseorangan)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja, PP No. 7 Tahun 2021
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission), obtain NIB number
Cost: Rp 50,000 application fee, free for small business
Timeline: 1-3 days approval
Authority: BKPM (Badan Koordinasi Penanaman Modal)
Link: https://oss.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: Tax obligations for small business revenue
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan, with final tax for SMEs
Process: Register NPWP if revenue > Rp 4.8 million/year, file monthly SOR (STR/pasal 4)
Cost: Free registration, tax rate 0.5% on revenue > Rp 500,000/day
Timeline: Annual filing, monthly reporting optional
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: Health and safety compliance for tailoring workspace
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2009 tentang Kesehatan, local zoning rules
Process: Basic sanitation inspection, ventilation requirements
Cost: Rp 0-100,000 local varies
Timeline: Self-assessment, on-demand inspection
Authority: Local Dinas Kesehatan
Link: https://www.kemenkes.go.id/
```
