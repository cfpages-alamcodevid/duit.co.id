**Sample Checklist Audit Menu (untuk writer copy-paste):**
1. Cek kalori/item vs AKG (pria/wanita 2000-2200 kcal)
2. Balance karbo 45-65%, protein 10-20%, lemak 25-35%
3. Portion size: 1/4 protein, 1/4 vegi, 1/2 karbo
4. Sodium <2000mg/hari, gula tambah <50g
5. Variasi mingguan (no repeat >2x)
6. Allergen label
7. Sumber bahan lokal/seasonal
Laporan: Score 1-10 + rekomendasi revisi
