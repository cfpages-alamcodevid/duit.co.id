## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Requirement:** NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha) under KBLI 77291 (Aktivitas Penyewaan Alat Pesta).
- **Regulation:** UU Cipta Kerja, Peraturan Pemerintah No. 24/2018.
- **Process:** Register via OSS (Online Single Submission) at oss.go.id; requires NPWP, business address, and risk assessment.
- **Cost:** Free for NIB; additional for legal consultations (Rp500k-2M).
- **Timeline:** 1-3 days for approval.
- **Authority:** Kemenkumham, local DPMPTSP.
- **Link:** https://oss.go.id/

- **Requirement:** UMK (Usaha Mikro Kecil) status for small businesses.
- **Regulation:** Permendag No. 18/2021.
- **Process:** Register as UMKM via online portal.
- **Cost:** Minimal (administrative fees).
- **Timeline:** Immediate after NIB.
- **Authority:** Kemenkop UKM.
- **Link:** https://umkm.kemenkopukm.go.id/
