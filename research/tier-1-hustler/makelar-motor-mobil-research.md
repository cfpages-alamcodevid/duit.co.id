## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Nomor Induk Berusaha (NIB) via OSS for low-risk business  
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja, KBLI 45101/45102  
Process: Register online via oss.go.id, provide KTP/NPWP  
Cost: Free (NIB), potential TDG Rp50rb if warehouse needed (not for brokerage)  
Timeline: 1-3 days online  
Authority: Kemenkumham/Ministry of Trade  
Link: https://oss.go.id/  

Requirement: No special license for brokerage (vs dealership requiring SIUP)  
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Perdagangan No. 47 Tahun 2023  
Process: Self-assessment for low-risk, report annually  
Cost: None additional  
Timeline: Immediate after NIB  
Authority: Ministry of Trade  
Link: https://jdih.kemendag.go.id/  

Requirement: Tax obligations (PPH 21 if >60M revenue, PPN if >4.8M turnover)  
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan  
Process: Register NPWP, report via e-filing  
Cost: Free registration, 0.5% tax on commissions  
Timeline: Monthly/annual reporting  
Authority: DJP  
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/
