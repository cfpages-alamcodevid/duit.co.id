### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** "If it doesn't work in 3 months, it's not worth pursuing"
Reality: Most successful Indonesian businesses take 6-12 months to show profit
Why it matters: This myth causes premature quitting during the critical learning phase
Source: Kemenkop UKM data shows average UMKM profitability timeline

**Myth:** "Successful entrepreneurs see immediate results from day one"
Reality: Even successful entrepreneurs often go through 6+ months of zero revenue
Why it matters: Creates unrealistic expectations and discourages persistence
Source: Case studies from Indonesian Success Stories book

**Myth:** "More money = faster results"
Reality: Persistence and strategy are more important than capital
Why it matters: Focuses attention on wrong factors (capital vs. execution)
Source: Research from IPB Business School

**Myth:** "If people don't respond immediately, my idea is bad"
Reality: Market awareness and trust building take time
Why it matters: Misinterprets normal business development as failure
Source: Consumer behavior studies
