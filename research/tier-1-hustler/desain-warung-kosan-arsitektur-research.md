## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business/legal articles, research required licenses/permits, tax obligations, relevant UU/regulations.

**Requirement: Surat Tanda Registrasi Arsitek (STRA)**
Regulation: UU No. 6 Tahun 2017 tentang Arsitek, PP No. 15 Tahun 2021
Process: Graduate with architecture degree → 2 years magang or 10 years experience → Uji Kompetensi → Register via IAI
Cost: Rp 500,000-1,000,000 (exam + registration)
Timeline: 3-6 months
Authority: Ikatan Arsitek Indonesia (IAI)
Link: https://iai.or.id/

**Requirement: Lisensi Praktik Arsitek (if designing buildings)**
Regulation: PP No. 15 Tahun 2021
Process: Have STRA → Get recommendation from professional organization → Apply for license
Cost: Rp 200,000
Timeline: 1 month
Authority: IAI branches
Link: IAI website

**Requirement: NPWP for business**
Regulation: Tax Law
Process: Register online via DJP website
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 week
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://djponline.pajak.go.id/

**Requirement: Business license (if forming PT/CV)**
Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja
Process: OSS system for micro business
Cost: Rp 50,000-100,000
Timeline: Online, 1 day
Authority: BKPM/OSS
Link: https://oss.go.id/
