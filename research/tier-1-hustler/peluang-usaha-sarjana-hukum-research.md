## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

Requirement: Understanding difference between legal consulting and licensed legal practice
Regulation: UU No. 18 Tahun 2003 tentang Advokat
Process: Graduates can provide consulting but litigation requires PERADI membership
Cost: Consulting services: No license fee; Advocacy: Rp 5-10 juta for registration
Timeline: Consulting: Immediate; Advocacy: 6 months training + exam
Authority: PERADI (Persatuan Advokat Indonesia)
Link: https://peradi.or.id/
Relevance: Allows business without full advocacy license

Requirement: Business registration for consulting services
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja, PP 43/2021 about PMSE
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur via OSS, get NIB
Cost: Free for basic registration
Timeline: 1-3 days online
Authority: BKPM/OSS system
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Tax obligations for freelance legal consultant
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 about Harmonization of Tax Regulations
Process: Register NPWP, report income quarterly, pay PPh 21
Cost: Free registration, 2.5-5% tax rate
Timeline: Monthly reporting
Authority: DJP (Directorate General of Taxes)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Data protection compliance when handling client information
Regulation: UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 about Personal Data Protection
Process: Obtain consent, secure data storage
Cost: Minimal (cloud storage fees)
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: KOMINFO
Link: https://pdp.go.id/
