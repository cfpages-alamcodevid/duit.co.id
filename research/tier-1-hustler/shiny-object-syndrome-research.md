## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: "Saya harus mencoba banyak bisnis untuk menemukan yang tepat"
Reality: Testing is good, but constant switching destroys momentum and capital
Why it matters: Each switch resets learning curve and wastes previous investment
Source: Research by Harvard Business School on entrepreneurial persistence

Myth: "Ide baru selalu lebih baik dari bisnis yang sedang dijalankan"
Reality: Most successful businesses are iterations, not completely new ideas
Why it matters: Perfectionism prevents execution and improvement
Source: Case study analysis of 100 successful Indonesian businesses

Myth: "Hustler harus selalu multitasking dan menjalankan banyak bisnis"
Reality: True hustlers focus deeply on one business until it works
Why it matters: Depth creates expertise and reputation that breadth cannot match
Source: Interview with successful Indonesian entrepreneurs

Myth: "Gonta-ganti bisnis itu tanda kreativitas dan adaptabilitas"
Reality: It's often a sign of fear of commitment and avoiding hard work
Why it matters: Creativity needs focus to produce results, not constant switching
Source: Psychological research on entrepreneurial behavior
```
