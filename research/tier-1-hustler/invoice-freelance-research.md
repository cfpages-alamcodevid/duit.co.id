### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: NPWP registration for tax compliance
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan 1984, PMK 168/2023
Process: Register online via djponline.pajak.go.id
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://djponline.pajak.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: PPh Final 0.5% for UMKM freelancers (omzet < Rp4.8M/year)
Regulation: PMK 99/2018, updated PMK 168/2023
Process: Use NPPN method for calculation, report via SPT 1770
Cost: Self-calculated
Timeline: Annual reporting by March 31
Authority: DJP
Link: https://pajak.go.id/id/artikel/kewajiban-perpajakan-freelancer
```

```
Requirement: PPN 11% for PKP freelancers (omzet > Rp4.8M/year)
Regulation: UU PPN 1984, PP 55/2024
Process: Register as PKP, collect and remit PPN
Cost: 11% of taxable transactions
Timeline: Monthly reporting
Authority: DJP
Link: https://pajak.go.id/id/ppn
```

```
Requirement: Invoice must include tax details for taxable transactions
Regulation: PER-24/PJ/2012 tentang Faktur Pajak
Process: Include NPWP, PPN amount, tax invoice number
Cost: N/A
Timeline: Per transaction
Authority: DJP
Link: https://pajak.go.id/peraturan

- Details needed: Format Word/Excel/PDF, bahasa Indonesia

- Warning: Include semua elemen wajib (nomor, tanggal, PPN)

- Details needed: Jatuh tempo 14-30 hari, denda keterlambatan

- Warning: Jangan terlalu longgar agar cashflow stabil

- Details needed: Email profesional dengan subjek jelas
- Example: Lampirkan PDF invoice + pesan singkat
- Warning: Jangan tunggu klien minta tagihan

Step 5: Lakukan follow-up proaktif
- Details needed: Email reminder 3 hari sebelum jatuh tempo
- Example: Template email pengingat dengan nada ramah
- Warning: Jika telat, eskalasi ke manajer atau kolektor

Step 6: Track dan catat semua pembayaran
- Details needed: Spreadsheet atau software akuntansi
- Example: Google Sheets dengan kolom status pembayaran
- Warning: Simpan bukti transfer untuk audit pajak
