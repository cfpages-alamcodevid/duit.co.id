## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: PIRT (Pangan Industri Rumah Tangga) for home food production <100kg/day
Regulation: Permentan No. 13/2018, BPOM guidelines
Process: Daftar online Dinkes kabupaten, inspeksi hygiene
Cost: Rp 200.000 - 500.000 (termasuk label)
Timeline: 1-2 bulan
Authority: Dinas Kesehatan setempat
Link: https://pirt.pom.go.id/

Process: MUI online
```
