## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Requirement:** Business registration (PT or individual for freelancers).  
  **Regulation:** UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas or UMKM registration.  
  **Process:** Register via OSS (Online Single Submission).  
  **Cost:** Rp 0-1 million (depending on type).  
  **Timeline:** 1-7 days.  
  **Authority:** Kemenkumham.  
  **Link:** https://oss.go.id/  

- **Requirement:** Tax obligations (NPWP).  
  **Regulation:** UU Pajak Penghasilan.  
  **Process:** Register online via DJP.  
  **Cost:** Free.  
  **Timeline:** 1-3 days.  
  **Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP).  
  **Link:** https://djponline.pajak.go.id/  

- **Requirement:** Data protection if handling client info.  
  **Regulation:** UU PDP (Perlindungan Data Pribadi).  
  **Process:** Ensure consent and secure data handling.  
  **Cost:** Minimal, compliance training.  
  **Timeline:** Ongoing.  
  **Authority:** Kemenkominfo.  
  **Link:** https://pdp.go.id/
