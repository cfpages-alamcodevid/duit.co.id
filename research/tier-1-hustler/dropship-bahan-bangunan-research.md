## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business registration (if formal), but dropship can start informal.
Regulation: General VAT collection on sales (Law No. 7/2021), no specific dropshipping law.
Process: Register as PKP if revenue > Rp 4.8B/year, collect 11% PPN.
Cost: Free registration, but accounting fees if needed.
Timeline: Immediate for informal start.
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes (DGT).
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Consumer protection compliance.
Regulation: Law No. 8/1999 on Consumer Protection.
Process: Ensure product quality, provide warranties, handle complaints.
Cost: None specific.
Authority: Ministry of Trade.
Link: https://www.kemendag.go.id/

Requirement: Import regulations if sourcing abroad.
Regulation: Customs Law No. 17/2006.
Process: Pay import duties, comply with SNI standards.
Cost: Duties vary by product.
Authority: Directorate General of Customs and Excise.
Link: https://www.beacukai.go.id/
