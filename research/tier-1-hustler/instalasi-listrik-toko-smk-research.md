## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business/legal articles, research: Required licenses/permits, tax obligations, relevant regulations, registration process, compliance requirements, penalties.

Requirement: Sertifikat Keahlian Tenaga Listrik (SKT) for installation work  
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri ESDM Nomor 19 Tahun 2020  
Process: Complete training through LSP PLN or Kemenperin certified centers, pass competency test  
Cost: Training Rp 500,000-1,000,000, exam Rp 200,000  
Timeline: 1-3 months for certification  
Authority: Kemenperin/PLN  
Link: https://pln.co.id/id/pelayanan/kualifikasi-tenaga-listrik  

Requirement: SKTT (Surat Keterangan Terdaftar Tenaga Teknik) for solo practitioners  
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja and ESDM regulations  
Process: Register with regional PLN office with SKT certificate  
Cost: Rp 100,000 administrative fee  
Timeline: 2-4 weeks approval  
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Energi Baru Terbarukan dan Konservasi Energi (EBTKE)  
Link: https://ebtke.esdm.go.id/content/penerbitan-sktt  

Requirement: NPWP for tax obligations if revenue >50MM/month  
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan  
Process: Register online via djponline.pajak.go.id  
Cost: Free, but annual compliance  
Timeline: Immediate if online  
Authority: DJP Kemenkeu  
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/usaha-kecil  

Requirement: License for hazardous electrical work compliance  
Regulation: Permen LHK 21/2021 tentang Pengendalian Pencemaran Udara  
Process: Report installations that may affect environment  
Cost: Minimal  
Timeline: As needed per project  
Authority: Kemen LHK  
Link: (none specific, general compliance)  

Penalties for non-compliance: Rp 10,000,000-100,000,000 fine or 1-5 years imprisonment for unauthorized installation causing damage (UU 28/2009 Ketenagalistrikan)
