## 4. Market Research (For Business Idea Articles)

**Capital Requirements:**
Minimum capital to start: Rp 5.000.000 - Rp 15.000.000
Breakdown:
- Manual Duplication Machine: Rp 2.500.000 - Rp 4.500.000
- Initial Key Blanks (Stock): Rp 1.000.000
- Basic Toolset (Lishi, pliers, screwdrivers): Rp 2.000.000
- Marketing (Banners, GMB): Rp 500.000
- Mobile Unit (Box for motorcycle): Rp 500.000
Total: ~Rp 7 - 10 Million (Basic)
Optional Add-on: Key Programmer (Immobilizer): Rp 10.000.000 - Rp 12.000.000

Where to buy:
- Tokopedia/Shopee: Search "Mesin Duplikat Kunci" or "Lishi Tools".
- Specialized distributors like PT Road Buck Indonesia (for high-end tools).

**Revenue Potential:**
Average selling price:
- Home key duplication: Rp 25.000 - Rp 50.000
- Car/Motorcycle loss (call-out): Rp 300.000 - Rp 800.000
- Immobilizer key: Rp 1.000.000 - Rp 2.500.000
Cost per unit: Material cost is usually < 10% of service price.
Profit margin: > 80%

Monthly projection:
- Conservative: 2 calls/day @ avg Rp 150k profit = Rp 9.000.000/month.
- Moderate: 4 calls/day + shop walk-ins = Rp 15.000.000 - Rp 20.000.000/month.
- Optimistic (Specialist): Rp 30.000.000+ (Specializing in BMW/Mercs/Smart Locks).

Time to break-even: 3 - 6 months.

**Market Demand:**
Search volume: High "Tukang kunci terdekat", "Ahli kunci 24 jam".
Trend: Shift from mechanical to digital.
Seasonality: None (Keys are lost every day).
Competition level: Medium (Traditional players), Low (High-tech specialists).
Target customers: People locked out of cars/houses, people who lost keys, office/apartments for smart lock installs.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Cuma duplikat kunci 5 ribuan."
Reality: The real money is in "Emergency Services" and "High-Tech Programming".
Myth: "Bisa dipelajari cuma dari YouTube."
Reality: YouTube helps, but hands-on practice on real locks is essential to avoid damaging customer property.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Learning the basics
- Join a locksmith course or apprentice with a senior.
- Learn types of keys: mechanical, laser-cut, transponder.
Step 2: Equipment Acquisition
- Buy a reliable duplication machine.
- Get a set of Lishi tools for non-destructive entry.
Step 3: Business Setup
- Register NIB.
- Setup Google Business Profile (Crucial).
Step 4: Marketing
- Target "24 Hour Locksmith" keywords.
- Partner with local apartment security or towing services.
Step 5: Ethical Guidelines
- Create a checklist: Verify Ownership -> Perform Work -> Provide Receipt.
