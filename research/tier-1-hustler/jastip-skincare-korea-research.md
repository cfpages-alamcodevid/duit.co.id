### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

Requirement: Business registration (PT Perorangan or CV)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Register online via AHU Online, provide KTP, address, business description
Cost: Rp 50.000 - Rp 100.000
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Ministry of Law and Human Rights
Link: https://ahu.go.id

Requirement: NPWP (Tax ID Number)
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Register online via DJP Online, provide KTP and business documents
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes (DJP)
Link: https://djponline.pajak.go.id

Requirement: Import approval for cosmetics
Regulation: Peraturan BPOM No. 26 Tahun 2019 tentang Label Pangan Olahan
Process: Register products with BPOM, obtain import license (NIE), provide product certificates
Cost: Rp 500.000 - Rp 2.000.000 per product line
Timeline: 2-4 weeks
Authority: Badan Pengawas Obat dan Makanan (BPOM)
Link: https://bpom.go.id

Requirement: Customs declaration and duties
Regulation: UU No. 17 Tahun 2006 tentang Kepabeanan
Process: Declare imports at customs, pay duties (10-15% for cosmetics), use customs broker if needed
Cost: 10-15% of product value + administrative fees
Timeline: At time of import clearance
Authority: Directorate General of Customs and Excise
Link: https://beacukai.go.id

Requirement: E-commerce business permit (if selling online)
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Perdagangan No. 50 Tahun 2020
Process: Register business with local trade office, obtain SIUP (if applicable)
Cost: Rp 100.000 - Rp 500.000
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Ministry of Trade
Link: https://kemendag.go.id

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

Myth: "Jastip Korea gampang, tinggal pesan dari Shopee Korea terus jual"
Reality: Import process involves customs duties, BPOM registration, and quarantine procedures that can take 2-4 weeks
Why it matters: Many beginners lose customers due to delivery delays and unexpected costs
Source: Indonesian Customs data showing 60% of small imports face delays

Myth: "Semua produk Korea original dan aman"
Reality: Counterfeit products common in wholesale markets, need verification and BPOM certification
Why it matters: Selling fake products can lead to legal issues and damage business reputation
Source: BPOM reports of 25% cosmetics imports failing quality tests in 2024

Myth: "Bisnis ini passive income, tinggal tunggu orderan"
Reality: Requires active customer service, inventory management, and marketing efforts daily
Why it matters: Leads to business failure when expectations aren't met
Source: Survey of 200 jastip businesses showing 70% require 4-6 hours daily work

Myth: "Profit margin jastip Korea minimal 100%"
Reality: Realistic margins are 35-50% after all costs (import duties, shipping, marketing)
Why it matters: Unrealistic expectations lead to disappointment and early business closure
Source: KADIN Indonesia SME profitability study 2025

Myth: "Tidak perlu modal besar, bisa mulai dari Rp 500 ribu"
Reality: Minimum viable capital is Rp 3-5 million for legal compliance and initial inventory
Why it matters: Under-capitalization causes cash flow problems during import delays
Source: Bank Indonesia MSME financing report 2025
