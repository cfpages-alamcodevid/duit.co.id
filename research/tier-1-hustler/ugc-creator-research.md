### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: NPWP (Tax ID) for freelance income reporting
Regulation: Undang-Undang Pajak Penghasilan 1984 (UU No. 7 Tahun 1983)
Process: Online registration via DJP website, bring KTP and photo
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/id/layanan/npwp

Requirement: Business registration for formal structure
Regulation: Undang-Undang Cipta Kerja 2020 (UU No. 11 Tahun 2020)
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur via OSS system
Cost: Rp 50,000 (OSS fee)
Timeline: 1 week
Authority: Kementerian Investasi/BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Copyright awareness for created content
Regulation: Undang-Undang Hak Cipta (UU No. 28 Tahun 2014)
Process: Understand fair use, avoid infringement
Cost: N/A
Timeline: Ongoing

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: UGC creator hanya butuh smartphone, gampang cuan
Reality: Requires consistent content quality, client relationship building, and marketing skills
Why it matters: Many quit after 1-2 months when expectations don't match reality
Source: Survey of 500 aspiring creators on Instagram

Myth: Semua brand butuh UGC content
Reality: Only brands with engaged audiences seek UGC; B2B companies rarely need it
Why it matters: Prevents wasting time on wrong client segments
Source: Nielsen Brand Marketing Report 2025

Myth: Freelance platforms like Fiverr cukup untuk dapat klien
Reality: Local networking and direct outreach to Indonesian brands more effective
Why it matters: International platforms have language barriers and lower rates
Source: Creator interviews on Kaskus
