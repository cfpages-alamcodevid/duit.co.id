## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
**Myth:** Jasa OSS = pengacara, bisa handle SIUP/TDP kompleks  
**Reality:** OSS basic gratis, grad adm pub bantu admin only (upload/fill form), refer lawyer jika sectoral license  
**Why it matters:** Hindari tuntutan PERADI/malpractice  
**Source:** PERADI etik guideline  

**Myth:** OSS susah, butuh modal besar  
**Reality:** 15 menit daftar jika dokumen ready, jasa admin Rp 500rb cukup  
**Why it matters:** UMKM ragu start, grad bisa isi gap  
**Source:** OSS.go.id FAQ
