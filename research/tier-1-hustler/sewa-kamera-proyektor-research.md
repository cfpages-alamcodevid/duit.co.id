## 4. Market Research (For Business Idea Articles)

### Capital Requirements:
Minimum capital to start: Rp 8-15 million  
Breakdown:  
- 1 DSLR camera (Canon EOS 1500D Kit): Rp 9 million  
- 1 Basic projector (Cheerlux C10): Rp 750,000  
- Accessories (tripod, bags, cables): Rp 2 million  
- Marketing budget (social media ads): Rp 1 million  
- Buffer for repairs/maintenance: Rp 1 million  
Total: Rp 13.75 million  

Where to buy:  
- Cameras: Galaxy Camera (galaxy.co.id), Bhinneka (bhinneka.com)  
- Projectors: BigGo (biggo.id), local electronics stores  
- Price comparison: Shop around major e-commerce platforms for best deals  

### Revenue Potential:
Average selling price: Camera Rp 75,000/day, Projector Rp 150,000/day  
Cost per unit: Equipment depreciation ~10% annually, minimal operating costs  
Profit margin: 60-70% after depreciation  

Monthly projection:  
- Conservative: 8 rentals/month = Rp 1.2 million revenue, Rp 800,000 profit  
- Moderate: 15 rentals/month = Rp 2.25 million revenue, Rp 1.5 million profit  
- Optimistic: 25 rentals/month = Rp 3.75 million revenue, Rp 2.5 million profit  

Time to break-even: 2-3 months with steady 10 rentals/month  

### Market Demand:
Search volume: High for "sewa kamera" and "sewa proyektor" in urban areas  
Trend: Increasing with rise in content creation, events, and remote work  
Seasonality: Peak during weddings (May-October), events, school projects  
Competition level: Medium in big cities, low in smaller towns  
Target customers: Students, young professionals, event organizers, small businesses  

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business Registration (NIB/NPWP)  
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja  
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission) system  
Cost: Free for basic registration, Rp 50,000-100,000 for NPWP  
Timeline: 1-3 days online  
Authority: Ministry of Investment/BKPM  
Link: https://oss.go.id/  

Requirement: Tax Registration (NPWP)  
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan  
Process: Apply online via djponline.pajak.go.id  
Cost: Free  
Timeline: 1-2 weeks  
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes  
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/  

Requirement: Local Business License (if applicable)  
Regulation: Regional regulations  
Process: Register at local district office  
Cost: Rp 100,000-500,000  
Timeline: 1-2 weeks  
Authority: Local government  
Link: Check local website
