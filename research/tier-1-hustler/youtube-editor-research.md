### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For freelance video editing business in Indonesia:

**Requirement: Business registration**
Regulation: UU No. 11/2020 tentang Cipta Kerja (Job Creation Law)
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur or PT perorangan through OSS-RBA system
Cost: Rp 50,000 - Rp 100,000 (online registration)
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Ministry of Law and Human Rights (Kemenkumham)
Link: https://oss.go.id/

**Requirement: Tax obligations**
Regulation: UU No. 36/2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan (Income Tax)
Process: Obtain NPWP, report income annually, pay PPh 21/26 if income > certain threshold
Cost: Free for NPWP, tax calculation based on income
Timeline: Annual reporting
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

**Requirement: Copyright and intellectual property**
Regulation: UU No. 28/2014 tentang Hak Cipta
Process: Register works if desired, understand fair use for client content
Cost: Voluntary registration Rp 50,000-100,000 per work
Timeline: N/A (voluntary)
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual (DGIP)
Link: https://www.dgip.go.id/

**Requirement: Contract agreements**
Regulation: KUHPER and general contract law
Process: Use written agreements for projects, specify deliverables, payment terms, IP rights
Cost: Template contracts (can use free templates)
Timeline: Per project
Authority: Not regulated by specific authority
Link: N/A
