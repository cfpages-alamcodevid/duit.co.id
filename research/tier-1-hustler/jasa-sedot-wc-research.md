## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Nomor Induk Berusaha (NIB)
Regulation: PP No. 5 Tahun 2021 (Risk-based OSS)
KBLI: 37011 (Pengumpulan Air Limbah Tidak Berbahaya)
Process: Register via OSS.go.id
Cost: Free (admin)
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: BKPM

Requirement: Kerjasama IPLT (Instalasi Pengolahan Lumpur Tinja)
Process: Contract with local government sanitation dept (DLH/PUPR).
Importance: Crucial to avoid "pembuangan liar" fines.
