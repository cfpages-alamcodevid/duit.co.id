### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business registration (PT/CV/PMSE)
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas, or PMSE for micro businesses
Process: Register via AHU online system
Cost: Rp 1-5 million (depending on entity)
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Kemenkumham
Link: https://ahu.go.id/

Requirement: Tax registration (NPWP)
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Apply online via DJP website
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Domain registration and hosting compliance
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2008 tentang Informasi dan Transaksi Elektronik
Process: Use accredited registrars, comply with data protection
Cost: Rp 100k-500k/year
Timeline: Immediate
Authority: Kemenkominfo
Link: https://kemenkominfo.go.id/
