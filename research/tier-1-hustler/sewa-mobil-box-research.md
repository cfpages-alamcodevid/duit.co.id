## Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business/legal articles, research:  
**Requirement:** Nomor Induk Berusaha (NIB) via OSS for low-risk business  
**Regulation:** Peraturan Pemerintah No. 5 Tahun 2021  
**Process:** Register online at oss.go.id, submit documents (KTP, NPWP)  
**Cost:** Rp 0 (free), but notary fees Rp 500K-1M if needed  
**Timeline:** 1-3 days approval  
**Authority:** Ministry of Law and Human Rights (Kemenkumham)  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Vehicle operating license for commercial rentals  
**Regulation:** Ministry of Transportation Regulation PM 108/2017  
**Process:** Obtain Izin Angkutan Sewa Khusus, periodic inspections  
**Cost:** Rp 5-10 million (STNK, KIR fees)  
**Timeline:** 1-2 months  
**Authority:** Ministry of Transportation  
**Link:** https://dephub.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Insurance (All Risk) for vehicles  
**Regulation:** OJK regulations  
**Process:** Purchase from authorized insurers (e.g., ACA, Asuransi Astra)  
**Cost:** Rp 8-15 million/year per unit  
**Timeline:** Immediate upon purchase  
**Authority:** OJK  
**Link:** https://ojk.go.id/

## Common Myths & Misconceptions
**Myth:** Starting car rental is easy with quick profits.  
**Reality:** Requires significant capital (Rp 400M+), slow break-even (4-5 years), and ongoing operational challenges.  
**Why it matters:** Prevents unrealistic expectations; many fail due to underestimating costs.  
**Source:** Multiple articles (LegalMP, SevenRent) highlighting high failure rates from poor planning.  

**Myth:** All rentals need tourism licenses.  
**Reality:** Commercial rentals like box trucks fall under transportation, not tourism, per PM 108/2017.  
**Why it matters:** Avoids unnecessary bureaucracy for logistics-focused businesses.  
**Source:** Government regulations and expert guides.
