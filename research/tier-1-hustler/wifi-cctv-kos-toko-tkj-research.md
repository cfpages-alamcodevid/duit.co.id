### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirements for tech installation services in Indonesia.

Requirement: Business license (NIB) for freelance service operations
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja, integrated to OSS system
Process: Register online at oss.go.id, no fee for micro enterprises
Cost: Free for NIB
Timeline: 1-3 days online
Authority: Kemenkumham melalui OSS online single submission
Link: https://oss.go.id/
Relevance: Required for invoicing and legal protection

Requirement: CCTV installation must comply with privacy laws (recording personal areas prohibited)
Regulation: UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 tentang Pelindungan Data Pribadi
Process: Obtain written consent from property owner for camera placement; post warning signs
Cost: None (compliance only)
Timeline: Immediate
Authority: Private only, enforced by local authorities if complaints
Link: https://pdp.kominfo.go.id/
Relevance: Prevents privacy violations, especially in shared kos
