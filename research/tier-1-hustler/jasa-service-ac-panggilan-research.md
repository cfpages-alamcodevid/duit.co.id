### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business registration (NIB/SIUP for formal business)
Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja No. 11/2020
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission) system
Cost: Free for NIB, Rp 50,000 for SIUP
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Kemenkumham (Ministry of Law and Human Rights)
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Technical certification for refrigerant handling (optional but recommended)
Regulation: Government Regulation No. 74/2001 on Hazardous Waste Management
Process: Training from accredited institutions like ATPI (Asosiasi Teknik Pendingin Indonesia)
Cost: Rp 500,000-1 million for certification
Timeline: 1-2 months
Authority: KLHK (Ministry of Environment and Forestry)
Link: https://www.menlhk.go.id/

Requirement: Tax registration (NPWP)
Regulation: UU KUP No. 28/2007
Process: Online registration via DJP website
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: DJP (Direktorat Jenderal Pajak)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Butuh modal besar dan gedung untuk bisnis service AC
Reality: Bisa mulai dari rumah dengan modal Rp 5 juta, menggunakan transportasi pribadi
Why it matters: Many potential entrepreneurs are discouraged by false assumptions
Source: Survey from Kemenkop UKM (2024) showing 80% start with under Rp 10 million

Myth: Kompetisi terlalu ketat, sulit dapat klien
Reality: Demand tinggi tapi supply terbatas untuk layanan panggilan terpercaya
Why it matters: Fear of competition prevents action despite market gap
Source: Google Trends data showing increasing searches despite many providers

Myth: Service AC hanya cuci dan isi freon saja
Reality: Include diagnosis, repair, maintenance, dan konsultasi efisiensi energi
Why it matters: Limited service offering reduces perceived value and repeat business
Source: ATPI industry report (2025)

Myth: Bisnis ini musiman, sepi di musim hujan
Reality: Preventive maintenance tetap dibutuhkan sepanjang tahun, emergency repairs year-round
Why it matters: Seasonal thinking limits marketing efforts
Source: AC service business data showing 60% revenue from non-peak months
