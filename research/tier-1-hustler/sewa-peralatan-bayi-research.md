## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
Case Study: "BabyRent ID" started with one stroller on Instagram. They focused on "Video Cleaning" as their main content. Parents trusted them because they saw the steam cleaning process.
Key learning: Trust is the #1 currency in baby rentals. Show, don't just tell, that it's clean.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
Persona: "Siska, 28 tahun, punya barang bayi anak pertama yang tidak terpakai."
Scenario: Siska lists her Nuna Leaf and Medela Swing on Instagram. She spends 200k on Instagram Ads targeting "New Moms" in her city.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Barang sewaan itu kotor dan banyak kuman."
Reality: Professional rentals often clean their items better than parents clean their own.
Myth: "Nanti kalau rusak bagaimana?"
Reality: You must use a "Deposit System" and a clear Rental Agreement (SOP for damage).

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Inventory Audit
- List what you have. Buy what's missing but high in demand (Car seats are essential for safety-conscious parents).
Step 2: Hygiene SOP
- Buy a steam cleaner and baby-safe detergent (Sleek, etc.).
- Create a "Cleaning Video" for marketing.
Step 3: Pricing & Policies
- Research local prices.
- Set deposit rules (usually 50-100% of one month's rent).
Step 4: Marketing
- Focus on Instagram & TikTok. Use "Aesthetic Mom" vibes.
- Partner with local pediatricians or baby shops.
Step 5: Scaling
- Reinvest profits into "High Turnover" items like cabin-sized strollers.
