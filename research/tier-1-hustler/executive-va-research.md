### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan
Process: Register online at pajak.go.id, provide KTP and business details
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Business license if operating as formal entity
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Online registration through OSS system
Cost: Free-Rp 50,000
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Data protection compliance for client information
Regulation: UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 tentang Pelindungan Data Pribadi

### 9. Action Steps Outline

Step 1: Assess skills and identify VA specialization
- Details: Evaluate current admin/organizational skills, choose executive focus area
- Example: Focus on executive email management and calendar coordination
- Warning: Don't try to be everything - specialize for higher rates

Step 2: Complete VA training and certification
- Details: Take online courses, practice with mock assignments
- Example: Udemy VA certification + practice portfolios
- Warning: Theory alone insufficient - need practical experience

Step 3: Setup business infrastructure
- Details: Create professional email, website/portfolio, pricing structure
- Example: Use free tools like Gmail, Carrd for website
- Warning: Professional presentation crucial for executive clients

Step 4: Develop service packages and pricing
- Details: Create tiered packages based on complexity and time commitment
- Example: Basic (Rp 50k/hour), Executive (Rp 150k/hour)
- Warning: Start with conservative pricing to build reviews

Step 5: Market and acquire first clients
- Details: Network on LinkedIn, join VA communities, offer trial services
- Example: Cold outreach to local businesses, portfolio showcase
- Warning: Focus on quality over quantity initially

Step 6: Deliver exceptional service and get testimonials
- Details: Over-deliver on promises, request feedback and referrals
- Example: Monthly client check-ins, detailed progress reports
- Warning: Client retention more valuable than acquisition
