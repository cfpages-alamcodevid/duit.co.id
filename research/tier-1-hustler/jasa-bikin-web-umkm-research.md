### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business Entity Registration (PT or CV)
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas / UU No. 3 Tahun 1982 tentang Wajib Daftar Perusahaan
Process: Register via AHU online system, get NIB from OSS
Cost: Rp 1-3 million (legal fees, stamp duty)
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Kemenkumham / BKPM
Link: https://ahu.go.id/

Requirement: Tax Registration (NPWP)
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Register online via DJP website, get NPWP card
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: NIB (Business Identification Number)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Apply via OSS system, select business classification
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 days
Authority: BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Client Website Compliance (if e-commerce)
Regulation: PMK No. 31/PMK.010/2019 tentang E-commerce
Process: Ensure client registers business license if selling online
Cost: Varies by client
Timeline: Client responsibility
Authority: Kemenperin
Link: https://www.kemenperin.go.id/
