## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Copyright registration for original beats  
Regulation: Undang-Undang Hak Cipta No. 28 Tahun 2014  
Process: Online registration through LMKN portal  
Cost: Rp 50 thousand per work  
Timeline: 1-2 weeks approval  
Authority: Lembaga Manajemen Kolektif Nasional (LMKN)  
Link: https://ekcipta.lmkn.id/  

Requirement: Business registration if operating as formal entity  
Regulation: Undang-Undang No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja  
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur via OSS  
Cost: Free for basic registration  
Timeline: 1 day online  
Authority: Kementerian Investasi/BKPM  
Link: https://oss.go.id/

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Learn music production fundamentals  
- Details: Master DAW interface, basic mixing, sound design  
- Example: Complete 20 YouTube tutorials on FL Studio  
- Warning: Avoid pirated software to prevent legal issues  

Step 2: Invest in essential equipment  
- Details: Buy DAW license, microphone, headphones  
- Example: Start with FL Studio Mobile for Android testing  
- Warning: Don't buy unnecessary plugins initially  

Step 3: Build a portfolio of 30+ beats  
- Details: Create beats in popular genres (hip-hop, trap, lo-fi)  
- Example: Upload to SoundCloud with proper tagging  
- Warning: Ensure all samples are royalty-free  

Step 4: Set up social media presence  
- Details: Instagram, TikTok, SoundCloud accounts  
- Example: Post daily beat previews and behind-the-scenes  
- Warning: Be consistent with posting schedule  

Step 5: Price and sell beats  
- Details: Start at Rp 100-200k, use Gumroad or direct WhatsApp  
- Example: Offer packages (beat + stems)  
- Warning: Always use contracts for custom work  

Step 6: Network and collaborate  
- Details: Join music production communities, collaborate with artists  
- Example: Free beats for exposure deals  
- Warning: Protect your intellectual property
