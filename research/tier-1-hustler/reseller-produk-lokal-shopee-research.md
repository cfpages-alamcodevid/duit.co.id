## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: NPWP (Taxpayer ID) for business income reporting
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Register online via DJP website or through marketplace
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/id/npwp
```

```
Requirement: Business license (if expanding beyond reselling)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Online registration via OSS system
Cost: Rp 50,000-100,000
Timeline: 3-7 days
Authority: Kemenkop UKM
Link: https://oss.go.id/
```


## 9. Action Steps Outline

```
Step 1: Choose your niche and research suppliers
- Research local products popular in your area
- Contact 5-10 UMKM suppliers for wholesale prices
- Test small orders (Rp 500,000-1 million)
- Warning: Don't buy too much inventory initially

Step 2: Set up seller accounts on Shopee and Tokopedia
- Complete verification process
- Create attractive store profiles
- Set up payment methods
- Example: Use professional photos for store banner

Step 3: Source and prepare products
- Negotiate with suppliers for best prices
- Take high-quality photos (use good lighting)
- Write authentic product descriptions
- Warning: Ensure product quality matches photos

Step 4: Price products for profit
- Research competitor pricing
- Calculate costs + desired margin
- Set competitive yet profitable prices
- Example: Cost Rp 100k, sell Rp 180k = 44% margin

Step 5: Launch and market your store
- List 20-30 products initially
- Post on social media, join local groups
- Offer promotions for first customers
- Warning: Don't discount too much initially

Step 6: Manage orders and customer service
- Respond within 1 hour to inquiries
- Pack products professionally
- Handle returns/complaints positively
- Example: Include thank you notes with local stories

Step 7: Scale and optimize
- Analyze best-selling products
- Increase inventory for winners
- Try marketing campaigns
- Goal: Reach Rp 3-5 million monthly revenue
```
