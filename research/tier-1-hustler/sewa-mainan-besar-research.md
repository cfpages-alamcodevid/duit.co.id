## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Bahaya, balonnya bisa meledak."
Reality: Inflatables are "Constant Air" systems; they don't pop like balloons. They just deflate slowly if power is cut.
Myth: "Butuh listrik besar."
Reality: Yes, 1500-2000W is mandatory. Always warn clients to check their house power limit (MCB).
