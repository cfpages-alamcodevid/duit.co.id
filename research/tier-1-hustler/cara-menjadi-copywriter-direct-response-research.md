### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
No specific licenses required for freelance copywriting in Indonesia. However:
- For business registration: If earning >Rp 4.8 million/year, register as freelancer with NPWP
- Tax obligations: Report freelance income through e-form SPT 1770
- Intellectual property: Own copyright on original copy unless work-for-hire

### 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Pelajari dasar copywriting (1-2 minggu)
- Baca buku "The Copywriter's Handbook"
- Ikuti kursus gratis di YouTube
- Warning: Jangan beli kursus mahal dulu

Step 2: Fokus direct response techniques (2-4 minggu)
- Pelajari AIDA, PAS, storytelling
- Practice tulis 10 headlines per hari
- Example: Ubah "Jasa laundry" jadi "Laundry Express: Bersih dalam 3 Jam atau Gratis!"

Step 3: Buat portfolio (1 minggu)
- Pilih 3 niche: e-commerce, SaaS, atau health
- Buat 5 contoh copy untuk masing-masing
- Warning: Jangan pakai copy klien tanpa izin

Step 4: Cari klien pertama (2-4 minggu)
- Join komunitas copywriter di Telegram/Facebook
- Offer jasa gratis untuk startup kecil
- Example: "Saya berikan 1 copy gratis untuk produk Anda"

Step 5: Scale up business (ongoing)
- Naikkan harga dari Rp 500rb ke Rp 2 juta/project
- Cari retainer clients
- Warning: Jangan ambil semua project, quality first
