### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Copyright registration for original designs
Regulation: UU Hak Cipta No. 28 Tahun 2014
Process: Register through Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual (DGIP)
Cost: Rp 50k-100k per registration
Timeline: 1-2 months
Authority: Kemenkumham
Link: https://dgip.go.id/
Relevance: Protects designer's work from plagiarism

Requirement: Business license for freelance design services
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 about Job Creation
Process: Online registration via OSS for micro business
Cost: Free for micro scale
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Tax obligations for income
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan
Process: Annual tax return if income > Rp 60 million/year
Cost: Free filing, or use tax consultant Rp 500k-1 juta
Timeline: April each year
Authority: DJP
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: UMKM tidak perlu branding profesional karena biaya mahal
Reality: Branding bisa mulai dari Rp 750k dengan tools seperti Canva, dan investasi ini kembali berlipat dari peningkatan penjualan
Why it matters: Tanpa branding, UMKM sulit bersaing dan bertahan di pasar
Source: Multiple case studies showing branding success

Myth: Lulusan DKV langsung bisa dapat klien besar
Reality: Mulai dari UMKM kecil, bangun portfolio, lalu skala ke klien lebih besar
Why it matters: Membangun reputasi bertahap lebih sustainable
Source: Alumni stories starting small

Myth: AI tools menggantikan desainer manusia
Reality: AI membantu, tapi sentuhan manusia untuk customization lokal diperlukan
Why it matters: UMKM butuh personalisasi sesuai budaya lokal
