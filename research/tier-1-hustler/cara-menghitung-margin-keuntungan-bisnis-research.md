## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
Persona 1: Budi, 32 tahun, pemilik warung makan di Surabaya  
- Monthly revenue: Rp 20 million  
- COGS: Rp 12 million  
- Operating costs: Rp 5 million  
- Profit: Rp 3 million  
- Margin: 15% net  

Calculation example:  
Gross Margin = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100 = (20M - 12M) / 20M × 100 = 40%  
Net Margin = Profit / Revenue × 100 = 3M / 20M × 100 = 15%  

Scenario: Increasing margins by 5% through cost reduction
