### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Business registration (NIB or UMKM registration)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission) system
Cost: Free for basic registration
Timeline: 1-3 days online
Authority: Kemenkop UKM or local district office
Link: https://oss.go.id

Requirement: Tax obligations (PPh Final 0.5%)
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan
Process: Register as individual taxpayer, report quarterly if revenue > Rp 4.8 juta/month
Cost: Free registration, tax rate 0.5% of gross revenue
Timeline: Register immediately when starting business
Authority: DJP (Direktorat Jenderal Pajak)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id

Requirement: Environmental compliance (waste disposal)
Regulation: UU No. 32 Tahun 2009 tentang Perlindungan dan Pengelolaan Lingkungan Hidup
Process: Dispose of chemical waste properly through licensed collectors
Cost: Minimal, include in operational costs
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: KLHK (Kementerian Lingkungan Hidup dan Kehutanan)
Link: https://ppkl.menlhk.go.id

Requirement: Health and safety standards
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Kesehatan No. 7 Tahun 2023
Process: Use approved cleaning chemicals, provide basic PPE
Cost: PPE costs Rp 200.000 initial
Timeline: Mandatory from start
Authority: Kemenkes (Kementerian Kesehatan)
Link: https://kesmas.kemkes.go.id
```
