## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For freelancers, focus on business registration and IP protection.

Requirement: NPWP (Tax ID) for freelance income over Rp 4.8 million/month
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Register online via e-NAP website
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://nap.djp.go.id/

Requirement: Business license for commercial services
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja (Article 62)
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur via OSS system
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: Kementerian Investasi/BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Copyright protection for original 3D models
Regulation: UU No. 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta
Process: Automatic protection, optional registration at Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual
Cost: Rp 100k-500k (optional)
Timeline: Immediate (automatic)
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal KI
Link: https://www.dgip.go.id/

## 9. Action Steps Outline

Step 1: Learn fundamentals (1-2 months)
- Install Blender and follow beginner tutorials
- Practice basic modeling, texturing, and lighting
- Details needed: Free resources, practice projects
- Example: Model simple objects like furniture or characters
- Warning: Don't get overwhelmed by advanced features

Step 2: Build portfolio (1 month)
- Create 5-10 sample projects
- Set up Behance/Artstation profile
- Example: Product visualizations, game assets
- Warning: Focus on quality over quantity

Step 3: Set up business basics (1 week)
- Get NPWP and business registration
- Create pricing structure
- Example: Calculate rates based on complexity and time
- Warning: Start with competitive but profitable rates

Step 4: Find first clients (1-2 months)
- Post on freelance platforms (Upwork, Fiverr)
- Network on LinkedIn and local design communities
- Example: Offer services to local businesses
- Warning: Deliver on time and communicate clearly

Step 5: Scale and specialize (ongoing)
- Learn advanced techniques
- Build client relationships
- Example: Specialize in game characters or architectural visualization
