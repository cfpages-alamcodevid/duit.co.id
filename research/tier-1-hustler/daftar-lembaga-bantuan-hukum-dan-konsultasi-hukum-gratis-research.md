### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Accreditation as OBH by BPHN
Regulation: UU 16/2011 tentang Bantuan Hukum and PP 42/2013
Process: Register with BPHN, meet verification criteria, receive accreditation.
Cost: Free for applicants, but government-funded.
Timeline: Accreditation valid for 3 years; renewal required.
Authority: Badan Pembinaan Hukum Nasional (BPHN)
Link: https://bphn.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: Proof of low income for eligibility
Regulation: UU 16/2011 and PP 42/2013
Process: Submit SKTM or equivalent (e.g., KIS, PKH card).
Cost: Free to obtain proofs.
Timeline: Immediate upon application to OBH.
Authority: Local government (lurah/kepala desa) or BPHN.
Link: https://bphn.go.id/layanan/bantuan-hukum/
```
