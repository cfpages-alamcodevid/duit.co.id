## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For starting a press release service business:  
**Requirement:** Business license (SIUP or TDP).  
**Regulation:** UU No. 20 Tahun 2008 about UMKM, and local business permits.  
**Process:** Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission), submit ID, business plan.  
**Cost:** Rp 50k-200k for registration.  
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Cooperatives and SMEs.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id/  

**Requirement:** If involving content creation, may need Izin Penyedia Konten from Komdigi.  
**Regulation:** Permen Komdigi No. 13/2019.  
**Process:** Apply via servicedesk.layanan.go.id for content provider license.  
**Cost:** Minimal, depends on scale.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Communication and Digital (Komdigi).  
**Link:** https://jdih.komdigi.go.id/produk_hukum/unduh/id/714/t/peraturan+menteri+komunikasi+dan+informatika+nomor+13+tahun+2019  

**Requirement:** Tax registration (NPWP).  
**Regulation:** UU Pajak.  
**Process:** Register at KPP nearest.  
**Cost:** Free.  
**Timeline:** 1 week.  
**Authority:** Directorate General of Taxes.  
**Link:** https://www.pajak.go.id/
