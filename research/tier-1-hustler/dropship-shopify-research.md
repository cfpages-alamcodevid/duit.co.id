### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Business registration in Indonesia
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas
Process: Register PT/LLC through AHU online system
Cost: Rp 2-5 juta (including notary, legal fees)
Timeline: 7-14 days
Authority: Kemenkumham
Link: https://ahu.go.id

Requirement: Import license for goods entering Indonesia (if needed)
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Perdagangan No. 27/M-DAG/PER/5/2012
Process: Apply through INSW if importing samples/products
Cost: Varies by product type
Timeline: 7-30 days
Authority: Kementerian Perdagangan
Link: https://insw.go.id

Requirement: US import compliance (tariffs, customs)
Regulation: US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations, Section 122 Proclamation (10% surcharge)
Process: HTS classification, country of origin declaration, duty payment
Cost: 10% global surcharge + 7.5-25% Section 301 + standard MFN duties
Timeline: Immediate (every shipment)
Authority: US CBP
Link: https://www.cbp.gov/trade/basic-import-export

Requirement: Sales tax collection for US sales
Regulation: State-specific sales tax laws, economic nexus rules
Process: Register with state tax authorities, collect and remit taxes
Cost: Varies by state (4-10% typically)
Timeline: Monthly reporting
Authority: State revenue departments
Link: https://www.avalara.com/us/en/learn/sales-tax.html

Requirement: Intellectual property compliance
Regulation: US Copyright Law, Trademark Law
Process: Ensure products don't infringe IP, register own trademarks if scaling
Cost: Legal consultation Rp 1-5 juta
Timeline: Ongoing
Authority: USPTO
Link: https://www.uspto.gov
```
