## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Harus modal besar beli spare lengkap semua merk
Reality: Mulai 5-10 item high-demand (Polytron, Cosmos), restock berdasarkan job
Why it matters: Banyak gagal stok mati Rp10jt+
Source: Survey FB Group Teknisi 2026

Myth: Repair gratis diagnostic biar dapat customer
Reality: Charge Rp50k (refund if repair), tingkatkan value
Why it matters: 70% no-show tanpa fee
Source: Praktik sukses case studies

Myth: No need warranty, pelanggan ogah bayar mahal
Reality: Warranty 3-7 hari tingkatkan repeat 40%
