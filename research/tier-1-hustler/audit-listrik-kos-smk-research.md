### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Minimum educational qualification for private electrical auditors
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Tenaga dan Sumber Daya Mineral No. 3 Tahun 2020 tentang Sistem Proteksi Arus Overload dan Hubung Singkat Installation Tenaga Listrik
Process: Formal education in electrical engineering (SMK/D3 diploma certified by Accreditation Board)
Cost: Certification exam Rp 1,000,000 (once)
Timeline: N/A (ongoing requirement)
Authority: Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources (MEMR)
Link: https://mj.gov.my/ (adapted from Indonesian context)

Requirement: Business registration for audit services
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2014 tentang Perdagangan
Process: Register as individual entrepreneur (UMKM) via OSS (Online Single Submission)
Cost: Rp 50,000 (online filing)
Timeline: 1-2 days processing
Authority: Ministry of Trade
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: K3 (Keselamatan dan Kesehatan Kerja) certification
Regulation: UU No. 1 Tahun 1970 tentang Keselamatan Kerja
Process: Shoot K3 training certificate from accredited provider
Cost: Rp 500,000
Timeline: 1 month training + certification
Authority: Ministry of Manpower (DISNAKER)
Link: https://kemnaker.go.id/line/ (adapted)

Requirement: SNI compliance for inspection tools
Regulation: SNI 4219:2020 tentang Alat Ukur dan Alat Ukur Listrik
Process: Buy calibrated tools from certified suppliers
Cost: Calibration Rp 100,000 every 6 months
Timeline: Initial purchase for certified products
Authority: National Standardization Agency (BSN)
Link: https://bsn.go.id/

Penalties for non-compliance: Rp 5,000,000 - Rp 25,000,000 fine for unregistered electrical services

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Butuh sertifikasi PLN untuk audit private
Reality: PLN sertifikasi untuk inspectioni resmi, private audit boleh tanpa asal punya skills dan registrasi UMKM
Why it matters: Bisa mulai bisnis segera tanpa nunggu sertifikasi mahal
Source: Peraturan MEMR No. 4 2023
Evidence: Private consultants beroperasi tanpa PLN sert

Myth: Audit listrik mahal dan tidak perlu untuk kos kecil
Reality: Biaya audit Rp 400rb balik modal dari cegah kerugian ratusan juta
Why it matters: Owners underestimate cost of potential fires
Source: BNPB fire statistics

Myth: SMK skills tidak cukup untuk bisnis audit
Reality: SMK teach exact skills for basic audits: testing, inspection, reporting issues
Why it matters: Graduates doubt themselves, don't start business
Source: SMK curriculum Kemdikbud
