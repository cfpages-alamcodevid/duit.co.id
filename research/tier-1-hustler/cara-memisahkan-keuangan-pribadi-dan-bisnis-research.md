## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
### Requirement: Separate business bank account
- **Regulation:** UU No. 10 Tahun 1998 tentang Perbankan, POJK No. 12/POJK.03/2021
- **Process:** Visit bank branch, provide KTP, NPWP, business license
- **Cost:** Rp 50,000-100,000 setup fee
- **Timeline:** 1-3 days
- **Authority:** Bank Indonesia (BI)

### Requirement: Separate tax reporting
- **Regulation:** UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan (HPP)
- **Process:** Register NPWP bisnis terpisah, SPT tahunan terpisah
- **Cost:** Free for registration, penalties if mixed: up to 100% of tax owed
- **Timeline:** Immediate after setup
- **Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)

### Requirement: Proper bookkeeping
- **Regulation:** UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas, PP No. 24 Tahun 2021
- **Process:** Maintain separate financial records for at least 5 years
- **Cost:** Rp 100,000-300,000/month for accounting services
- **Timeline:** Ongoing
- **Authority:** Kementerian Hukum dan HAM
