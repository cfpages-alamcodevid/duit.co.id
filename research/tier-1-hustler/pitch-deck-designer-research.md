## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) for client information  
**Regulation:** UU No. 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta  
**Process:** Use standard NDA templates; sign before project start  
**Cost:** Free templates online; legal review Rp 500k-1 juta  
**Timeline:** 1 day  
**Authority:** Notary public for validation  
**Link:** https://jdih.kemenkumham.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Copyright protection for designs  
**Regulation:** UU Hak Cipta  
**Process:** Register designs with Dirjen HKI if needed  
**Cost:** Rp 500k-2 juta for registration  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months  
**Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual (DJKI)  
**Link:** https://www.dgip.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Business license for freelance services  
**Regulation:** UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja  
**Process:** Register as individual entrepreneur via OSS  
**Cost:** Free  
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks  
**Authority:** Kemenkumham  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id/
