## 4. Market Research (For Business Idea Articles)

### Capital Requirements
**Minimum capital to start:** Rp 0-500,000 (if using free design tools and platforms)  
**Breakdown:**  
- Design software: Rp 0 (Canva free) or Rp 50,000/month for premium  
- Platform fees: Rp 0 for Redbubble/Etsy basic, but listing fees Rp 0.20/item on Etsy  
- Marketing: Rp 100,000-300,000 for social ads initial testing  
- Sample products: Rp 50,000-200,000 for testing quality  
**Total:** Rp 200,000-800,000 realistic for beginners  

**Where to buy:**  
- Design tools: Canva.com or Adobe Express (free tiers available)  
- POD platforms: Etsy.com, Redbubble.com, Printful.com  
- Marketing: Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, Tokopedia affiliate links  

### Revenue Potential
**Average selling price:** Rp 150,000-350,000 per item (depending on product type)  
**Cost per unit:** Rp 50,000-150,000 (including POD provider costs)  
**Profit margin:** 30-50% net after all fees  

**Monthly projection:**  
- Conservative: Rp 2-5 million (20-50 sales)  
- Moderate: Rp 5-10 million (50-100 sales)  
- Optimistic: Rp 10-20 million (100+ sales with scaling)  

**Time to break-even:** 1-3 months with consistent effort

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** Business registration for online sales  
Regulation: Government Regulation No. 80 of 2019 on Trading Through Electronic Systems  
Process: Register as individual seller or PT, obtain NIB (Business Identification Number)  
Cost: Rp 0-50,000 for basic registration  
Timeline: 1-7 days online  
Authority: Ministry of Trade/OSS (Online Single Submission)  
Link: https://oss.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Tax obligations for online income  
Regulation: Income Tax Law (UU PPh) and VAT for e-commerce  
Process: Report monthly if income >Rp 4.8 million/year, use e-billing for VAT  
Cost: 0.5-2% tax rate depending on income  
Timeline: Monthly reporting  
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes  
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Import regulations for POD products  
Regulation: Customs Law for imported goods  
Process: POD platforms handle customs, but sellers need awareness of duties  
Cost: 5-15% import duty depending on product type  
Timeline: Automatic with shipping  
Authority: Directorate General of Customs and Excise  
Link: https://www.beacukai.go.id/

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
**Myth:** POD requires expensive design skills  
Reality: Basic designs using Canva can succeed; many top sellers aren't professional designers  
Why it matters: Removes barrier for beginners  
Source: Printify seller data showing 70% use free tools  

**Myth:** POD is fully passive income  
Reality: Requires consistent design uploads and marketing effort  
Why it matters: Sets realistic expectations to avoid disappointment  
Source: Industry reports on seller abandonment rates  

**Myth:** International platforms like Etsy/Redbubble don't work for Indonesian sellers
