### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business registration as PT Perorangan or CV for freelance services
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas & UU Cipta Kerja 2020
Process: Online registration via AHU website, submit NPWP, pay fees
Cost: Rp 300,000 for PT Perorangan
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Kemenkumham (Ministry of Law and Human Rights)
Link: https://ahu.go.id/

Requirement: NPWP (Tax ID) for income tax reporting
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan
Process: Register online via djponline.pajak.go.id
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Platform-specific seller verification for accessing advanced features
Regulation: Platform policies (Shopee/Tokopedia seller terms)
Process: Submit KTP, business documents, bank account verification
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-7 days
Authority: Marketplace platforms
Link: https://seller.shopee.co.id/ & https://seller.tokopedia.com/

Requirement: Data protection compliance when handling client product information
Regulation: UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 tentang Pelindungan Data Pribadi
Process: Obtain client consent, secure data storage
Cost: Minimal (free tools available)
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika
Link: https://pdp.go.id/

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Product listing gampang, tinggal upload foto dan deskripsi doang"
Reality: Requires SEO optimization, high-quality photography, platform algorithm knowledge, and client communication skills
Why it matters: Poor listings lead to low visibility and client dissatisfaction
Source: Shopee seller education materials, 2025
Evidence: Optimized listings get 3x more views than basic ones

Myth: "Freelance product lister butuh modal besar untuk tools mahal"
Reality: Can start with free tools (Google Photos, Canva free), smartphone camera, basic laptop
Why it matters: Lowers barrier to entry for young freelancers
Source: Successful lister interviews, 2025
Evidence: Many start with Rp 1-2 million total investment

Myth: "Semua marketplace sama, skill transferable"
Reality: Each platform (Shopee/Tokopedia/Lazada) has different algorithms, policies, and optimization requirements
Why it matters: Clients expect platform-specific expertise
Source: Tokopedia University courses, 2025
Evidence: Platform-specific training required for optimal results

Myth: "Product listing cuma sambilan, gak bisa jadi career utama"
Reality: Can scale to agency with multiple listers, earn Rp 20-50 million/month
Why it matters: Opens career progression opportunities
Source: E-commerce consultant reports, 2025
Evidence: Several successful agencies started as solo freelancers
