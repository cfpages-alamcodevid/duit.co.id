## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)
Regulation: UU No. 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta
Process: Buat template NDA, tandatangani sebelum mulai
Cost: Rp 100-500rb (konsultasi hukum)
Timeline: 1 hari
Authority: Kemenkumham (untuk legalitas)
Link: https://jdih.kemenkumham.go.id/
Relevance: Melindungi kerahasiaan klien

Requirement: Hak cipta konten
Regulation: UU Hak Cipta
Process: Transfer hak cipta ke klien via kontrak
Cost: Gratis (dalam kontrak)
Timeline: Saat penandatanganan
Authority: Dirjen HAKI
Link: https://www.dgip.go.id/

Requirement: Pajak freelance

Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/
