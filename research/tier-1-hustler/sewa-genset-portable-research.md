## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Genset itu berisik dan berasap."
Reality: Modern Inverter gensets are very quiet and cleaner. Marketing these "Silent" units allows for higher rental prices.
Myth: "Cuma laku kalau mati lampu."
Reality: Most rentals are for *outdoor events* where there is no power source at all, not just for backups.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Niche Selection
- Decide between "Silent/Inverter" (for events/electronics) or "Standard" (for construction).

Step 4: Operational Readiness

Step 5: Maintenance Routine
- Oil change every 50-100 hours.
- Run the machine once a week even if no rental.
