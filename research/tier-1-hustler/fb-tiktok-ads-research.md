Regulation: Meta Business Certification Program
Process: Daftar online, ikut exam 410-101, dapat certificate
Cost: Gratis untuk basic, premium features berbayar
Timeline: 1-2 minggu
Authority: Meta/Facebook
Link: https://www.facebook.com/business/learn/certification/exams/410-101-exam
Relevance: Mandatory untuk kredibilitas Facebook ads

Requirement: TikTok Media Buying Certification
Regulation: TikTok Academy Certification
Process: Register, study guide, practice test, 2-hour proctored exam
Cost: ~USD 150 (exam fee)
Timeline: 1 bulan persiapan
Authority: TikTok
Link: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/en-US/academy/tiktok-certification

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Media Buyer cuma klik tombol, gampang dan cepat kaya
Reality: Butuh analisis data, optimasi berkelanjutan, understanding algoritma platform
Why it matters: Banyak pemula gagal karena underestimate complexity
Source: Expert testimonials di TikTok Academy

Myth: Degree marketing wajib untuk jadi Media Buyer
Reality: Banyak sukses tanpa degree, tapi sertifikasi platform penting
Why it matters: Buka peluang untuk fresh graduate bidang lain
Source: YouTube "How to Become Media Buyer 2026"

Myth: Cukup satu platform (Facebook atau TikTok) saja
Reality: Multi-platform skill lebih dicari, bisa cross-promote
Why it matters: Lebih banyak peluang kerja
Source: Job postings di LinkedIn
