## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For Indonesian freelancers:

Requirement: NPWP (Tax ID) if annual income > Rp4.8 million
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan 1984
Process: Register online via djponline.pajak.go.id
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: PT Perorangan registration for formal business
Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja 2024
Process: Online via oss.go.id
Cost: Rp 50.000-100.000
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Kementerian Investasi/BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Business license (NIB) for commercial activities
Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja
Process: Apply via OSS system
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id/
