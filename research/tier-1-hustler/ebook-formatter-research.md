## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### Amazon KDP Policies
*   **EPUB3 Requirement:** Since 2022, Amazon deprecated MOBI. Formatters MUST provide EPUB3 files.
*   **Content Guidelines:** Amazon rejects books with poor navigation (missing Table of Contents).

### Indonesian Context (Freelance)
*   **Tax (W-8BEN):** Freelancers on Upwork/Fiverr must fill this. Use **16-digit NIK (KTP)** to claim the 10% tax treaty rate (instead of 30%).
*   **Payments:** Use **Payoneer** or **Wise** to receive USD and withdraw to local Indonesian banks (BCA/Mandiri).
*   **Language Barrier:** Amazon KDP **does NOT officially support Bahasa Indonesia for eBooks** (per 2025). 
    *   *Advice for service providers:* Focus on global clients (English-language books) or help Indonesian authors publishing in English.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Bisa langsung upload file Word ke Amazon."
*   **Reality:** Bisa, tapi hasilnya sering berantakan (indentasi hilang, gambar pecah, TOC tidak jalan). 
*   **Myth:** "Harus jago desain grafis."
*   **Reality:** E-book formatting lebih ke arah "coding ringan" (HTML/CSS) atau penggunaan tool otomasi. Tidak perlu gambar manual.

## 9. Action Steps Outline

### Step 1: Kuasai "The Holy Trinity" of Formatting
*   Belajar standar EPUB3.
*   Download Kindle Create (Official Amazon tool).
*   Pahami cara kerja Table of Contents (TOC) yang "clickable".
