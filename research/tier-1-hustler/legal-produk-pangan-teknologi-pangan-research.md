## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** PIRT (Pangan Industri Rumah Tangga)  
**Regulation:** PerBPOM 17/2023, OSS RBA  
**Process:** Daftar OSS → NIB → PIRT auto-issue (gratis <100kg/hari)  
**Cost:** Gratis  
**Timeline:** 1-3 hari  
**Authority:** BPOM via OSS  
**Link:** oss.go.id, e-ppirt.pom.go.id  

**Requirement:** Label Nutrisi & Claims  
**Regulation:** PerBPOM 24/2023 Label Pangan  
**Process:** Hitung nutrisi (software gratis BPOM), desain comply font/size  
**Cost:** Gratis (DIY)  
**Timeline:** 1 hari  
**Authority:** BPOM  
**Link:** pom.go.id/label-pangan  

**Requirement:** BPOM Registrasi (ML/ED)  
**Regulation:** UU 18/2012 Pangan, PerBPOM 27/2024  
**Process:** Dokumen (analisis lab Rp500rb-1jt), submit e-reg.pom.go.id  
**Cost:** Rp100rb-3jt (kategori)  
**Timeline:** 30-90 hari  
**Authority:** BPOM  
**Link:** e-reg.pom.go.id  

**Requirement:** Sertifikasi Halal  
**Regulation:** UU 33/2014 JPH, PerBPJPH 28/2021  
**Process:** Audit LPH (LPPOM MUI), submit cerol.lppom.com  
**Cost:** Rp1-5jt (skala UMKM)  
**Timeline:** 30-60 hari  
**Authority:** BPJPH/MUI  
**Link:** halalmui.org/cerol
