## Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** No mandatory license required for motorcycle inspection services
**Regulation:** General business regulations under UU No. 20 Tahun 2008 about Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises
**Process:** Register as individual/business via OSS (Online Single Submission) system, get NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)
**Cost:** Free for initial registration, Rp 50k for annual renewal
**Timeline:** 1-2 days online
