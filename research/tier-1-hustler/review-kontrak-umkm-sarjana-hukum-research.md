### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business/legal articles, research required licenses/permits, tax obligations, regulations.

**Requirement: Business License (NIB)**
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 about Job Creation
Process: Online application through OSS system
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Ministry of Investment
Link: https://oss.go.id/
Relevance: Required to legally operate any business in Indonesia

**Requirement: Tax Registration (NPWP)**
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 about Tax Harmonization
Process: Online registration via e-Registration
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 day
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes
Link: https://ereg.pajak.go.id/
Relevance: Mandatory for income tax reporting

**Requirement: Contract Review Boundaries**
Regulation: UU No. 18 Tahun 2003 about Advocates, PERADI Code of Ethics
Process: Define services as "basic document review", refer complex cases to licensed advocates
Cost: None
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: PERADI (Indonesian Advocates Association)
Link: https://peradi.or.id/
Relevance: Fresh graduates cannot practice law without advokat license

**Requirement: Data Protection Compliance**
Regulation: UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 about Personal Data Protection
Process: Implement data protection in client handling
Cost: Minimal (privacy policy template)

Authority: PDP Authority
