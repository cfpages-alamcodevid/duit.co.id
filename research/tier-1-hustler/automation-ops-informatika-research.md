**Minimum capital to start:** Rp 1-2 million.  
**Breakdown:**  
- Laptop/computer: Rp 3-5 million (if not owned).  
- Internet subscription: Rp 200k/month.  
- Tool subscriptions (Zapier/Make): Rp 100k-300k/month.  
- WhatsApp Business API: Free basic, API via providers Rp 500k setup + per-message costs.  
- Google Workspace: Rp 50k/month.  
- Marketing (social media): Rp 500k initial.  
**Total:** Rp 5-10 million initial (including buffer).  

**Where to buy:**  
- Tools: Zapier.com, Make.com, Google Workspace.  
- WhatsApp API: 360Dialog, Qontak, Kirimi.id.  
- Physical: Computer from Tokopedia/Shopee.  

**Price comparison:**  
- Zapier Pro: $19.99/mo (~Rp 300k).

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** Register as individual/freelancer or PT for services.  
**Regulation:** UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas (PT) or PMSE license for digital services.  
**Process:** Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission), get NIB.  
**Cost:** Rp 50k-500k for registration.  
**Timeline:** 1-7 days.  
**Authority:** Kemenkumham for PT, BKPM for OSS.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Tax registration as PKP if revenue >Rp 4.8 billion/year.  
**Regulation:** UU Pajak Penghasilan.  
**Process:** Register at KPP, use e-Faktur.  
**Cost:** Free registration.  
**Timeline:** 1 month.  
**Authority:** DJP (Direktorat Jenderal Pajak).  
**Link:** https://www.pajak.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Data protection compliance if handling customer data.  
**Regulation:** UU PDP No. 27 Tahun 2022.  
**Process:** Appoint DPO, conduct DPIA for high-risk processing.  
**Cost:** Minimal, training Rp 500k.  
**Timeline:** Ongoing.  
**Authority:** Kemenkominfo.  
**Link:** https://pdp.go.id/
