### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For affiliate marketing business in Indonesia:

Requirement: Business registration via OSS (optional for small scale)
Regulation: UU Cipta Kerja No. 11/2020
Process: Register online at oss.go.id if revenue exceeds Rp 500 million/year
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-2 days
Authority: BKPM
Link: https://oss.go.id

Requirement: Tax registration (NPWP) if annual income > Rp 60 million
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan No. 36/2008
Process: Apply online via djponline.pajak.go.id
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1 week
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://pajak.go.id

Requirement: No specific affiliate marketing license required
Regulation: General e-commerce regulations apply
Process: Self-regulatory, follow platform terms
Cost: None
Timeline: N/A
Authority: Ministry of Communication and Information
Link: https://kominfo.go.id
