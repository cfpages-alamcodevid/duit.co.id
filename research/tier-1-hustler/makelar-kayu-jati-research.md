For business/legal articles, research:
- Required licenses/permits
- Tax obligations
- Relevant UU/regulations
- Registration process
- Compliance requirements
- Penalties for non-compliance

**Format:**
```
Requirement: Business Identification Number (NIB)
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission) system
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 hours online
Authority: BKPM (Badan Koordinasi Penanaman Modal)
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Timber Legality and Sustainability Verification System (SVLK) certificate
Regulation: PP No. 23 Tahun 2021, Permen LHK No. 8 Tahun 2021
Process: Obtain from accredited certification bodies (LVLK), undergo audit for sustainable practices
Cost: Rp 5-20 juta (depending on business size)
Timeline: 1-3 months
Authority: Ministry of Environment and Forestry
Link: https://www.menlhk.go.id/svlk

Requirement: Legal Transport Document (SKSHH for state forest, Nota Angkutan for private land)
Regulation: Permen LHK No. P.32/MenLHK/Setjen/2015
Process: Obtain from forestry office before transport, includes barcode and volume details
Cost: Rp 50rb-200rb per document
Timeline: 1-7 days
Authority: Local forestry office
Link: https://www.menlhk.go.id/

Requirement: V-Legal document for exports
Regulation: Permen Perdagangan No. 97 Tahun 2015
Process: Issued by certification body after SVLK compliance for shipments
Cost: Included in SVLK certification
Timeline: Per shipment
Authority: Ministry of Trade
Link: https://www.kemendag.go.id/
```
