## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Register as Electronic System Provider (PSE) if using digital platforms
Regulation: Government Regulation No. 71/2019 on Electronic Systems and Transactions
Process: Register through Ministry of Communication and Digital Affairs (MOCDA)
Cost: Free for basic registration
Timeline: 1-2 months
Authority: MOCDA
Link: https://kominfo.go.id/

Requirement: Implement personal data protection measures
Regulation: Law No. 27/2022 on Personal Data Protection (PDP Law)
Process: Conduct DPIA, implement security measures, provide privacy policy
Cost: Rp 500.000-2 juta for compliance setup
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: Personal Data Protection Agency
Link: https://pdp.go.id/

Requirement: Business license for document services
Regulation: Risk-Based Business Licensing via OSS system
Process: Register through Online Single Submission (OSS) system
Cost: Varies by business size (Rp 100.000-500.000)
Timeline: 1-3 months
Authority: Ministry of Investment
Link: https://oss.go.id/

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Riset pasar dan persiapan
- Identifikasi target customer (SMEs, kantor, rumah sakit)
- Hitung modal minimal dan sumber equipment
- Belajar basic scanning dan software OCR
- Warning: Jangan beli equipment mahal tanpa test dulu

Step 2: Setup bisnis dan legal
- Daftar OSS untuk izin usaha
- Setup workspace (rumah atau sewa kecil)
- Beli scanner dan software
- Register PSE jika perlu platform digital

Step 3: Marketing dan akuisisi klien pertama
- Buat website sederhana dan sosial media
- Tawarkan paket promo untuk klien awal
- Networking dengan UMKM lokal
- Warning: Jangan janji deadline terlalu ketat di awal

Step 4: Operasional dan scaling
- Implement workflow scanning-indexing-delivery
- Hire freelancer untuk volume tinggi
- Upgrade equipment saat revenue stabil
- Warning: Backup data penting untuk cegah kehilangan
