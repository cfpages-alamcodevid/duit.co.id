## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** Non-diagnostic content only (no advice, symptoms Rx)  
**Regulation:** UU Kedokteran No.29/2004, Kode Etik IDI (Pasal 22: non-praktik tanpa izin)  
**Process:** Cite PubMed/Kemenkes, disclaimer &quot;Bukan pengganti konsultasi dokter&quot;  
**Cost:** Rp 0  
**Timeline:** Instant
