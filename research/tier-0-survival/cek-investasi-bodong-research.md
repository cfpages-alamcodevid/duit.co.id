## 4. Market Research (Disguise Tactics)
- **Tactic 1: Deepfake Experts.** Using AI-generated videos of famous figures (e.g., Raffi Ahmad or Jokowi) to promote a fake platform.
- **Tactic 2: Impersonation.** Copying the name of a real, licensed company (e.g., PT ABC Sekuritas) but with a slightly different website or Telegram group.
- **Tactic 3: Crypto/AI/Gold.** Using "trending" buzzwords to hide the classic Ponzi structure.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Requirement:** Must have an Izin Usaha from OJK (for Finance), Bappebti (for Commodities/Crypto), or BI (for Payments).
- **Regulation:** UU PPSK (2023) and POJK regulations.
- **Principle:** **2L (Legal & Logis)**.
- **Authority:** OJK (Satgas PASTI).

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Expert Quote:** "High return + Zero Risk = 100% Scam. There is no exception."

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Scenario: The "WhatsApp Work" Scam**
1. You get a WA: "Want to earn Rp 500k/day by just liking YouTube videos?"
2. You do 3 tasks, you get Rp 30k. (The Bait).
3. Then: "To get higher commission, join our 'Premium Task'. Deposit Rp 1 million to get Rp 1.5 million."
4. You deposit, and they ask for more "tax" or "fees" to withdraw. You never get the money back.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "If I can withdraw the first few times, it's safe."
- **Reality:** That's how Ponzi works—using new members' money to pay old ones to build trust.
- **Myth:** "They showed me their OJK license certificate."
- **Reality:** Certificates can be photoshopped. Always verify via official OJK channels.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
- **Step 1: The WhatsApp Check.** Chat OJK at 081-157-157-157. Type the name of the app/company.
- **Step 2: Check the "Logis" Factor.** Does the return exceed 10-15% per MONTH? If yes, it's not logical.
- **Step 3: Search "Satgas PASTI List."** Look for the latest PDF on ojk.go.id.
- **Step 4: Check Domain/Social Media.** Scams use Telegram groups and "dodgy" URLs (e.g., .xyz, .site, or long strings of numbers).
- **Step 5: Report.** If you find one, report to waspadainvestasi@ojk.go.id.
