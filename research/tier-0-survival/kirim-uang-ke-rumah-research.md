## 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **Data:** Standard interbank transfer fee in Indonesia is Rp 6.500. BI-FAST reduced it to Rp 2.500.
*   **App Limits (2025/2026):**
    *   **Flip:** Free up to Rp 5M/day (then Rp 2.500).
    *   **DANA:** 10 free transfers/month (min Rp 50k).
    *   **ShopeePay:** Unlimited free transfers for verified accounts (policy varies).
*   **Source:** Official app terms and OJK updates 2025.
*   **Relevance:** Saving Rp 6.500 per week = Rp 338.000 per year (enough for 1 month of electricity).

## 4. Market Research: Transfer Methods
| Method | Cost | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|

| **BI-FAST** | Rp 2.500 | Integrated in mobile banking | Not all banks fully sync |

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **The "ATM Walking" Syndrome:** Many parents see their children as "living ATMs" because there's no transparency about the child's own expenses.
*   **Case Study:** "Sari, 28, Buruh Pabrik". She sent 50% of her salary home, but her brother used it for gaming. She learned to send "item-based" money (paying the electricity bill directly via app) instead of cash.
*   **Key Learning:** Controlling *how* the money is spent is as important as *how much* is sent.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Kirim sedikit-sedikit lebih ringan."
*   **Reality:** Each transaction accumulates admin fees. Bulk sending or direct bill payment is cheaper.
*   **Myth:** "Aplikasi transfer gratis itu nggak aman."
*   **Reality:** Apps like Flip/DANA are supervised by Bank Indonesia.
