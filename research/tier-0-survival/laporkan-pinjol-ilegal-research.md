## 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Satgas PASTI:** In 2024 alone, thousands of illegal entities were blocked.
- **Success Rate:** Reporting to OJK/Satgas results in the app/website being blocked, but reporting to Polisi is needed for criminal prosecution.
- **Key Evidence Needed:**
  1. Transaction History (Mutasi Rekening)
  2. Screenshots of threats (WA/SMS/Social Media)
  3. App Name and Download Link
  4. Phone numbers used by DC

## 4. Market Research (N/A)
- **Cost:** Reporting to OJK and Polisi is FREE. Beware of scams offering "jasa tutup pinjol" or "jasa hapus data" which are 99% scams.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Regulation:** UU PPSK (2023) gives more power to Satgas PASTI to block and prosecute.
- **Basis for Police Report:** Pasal 27-29 UU ITE (Intimidasi, Pencemaran nama baik, penyebaran data pribadi) and Pasal 368 KUHP (Pemerasan).
- **Process:**
  - OJK: Administrative blocking.
  - Police: Criminal investigation.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Expert Insight:** "Don't just delete the app. Screenshot everything first. Evidence is your only weapon." - Cyber Crime Investigator.
- **Case Study:** A victim reported to `patrolisiber.id` with complete screenshots and log calls. Within 48 hours, the DC numbers were blocked by providers and the case was logged for future group action.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
- **The "Reporting Template":**
  "Saya [Nama], ingin melaporkan aplikasi [Nama Pinjol] yang tidak berizin OJK. Saya telah mendapatkan ancaman berupa [sebutkan ancaman] dari nomor [sebutkan nomor]. Terlampir bukti mutasi dan tangkapan layar chat."

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Melapor ke OJK otomatis melunasi hutang saya."
- **Reality:** Melapor menghentikan aktivitas ilegalnya, tapi kewajiban bayar pokok tetap ada secara moral/perdata, meski secara hukum ilegal tidak punya legal standing untuk menagih.
- **Myth:** "Polisi tidak akan mengurus kasus kecil seperti ini."
- **Reality:** Laporan Anda membantu Polisi memetakan jaringan sindikat pinjol ilegal.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1. **Document Everything:** Use a dedicated folder for screenshots.
2. **Step 1: Report to Satgas PASTI (OJK):** Use WhatsApp or Email.
3. **Step 2: Report to Kominfo:** For app/website blocking.
4. **Step 3: Report to Polri (Cyber Crime):** For criminal intimidation.
5. **Step 4: Report to Bank:** If they use a specific bank account for collection, report it to the bank to freeze that account.
