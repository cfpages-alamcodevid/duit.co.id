### 6. Extreme Frugality Hacks (2026 Edition)
- **Transport:** Maximize "Kartu Transportasi" subscriptions (e.g., MRT/LRT monthly pass).
- **Food:** "Meal Prep Minggu Malam" - Cooking rice and 1 protein for 3 days.

### 7. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Gaji UMR gak mungkin bisa nabung."
- **Reality:** Savings start with Rp 10,000. Consistency > Amount.
- **Myth:** "Self-reward itu wajib tiap gajian."
- **Reality:** Self-reward is for winners who hit their savings goal, not for every "hard week."
- **Myth:** "Investasi cuma buat orang kaya."
- **Reality:** RDPU starts at Rp 10,000.

### 8. Action Steps Outline
**Step 1: The "Financial Audit"**
- Track every 1,000 Rupiah for 30 days using an app or notebook.
**Step 2: Fix the Leak**
- Identify "Ghost Expenses" (admin fees, unnecessary snacks).
**Step 3: The 50/20/30 (Survival Mod)**
- 50% Needs, 20% Emergency Fund (Until reached), 30% Everything else.
**Step 4: Automated Savings**
- Set up auto-debit of Rp 200,000 on payday. If you don't see it, you don't spend it.
**Step 5: Income Booster (Tier 1 Preview)**
- Look for side hustles that don't require capital (Skill-based).
