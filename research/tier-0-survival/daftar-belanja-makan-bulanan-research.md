### 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Assess current food spending and waste
- Details: Track 1 week expenses, identify waste patterns
- Example: Use simple notebook or free app
- Warning: Don't judge, just observe

Step 2: Plan weekly menus based on sales/promotions
- Details: Choose 5-7 core recipes, vary proteins
- Example: Monday: Ayam goreng, Tuesday: Ikan bakar
- Warning: Include family preferences to ensure compliance

Step 3: Create shopping list with quantities
- Details: Calculate portions, check pantry first
- Example: 2kg beras, 1kg ayam, vegetables list
- Warning: Add buffer for unexpected needs

Step 4: Shop strategically (traditional market + online)
- Details: Compare prices, use apps for deals
- Example: Buy staples in bulk, fresh items weekly
- Warning: Stick to list to avoid impulse buys

Step 5: Track and adjust monthly
- Details: Review spending vs plan, adjust recipes
- Example: If over budget, reduce portions or find cheaper alternatives
- Warning: Be flexible, not rigid
