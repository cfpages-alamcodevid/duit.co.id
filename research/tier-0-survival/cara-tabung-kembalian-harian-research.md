# Research: Cara Tabung Kembalian Harian

## Mathematical Case Study
```
Belanja: Rp 22.500
Bayar: Rp 23.000
Kembalian: Rp 500
5x sehari = Rp 2.500/hari
25 hari = Rp 62.500/bulan
1 tahun = Rp 750.000
```

## 4 Methods of Micro-Saving

### Method 1: Transparent Jar (Beginners)
- Tools: Transparent plastic jar (Rp 15-30rb)
- Rules: Koin 100, 200, 500 masuk jar; Koin 1.000+ simpan terpisah
- Target: Rp 50.000-200.000/bulan

### Method 2: Daily Envelopes (Office Workers)
- Tools: Small envelopes, drawer at work
- Process: Each transaction → envelope with date/amount → count Friday → transfer to bank
- Benefit: Visible progress, reduces temptation

### Method 3: Digital Coin Apps
- Jago (Bank Jago): "Kotak Jago" auto-save from Gopay/OVO
- Jenius (BTPN): "Teman Jago" auto-cut Rp 1.000-5.000 per transaction
- OVO/Gopay/Dana: Auto-save to digital savings

### Method 4: Three-Box System (Families)
- Box 1: DARURAT (60%)
- Box 2: HIBURAN (20%)
- Box 3: INVESTASI (20%)

## Withdrawal Rules

### ALLOWED (Emergency)
- Sudden illness (fever, accident)
- Urgent transport (to hospital, work)
- Parking payment for important meetings

### FORBIDDEN (Strict)
- New clothes (unless damaged)
- Treating friends (non-emergency)
- New gadgets (unless broken for work)
- Gambling/online games
- Cinema/luxury entertainment

## Psycological Trics
1. **Visual Effect**: Use transparent jar, place in visible location
2. **"Half-Forbidden" Rule**: Can only withdraw when half-full
3. **1-Minute Daily Logging**: Record in small notebook
4. **Gamification**: Set level targets (Rp 100k=level 1, Rp 500k=level 5)
5. **Buddy System**: Compete with roommate/friend

## Emergency Conversion to Cash
1. Sell to scrap collector (Rp 50-100rb/karung)
2. Exchange at pulsa counter (convert to credit/data)
3. Bring to bank (free service, takes 1-2 days)
4. Pegadaian (for silver/rare coins)

## Common Mistakes
1. Forgetting to insert coins daily → Place jar at door/workspace
2. Lazy to sort coins → Dump in big jar monthly, count at month-end
3. Fear of theft → Buy small digital safe (Rp 100-200rb)
4. Coins too dirty → Wash with soap, dry under sun
