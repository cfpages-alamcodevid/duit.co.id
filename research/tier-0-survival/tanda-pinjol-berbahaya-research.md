### 4. The 10 Red Flags (2026 Update)
1.  **Offers via WhatsApp/SMS/Social DM:** Legal pinjol are BANNED from marketing via private channels.
2.  **Asking for "Advance Fees":** Requesting "admin/deposit" money before the loan is liquidated.
3.  **Contact/Gallery Access:** Asking for permission to see your photos or friend list.
4.  **No Clear Physical Office:** Website uses generic images or has no verifiable address.
5.  **Extremely High Interest (>0.1% per day):** Legal limit is now 0.1% for consumer loans.
6.  **Unclear Fees:** Not showing the total amount to be paid back clearly before signing.
7.  **Modus "Salah Transfer":** Sending money randomly to your account and then demanding interest.
8.  **Threatening Language:** Using words like "Lapor Polisi," "Sebar Data," or "Datang ke Rumah" in the initial offer phase.
9.  **APK via Chat:** Asking you to download a file outside the Google Play Store/App Store.
10. **Impersonating Big Brands:** Using logos of OJK, Bank Indonesia, or famous banks (BCA/BRI) in a fake app.

### 5. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Scenario: The "Salah Transfer" Trap**
- **Victim:** Receives Rp 1,000,000 in their bank account.
- **The Call:** "Maaf kami salah transfer, tolong kembalikan Rp 1,500,000 sekarang atau kami laporkan penipuan."
- **Correct Action:** DO NOT transfer to the caller. Go to your bank, report the suspicious transfer, and ask the bank to return it to the source officially. Report to Satgas PASTI.

**Scenario: The "AI Deepfake" Scam**
- **Victim:** Gets a voice note from "Mom" saying she's held by a debt collector and needs Rp 5M now.
- **Correct Action:** Call Mom directly on her known number. Do not trust voice notes.

### 6. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Kalau ada logo OJK, pasti aman."
- **Reality:** Scammers use OJK logos easily. Always check the OJK whitelist via WA 081157157157.
- **Myth:** "Pinjol ilegal gak usah dibayar, aman kok."
- **Reality:** While legally void, the *harassment* and *data leakage* are real and damaging. Better to avoid than to "play" with them.
- **Myth:** "Pinjol legal gak mungkin kasar."
- **Reality:** Some rogue legal DC agents exist, but you have the right to report them to OJK/AFPI.
