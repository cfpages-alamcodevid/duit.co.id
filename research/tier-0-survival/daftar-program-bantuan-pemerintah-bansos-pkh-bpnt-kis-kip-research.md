## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Registration in Data Tunggal Sosial Ekonomi Nasional (DTSEN)
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Sosial (Permensos) No. 1 Tahun 2018 and updates
Process: Automatic inclusion through village/sub-district verification or manual application via local social office
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-3 months verification
Authority: Kementerian Sosial (Kemensos)
Link: https://kemensos.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: Valid Indonesian citizenship with NIK and KK
Regulation: Undang-Undang No. 24 Tahun 2013 tentang Administrasi Kependudukan
Process: Birth registration and ID issuance through Dukcapil
Cost: Free for basic registration
Timeline: Immediate upon birth/application
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Kependudukan dan Catatan Sipil (Dukcapil)
Link: https://dukcapil.kemendagri.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: BPJS Health membership for PBI recipients
Regulation: Undang-Undang No. 40 Tahun 2004 tentang Sistem Jaminan Sosial Nasional
Process: Automatic enrollment upon DTSEN verification
Cost: Government-subsidized (Rp42,000/month)
Timeline: Immediate upon approval
Authority: BPJS Kesehatan
Link: https://bpjs-kesehatan.go.id/
```

```
Requirement: School enrollment verification for PIP
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Pendidikan dan Kebudayaan No. 19 Tahun 2023
```

```
- Warning: Report discrepancies immediately to avoid suspension
```
