## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** "Pinjol resmi tidak akan ancam atau sebar data"
Reality: Banyak laporan korban pinjol legal juga mengalami penagihan kasar
Why it matters: Tidak semua legal berarti aman, tetap waspada
Source: OJK consumer reports 2025

**Myth:** "Kalau sudah bayar lunas, pinjol tidak akan ganggu lagi"
Reality: Kasus HFS membayar lunas tapi tetap diperas 4 tahun
Why it matters: Data sudah dicuri, bisa digunakan terus
Source: Bareskrim Polri case 2025

**Myth:** "Verifikasi OTP aman karena dari bank resmi"
Reality: Scammer bisa phish OTP melalui malware
Why it matters: OTP bukan jaminan 100% aman
Source: Mastercard Scam Report 2025

**Myth:** "Literasi digital cuma untuk orang tua, anak muda sudah paham"
Reality: 66% youth encountered scams in 2025
Why it matters: Young adults equally vulnerable
Source: Indonesia Anti-Scam Center

## 9. Action Steps Outline

**Step 1:** Assess your digital literacy level
- Check current knowledge gaps
- Take free OJK digital literacy quiz
- Example: Score yourself 1-10 on scam recognition

**Step 2:** Verify before sharing data
- Always check OJK licensed platforms
- Use Privy for document verification
- Warning: Never share full KTP for small loans

**Step 3:** Create secure digital habits
- Use password manager and 2FA
- Limit app permissions
- Warning: Don't save payment info on unsecured sites

**Step 4:** Know your rights and reporting
- Report scams within 5 minutes to IASC
- Understand consumer protection laws
- Warning: Delayed reporting reduces recovery chances

**Step 5:** Build emergency response plan
- Save emergency contacts
- Prepare mental health support
- Warning: Don't isolate yourself when victimized
