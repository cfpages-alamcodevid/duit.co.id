### 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **POJK 38/2025:** OJK now has "Institutional Right to Sue" (Hak Gugat Institusional) to help consumers recover losses.
- **Interest Cap 2025-2026:** Consumer interest & late fees capped at **0.2% per day (2025)** and **0.1% per day (2026)**.
- **App Access Limit (CAMILAN):** Legal apps can ONLY access **Camera, Microphone, and Location**. Contacts/Gallery access is a crime.
- **Criminal Penalties (UU PPSK):** Up to **10 years prison** or **Rp 250 billion fine** for unethical collection/data abuse.

---

### 4. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **UU Pelindungan Data Pribadi (UU PDP):** Protects your digital identity. Creditors cannot use your data for anything other than the agreed purpose.
- **UU Pengembangan dan Penguatan Sektor Keuangan (UU PPSK):** Elevates financial consumer protection to the same level as other major crimes.
- **POJK 22/2023:** Specifically bans the use of "Emergency Contacts" as targets for debt collection.

### 7. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Hutang pinjol bisa bikin masuk penjara."
- **Reality:** Utang-piutang is a civil matter. You cannot be jailed for being poor/unable to pay.
- **Myth:** "DC boleh datang kapan saja dan ambil barang di rumah."
- **Reality:** DC can only come between 08.00 - 20.00. They CANNOT seize goods without a court order (unlike a bank with Fidusia collateral).
- **Myth:** "Kontak darurat wajib ikut bayar utang."
- **Reality:** Emergency contacts are only for *confirmation of location*, not for debt responsibility.
