## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Requirement:** Debt collectors must have SPPI (Sertifikasi Profesi Penagihan Pembiayaan) certification.
- **Regulation:** POJK 10/2022 and SEOJK 19/2023.
- **Authority:** OJK and Satgas PASTI (Pemberantasan Aktivitas Keuangan Ilegal).
- **Time Limits:** Collection only allowed until 20.00 local time.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Expert Insight:** "Debt collectors of illegal lenders use psychological terror because they have no legal standing to sue you in court." - Common legal aid advice (LBH Jakarta/LBH Fintech).
- **Case Study:** Someone who paid "extension fees" (*biaya perpanjangan*) multiple times but the principal never decreased. Result: Lost Rp20 million for a Rp1 million loan.
- **Key Learning:** Stop paying extension fees. Only pay the principal if you have the money, but only to verified legal accounts (if legal) or better, report them first if illegal.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
- **Scenario 1:** DC threatens to blast photos to contacts.
- **Action:** Inform contacts first (the "Blast Clarification" message), block the DC, report to OJK.
- **Scenario 2:** DC claims to be "Polisi" or "Tim Lapangan Pusat".
- **Action:** Ask for their KTA (Kartu Tanda Anggota) and name of the police station. Usually, they will go silent.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Saya bisa dipenjara karena tidak bayar pinjol."
- **Reality:** Hutang piutang adalah perkara perdata, tidak bisa dipidana penjara (Pasal 19 ayat 2 UU No. 39 Tahun 1999 tentang HAM).
- **Myth:** "Debt collector ilegal punya hak menyita barang di rumah."
- **Reality:** Penyitaan hanya boleh dilakukan oleh juru sita pengadilan dengan putusan hakim.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1. **Stop the Panic:** Turn off the phone for a few hours to regain focus.
2. **Secure Your Digital Perimeter:** Lock social media, notify close contacts.
3. **Audit the Debt:** Is it legal or illegal? Check OJK status.
4. **Collect Evidence:** Screenshot all threats, don't delete chats.
5. **The "Silent Treatment" or "Firm Script":** Only respond once saying "I will pay through the app when I have the funds, stop the harassment or I will report you to Satgas PASTI."
6. **Official Reporting:** Report to OJK, Polisi, and Kominfo.
