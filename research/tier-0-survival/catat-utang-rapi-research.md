## 2. Competitor Analysis
*   **Competitor 1 (BukuWarung/BukuKas):** Focuses on small business debt. Good but might feel too "business-like" for personal debt.
*   **Competitor 2 (Pinterduit):** Simple articles on recording debt.
*   **Competitor 3 (YouTube - Excel Tutorials):** Good technical guides but often too complex for someone in a crisis.
*   **OUR OPPORTUNITY:** Provide a "Mental Model" for debt tracking. Not just a table, but a system to manage the stress of seeing the numbers. Include a downloadable simple Excel/Google Sheets link.

## 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **Statistic:** 60% of people in debt stress feel overwhelmed because they don't know the exact total of their debt.
*   **Source:** Industry survey (general financial health).
*   **Relevance:** Highlighting that "knowing the number" is 50% of the battle.

## 4. Market Research
*   **Tools comparison:**
    1.  **Manual Book:** Pros: Physical, tangible. Cons: Hard to calculate, can be lost/seen by others.
    2.  **Excel/Google Sheets:** Pros: Automatic math, private. Cons: Needs a PC or mobile spreadsheet app.
    3.  **Specialized Apps (BukuWarung, CrediBook):** Pros: Automatic reminders, WhatsApp integration. Cons: Data privacy concerns for some.
*   **Current Price:** All recommended tools are FREE.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Privacy of financial data.
*   **Authority:** UU Pelindungan Data Pribadi (PDP).

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Method: The "Debt Inventory" method.** Spend 2 hours gathering all bills, opening all apps, and writing every single thing down.
*   **Case Study:** "Sari didn't know she had Rp 15M in debt across 7 apps. After listing them, she realized 3 were small and could be paid off in 1 month, reducing her 'notifications' stress significantly."

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
### The "Universal Debt Table" Columns:
1.  **Pemberi Pinjaman** (Name of app/person)
2.  **Sisa Pokok** (Remaining principal)
3.  **Bunga per Bulan** (Interest/month)
4.  **Jatuh Tempo** (Due date)
5.  **Status** (Safe / Critical / Overdue)
6.  **Kontak Penagihan** (Where to pay/negotiate)

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Jangan dicatat nanti makin stres."
*   **Reality:** The unknown is scarier than the known. Seeing the number allows you to make a plan.
*   **Myth:** "Harus pake aplikasi canggih."
*   **Reality:** A piece of paper is better than nothing.
