## 9. Action Steps Outline

Step 1: Prepare for the conversation
- Choose right time and place
- Gather all financial documents
- Prepare emotionally (manage fear/shame)
- Example: Weekend family meeting with agenda

Step 2: Start with empathy and facts
- Acknowledge feelings first
- Present facts without blame
- Use "I" statements
- Warning: Avoid accusatory language

Step 3: Create joint financial assessment
- List all debts, incomes, expenses together
- Identify immediate priorities
- Example: Family budget planning session

Step 4: Develop communication habits
- Regular financial check-ins
- Family financial education
- Warning: Don't let one person dominate decisions

Step 5: Seek professional help if needed
- Financial advisors for debt restructuring
- Family counselors for communication issues
- Example: OJK mediation for severe cases
