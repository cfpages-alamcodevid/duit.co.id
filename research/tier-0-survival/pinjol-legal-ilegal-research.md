### 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Total Entities Blocked (2026 Target):** Satgas PASTI aims to block 3,000+ new illegal entities annually.
- **CAMILAN Rule:** Legal apps can ONLY access **Camera, Microphone, Location**. Illegal ones ask for **Contacts, Gallery, Call History**.
- **Interest Cap 2026:** Legal consumer loans capped at **0.1% per day**. Illegal ones often 1% - 3% per day.
- **OJK WhatsApp Bot:** **081 157 157 157** (Primary verification tool).

### 4. Market Research (Modus Operandi 2026)
- **Clone Apps:** Scammers create apps with names very similar to legal ones (e.g., "Kredivoo" instead of "Kredivo").
- **SMS/WA Marketing:** 100% of pinjol offers via SMS or WA to unknown numbers are **ILEGAL**. Legal ones are forbidden by OJK from doing this.
- **Transfer "Salah Sasaran":** Sending money to people who never applied as a trap.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The 5-Minute Verification Checklist:**
1.  **Check the Channel (1 min):** Did you get the offer via SMS/WA? Yes -> ILEGAL.

- **Myth:** "Kalau ada di Google Play Store pasti legal."

- **Myth:** "Pinjol legal bunganya sama mahalnya dengan yang ilegal."
- **Reality:** Legal rates are capped (0.1% in 2026). Illegal rates have no limit and can double your debt in a week.
- **Myth:** "Pinjol ilegal gak punya DC lapangan."

### 9. Action Steps Outline
**Step 1: The "Bot First" Rule**
- Save the OJK WhatsApp number. Always check there first.
**Step 2: Scrutinize the App Store Listing**
- Look for the developer name. If it's a gmail address or a random name (e.g., "John Doe Dev"), it's illegal.
**Step 3: Read the Permissions Pop-up**
- If it asks for "Contacts" or "Storage," click DENY and uninstall.
**Step 4: Verify the Official Website**
- Check if the app has a professional website with a `https` protocol and a physical address in Indonesia.
**Step 5: Share the Knowledge**
- Tell your family about the CAMILAN rule.
