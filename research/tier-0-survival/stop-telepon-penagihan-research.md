## 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **Statistic:** Debt collection calls are only legal between 08:00 AM - 08:00 PM local time.
*   **Source:** SEOJK No. 19/SEOJK.06/2023.
*   **Year:** 2024-2026.
*   **Relevance:** Anything outside these hours is a violation.

## 4. Market Research
*   **Blocking Tools:**
    1.  **GetContact:** Best for Indonesia (shows tags like "DC Pinjol XXX").
    2.  **Truecaller:** Good global database.
    3.  **Android "Block Unknown Callers":** Very effective but blocks everyone not in your contacts.
*   **DC Tactics:** "Robocalls" (autodialers) that call every 5 minutes. Teaching the "Silent Mode" or "DND" strategy is crucial.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** DC must have a "Sertifikat Penagihan" (SPPI) and show it if requested.
*   **Restriction:** Cannot contact family/friends NOT listed as Emergency Contacts.
*   **Restriction:** Even Emergency Contacts can only be asked about the debtor's location, NOT asked to pay the debt.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Case Study:** "Andi was called 40 times a day by an illegal pinjol. He tried to explain but it didn't work. He then used GetContact to block all 'Spam' calls and informed his contacts via WA Status. The harassment stopped after 1 week of zero response."
*   **Expert Advice:** "Don't engage with illegal DC. They use psychological warfare. The only win is to not play."

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
### The "Digital Shield" Protocol:
1.  **Install GetContact:** Enable "Spam Protection."
2.  **Phone Settings:** Enable "Silence Unknown Callers" (iOS) or "Block numbers not in contacts" (Android).
3.  **WA Settings:** Change "Who can add me to groups" to "My Contacts" only.
4.  **Auto-Reply (Optional):** "Nomor ini tidak menerima panggilan dari pihak yang melanggar aturan penagihan OJK. Silakan hubungi via email resmi."

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Kalau diblokir nanti polisi datang ke rumah."
*   **Reality:** Polisi handles criminal cases. Debt is civil (perdata). Blocking a harassing phone call is your right to peace.
*   **Myth:** "DC boleh menagih ke kantor atau bos kita."
*   **Reality:** Strictly forbidden unless that office number is the primary contact provided (and even then, there are limits).

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Verify the Creditor:** Legal (OJK) or Ilegal?
2.  **Set the Boundary:** Inform them of OJK rules (hours, ethics).
3.  **Deploy Tech Shields:** GetContact / Truecaller setup.
4.  **Manage Contacts:** Preparing friends/family for potential "blast" calls.
5.  **Official Complaint:** Reporting to OJK/AFPI/Polri if harassment continues.
