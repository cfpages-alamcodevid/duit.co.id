### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Accreditation as Organisasi Bantuan Hukum (OBH) by BPHN
Regulation: Undang-Undang Nomor 16 Tahun 2011 tentang Bantuan Hukum
Process: Apply for accreditation through BPHN, meet requirements for advocates and paralegals
Cost: Free accreditation process, no fees for users receiving aid
Timeline: 3-6 months accreditation process
Authority: Badan Pembinaan Hukum Nasional (BPHN) under Kementerian Hukum dan HAM
Link: https://bphn.go.id/

Requirement: Free legal aid for eligible recipients
Regulation: UU 16/2011 and POJK 77/2016 for fintech-related issues
Process: Assessment of economic status and case merit by LBH
Cost: Completely free for approved cases
Timeline: Immediate consultation, case handling varies by complexity
Authority: Individual LBH organizations
Link: https://jdih.kemenkumham.go.id/

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
Case Study: LBH Jakarta Pinjol Complaint Center
Background: LBH Jakarta opened dedicated center for pinjol victims in 2018, expanded in 2021
Starting point: Responding to 283 initial complaints of illegal debt collection practices
Result: Helped hundreds of victims stop harassment, negotiate fair settlements, report illegal lenders to authorities
Timeline: Ongoing since 2018, with peak activity 2021-2025
Key learning: Many victims unaware of their rights; debt collectors often violate criminal laws on harassment
Source: LBH Jakarta annual reports and Kompas news articles

Case Study: LBH Pekanbaru Pinjol Posko
Background: Rural-based LBH in Riau province
Starting point: Opening complaint post for online lending victims in 2019
Result: Documented systematic violations including illegal interest rates and family harassment
Timeline: 2019-2025, handled dozens of cases leading to police reports
Key learning: Rural areas particularly vulnerable to fintech abuses due to limited financial literacy
Source: LBH Pekanbaru website and local news

Expert Opinion: "Legal aid must reach remote areas where financial exploitation is highest"
Source: YLBHI Director, various interviews
Relevance: Supports focus on geographical accessibility for tier-0 audience

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Bantuan hukum LBH hanya untuk orang miskin ekstrem
Reality: Bantuan hukum gratis tersedia untuk siapa saja yang tidak mampu membayar pengacara swasta, termasuk kelas menengah terdampak krisis
Why it matters: Banyak orang menghindari LBH karena stigma, padahal UU 16/2011 mengatur untuk "setiap orang" yang membutuhkan
Source: UU 16/2011 Pasal 5

Myth: LBH hanya menangani kasus pidana berat
Reality: LBH menangani berbagai kasus termasuk perdata, konsumen, dan keuangan seperti pinjol, kredit, dan waris
Why it matters: Orang berpikir LBH seperti pengacara pidana saja, padahal cakupan luas
Source: YLBHI service reports

Myth: Bantuan hukum di daerah terpencil tidak ada atau mahal
Reality: Ada LBH di 34 provinsi dengan cabang di kabupaten, didanai pemerintah sehingga gratis
Why it matters: Stereotip bahwa hukum mahal mencegah akses, padahal sistem bantuan hukum dirancang untuk daerah remote
Source: BPHN accreditation data
