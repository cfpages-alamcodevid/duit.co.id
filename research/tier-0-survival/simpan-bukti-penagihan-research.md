## 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **Statistic:** Digital evidence is valid in Indonesia under UU ITE (Electronic Information and Transactions Law).
*   **Source:** UU No. 11 Tahun 2008 jo UU No. 19 Tahun 2016.
*   **Relevance:** Legal basis for using screenshots and recordings.

## 4. Market Research
*   **Technical Challenges:**
    1.  **Android 10+:** Google blocks call recording apps. Need "Internal Audio + Mic" screen recording or a second phone.
    2.  **WhatsApp Export:** People often just screenshot. A full "Export Chat" (.txt) is much better for large logs.
    3.  **Deleted Messages:** "Message Deleted" by DC is a common tactic. Need to teach how to use "Notification History" on Android.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Evidence must be authentic and whole (original).
*   **Authority:** Kepolisian RI & OJK.
*   **Validation:** Screenshots should show the sender's full phone number, not just a name.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Tip:** Always do a "Screen Recording" of you scrolling through the chat. This proves it's not a single edited image (Photoshop).
*   **Case Study:** "Debitur A reported a DC for threats. The report was rejected because he only had cropped screenshots. Debitur B's report was accepted because he had a video of himself scrolling through the threatening messages."

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
### The "Digital Evidence Box" (Checklist):
- [ ] **Full Chat Export:** WhatsApp -> More -> Export Chat (Include Media).
- [ ] **Scrolling Video:** Screen record yourself scrolling the chat from top to bottom.
- [ ] **Caller Log:** Screenshot of call history showing the frequency (e.g., 50 calls in 1 hour).
- [ ] **Contact Info:** Screenshot of the "Contact Info" page in WA showing the phone number.
- [ ] **Cloud Backup:** Save all these to a specific Google Drive/iCloud folder named "Bukti Pinjol".

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Screenshot aja cukup."
*   **Reality:** Screenshots are easily faked. Video and .txt exports are much stronger.
*   **Myth:** "Kalau chat dihapus DC, buktinya hilang."
*   **Reality:** Use Notification History or Third-party notification loggers (carefully) to catch "deleted" messages.
