# Research: Cara Jual HP Lama dengan Aman

## Data Preparation Checklist

### 1. Backup All Data
- Photos/videos → Google Photos/laptop
- Contacts → Export to Google account
- WhatsApp messages → Google Drive backup
- Documents, notes, music

### 2. Sign Out from Accounts (CRITICAL!)
**Android**:
- Settings → Accounts → Remove Google/Samsung/Mi accounts
- Turn OFF Find My Device
**iPhone**:
- Settings → [Name] → Sign Out
- Turn OFF Find My iPhone
- Remove from Account

### 3. Factory Reset
**Android**: Settings → System → Reset → Erase all data
**iPhone**: Settings → General → Transfer or Reset → Erase All Content
**Important**: Leave HP in setup wizard state (ready for new owner)

### 4. Physical Cleaning
- Microfiber cloth + 70% alcohol
- Cotton buds for ports/speakers
- Remove dust from charing port, jack, speakers
- Clean front/back cameras gently
- Minor scratch removal: toothpaste on cotton bud

### 5. Condition Checklist
```
HARDWARE:
□ Screen: pristine/minor scratches/cracks
□ Body: minor/major scratches
□ Camera: clear/blurry/foggy
□ Battery: healthy/draining fast (%-life)
□ Charging port: tight/loose
□ Buttons: responsive/stiff
□ Speakers/mic: clear/crackling

COMPLETENESS:
□ HP unit (complete)
□ Original charger (yes/no, brand)
□ Data cable (condition)
□ Earphones/headset (yes/no, working)
□ Sim tool (yes/no)
□ Box & manual (yes/no - usually lost for old HP)
□ Bonus: tempered glass, casing, stickers
```

## Safe Transaction Protocol

### Meeting Location (Priority Order)
1. **Busy area daytime** (10:00-16:00): Mall food courts, Starbucks, Kopi Kenangan
2. **24h minimarkets**: Indomaret/Alfamart with CCTV
3. **Train stations**: Waiting areas (Jakarta Kota, Sudirman, Gondangdia)

**NEVER meet at**:
- Small residential alleys
- Dark night streets
- Underground parking
- Private homes (yours or theirs)

### Safety Protocol
**Before meeting**:
1. Screenshot buyer profile, send to close friend
2. Inform friend: "Meeting at [location] at [time]"
3. Share Google Maps location live
4. HP battery >50%, bring powerbank
5. Wear safe clothing (wallet in front pocket)

**During meeting**:
1. Observe surroundings for suspicious people
2. Sit in open area, not dark corner
3. Let buyer inspect HP first
4. **Count money directly** when received
5. Keep money in front pocket immediately

**Transfer safety**:
- Only accept via **bank transfer** (not fake screenshots)
- Verify funds **actually in account** (check balance, not SMS)
- Use platform escrow (OLX Pay, Tokopedia)
- **NEVER refund** "overpayment" without bank confirmation

## Scam Patterns

### Modus 1: Fake Transfer Screenshot
- Sends screenshot of transfer (larger amount than price)
- Asks to refund excess to different number
- **Prevention**: Call bank CS to confirm, never refund

### Modus 2: HP Switching
- While "checking", drops HP "accidentally"
- Offers exchange: "My HP is better, take this + cash"
- **Prevention**: Never accept HP exchange, hold HP firmly

### Modus 3: Intentional Damage
- Presses buttons hard, drops, splashes water
- Demands 50% price cut
- **Prevention**: Hold HP while demonstrating, don't release grip

### Modus 4: Robbery Disguised
- Group of 2-3 "buyers" distract you
- One grabs HP/wallet
- **Prevention**: Avoid groups, always bring friend, meet in busy places

## Emergency Alternatives

- Bring HP + KTP

- Cash same day
- Can redeem within 3 months
- **Note**: Only accepts certain brands (iPhone, Samsung, Xiaomi, OPPO, Vivo)

### Option 2: Sell to Electronic Scrap Collectors
- Quick cash (30-50% of market)
- No hassle, no photos needed
- **Only for**: Extreme emergencies

### Option 3: Trade-in at Electronic Stores
- Bring old HP to Ace, Erafone, Cellular
- Get discount on new HP
- **Drawback**: Much lower value than private sale

### Option 4: Sell to Close Relatives/Friends
- Safest option (100% safe)
- Discount 10-20% from market
- Can credit (installments) if trusted
