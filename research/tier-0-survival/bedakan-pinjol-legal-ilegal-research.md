## 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Interest Rate Caps (2026):** 0.1% per day for consumer loans (SEOJK 19/2023).
- **Permissions (CAMILAN):** Camera, Microphone, Location. **NOT** Contacts, Gallery, or Social Media.
- **Status 2026:** All legal P2P must be "Berizin" (Licensed). The "Terdaftar" status is no longer valid for old players.
- **New Rule:** Borrowers are limited to a maximum of 3 P2P platforms simultaneously.

## 4. Market Research
- **Illegal P2P Modus:** They often use names very similar to legal ones (e.g., "AdaKami" vs "AdaKreditKami").
- **Marketing:** Illegal pinjol use SMS/WA spam. Legal pinjol are prohibited from marketing via private communication without consent.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **OJK Contact:** WhatsApp 081-157-157-157 (Type the app name to check).
- **Regulation:** POJK 10/2022 and its 2024 updates regarding "Manfaat Ekonomi" (Total cost of loan).
- **Identification:** Legal P2P must display the OJK logo AND the AFPI (Asosiasi Fintech Pendanaan Bersama Indonesia) logo.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Expert Tip:** "If an app asks for access to your contacts before the loan is even approved, it's 100% illegal."
- **Case Study:** A user downloaded an app that looked exactly like "Kredit Pintar" from a link in an SMS. It was a "cloned" illegal app.
- **Learning:** Only download from official Google Play Store / Apple App Store and verify the developer name.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Semua yang ada di Play Store itu legal."
- **Reality:** Google tries to filter, but illegal apps often sneak in using different names or as "Calculators/Guides" before switching to lending.
- **Myth:** "Kalau ada logo OJK, pasti aman."
- **Reality:** Logo OJK sangat mudah dicatut. Verifikasi via WA OJK adalah satu-satunya cara valid.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1. **The 3-Second Visual Check:** Look for OJK & AFPI logos.
2. **The Permission Check:** Go to Phone Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Permissions. If "Contacts" or "Storage" is required, uninstall.
3. **The WA OJK Verification:** Send app name to 081-157-157-157.
4. **The Marketing Check:** Did you get the link from an unsolicited SMS? If yes, it's illegal.
5. **The Interest Check:** Read the simulation carefully. Does it exceed 0.1% per day?
