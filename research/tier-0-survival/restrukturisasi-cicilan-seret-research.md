*   **Relevance:** Legal basis for demanding relief.

## 4. Market Research
*   **Restructuring Types:**
    1.  **Rescheduling:** Extending tenor (e.g., from 12 months to 24 months).
    2.  **Reconditioning:** Changing terms (e.g., lower interest rate).
    3.  **Restructuring:** Combination of both + potentially partial principal write-off (rare for individuals).
    4.  **Grace Period:** Interest-only payments for a few months.
*   **Impact on SLIK:** It will be marked as "Kol 2" or "Restrukturisasi," which might affect future loans but saves you from "Kol 5" (Bad Debt).

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Must be applied BEFORE total default (Galbay) for better chance of approval.
*   **Documents needed:** KTP, Proof of Income decrease (Surat PHK, etc.), Application Letter.
*   **Process:** Submission -> Review -> Negotiation -> New Contract (Adendum).

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Case Study:** A motorcycle rider facing PHK. He applied for restructuring to his leasing company before the 3rd installment was due. He got a 6-month extension and lower monthly payments, avoiding repo of his bike.
*   **Common Reason for Rejection:** No proof of income decrease or history of being uncooperative.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Restrukturisasi itu penghapusan utang."
*   **Reality:** It's just a relief/delay. You still have to pay, but with a lighter rhythm.
*   **Myth:** "Kalau minta restrukturisasi nanti nggak bisa pinjam lagi selamanya."
*   **Reality:** It affects your score temporarily, but bad debt (default) is much worse.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Self-Audit:** Calculate how much you can actually afford per month.
2.  **Contact Creditor:** Call CS or visit the office.
3.  **Prepare Evidence:** Collect Surat PHK, hospital bills, or financial statements.
4.  **Submit Proposal:** Use official channels (Email/App/Physical).
5.  **Monitor:** Follow up every 3 days.
