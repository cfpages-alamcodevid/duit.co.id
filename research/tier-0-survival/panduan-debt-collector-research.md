## 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Allowed Hours:** 08:00 - 20:00 local time (SEOJK 19/2023).
- **Mandatory Documents:**
  1. Identity Card (KTP DC)
  2. Sertifikat Profesi Penagihan (SPPI)
  3. Surat Tugas dari Perusahaan Pinjaman
  4. Bukti dokumen wanprestasi (Surat Peringatan/SP)
- **Trend 2025:** Increased use of 3rd party DC agencies by top fintechs to manage rising NPL (Non-Performing Loans).

## 4. Market Research
- **Cost of Collection:** Usually added to the debt as "biaya penagihan" or "biaya administrasi", but OJK limits the total benefit (interest + fees) to 100% of the principal for most loans.
- **Market Practice:** DCs often get a percentage of the collected amount (incentive-based), which explains their persistence.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Regulation:** POJK No. 10/POJK.05/2022 (Fintech P2P) and POJK No. 35/POJK.05/2018 (Multifinance/Leasing).
- **Ethics:** Dilarang menggunakan kekerasan, ancaman, atau mempermalukan debitur (e.g., shouting so neighbors can hear).
- **Location:** Home visit is allowed, but only to the address registered in the agreement.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Common Issue:** DC comes without SPPI certificate.
- **Action Recommendation:** If they can't show the 4 mandatory documents, you have the right to ask them to leave and return when they have them.
- **Case Study:** A debtor successfully negotiated a "bayar pokok saja" (payment of principal only) when the DC realized the debtor really had no assets, but this usually happens in later stages of delinquency.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
- **Scenario:** DC comes at 9 PM.
- **Action:** Politely point out OJK rules that collection ends at 8 PM. Record the conversation. Refuse to open the door.
- **Scenario:** DC tries to take your motorbike (leasing).
- **Action:** Ask for "Sertifikat Jaminan Fidusia". Without it, they cannot seize the unit on the street/at home. Call the police if they persist.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "DC boleh masuk ke dalam rumah tanpa izin."
- **Reality:** Masuk tanpa izin adalah pelanggaran Pasal 167 KUHP (Memasuki rumah orang lain tanpa izin).
- **Myth:** "Saya harus bayar tunai ke DC di tempat."
- **Reality:** Sangat disarankan untuk membayar HANYA melalui sistem (Virtual Account/App). Bayar tunai ke DC berisiko uang tidak disetorkan.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1. **The "Intercom" Protocol:** Don't open the door immediately. Ask who it is and from which company.
2. **The Document Check:** Request photos/sight of the 4 mandatory documents.
3. **The Recording:** Start recording video/audio for your protection.
4. **The Conversation:** Keep it outside the house. Stay calm.
5. **The Negotiation:** State your financial situation honestly. Don't make promises you can't keep.
6. **The Conclusion:** If they are polite, thank them. If they are aggressive, call for local security/RT/Police.
