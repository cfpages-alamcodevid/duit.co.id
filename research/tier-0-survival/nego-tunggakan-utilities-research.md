## 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **PLN Policy:** Disconnection starts after 1 month of unpaid bill. After 3 months, the meter is usually removed.
*   **PDAM Policy:** Varies by city, but generally 2-3 months of arrears lead to disconnection.
*   **Denda (Late Fees):**
    *   PLN: Based on VA (e.g., 900VA is Rp 3.000, 1300VA is Rp 5.000 per month).
    *   PDAM: Usually a percentage or flat fee per month.
*   **Source:** PLN Official (2025) & Regional PDAM Bylaws.

---

## 4. Market Research: Official Installment Schemes
*   **PLN "Angsuran Tunggakan":** Requires 30-40% Down Payment (DP). Remaining can be split into 3-12 months. Must be done at the local ULP (Unit Layanan Pelanggan), not via app.
*   **PDAM "Keringanan":** Usually given for leaks or extreme financial hardship. Requires a letter to the Director.
*   **Dislokasi/Relokasi:** Understanding the costs if the meter needs to be moved or reinstalled.

---

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Photocopy of KTP (owner), ID Pelanggan, and a signed "Surat Kesanggupan Membayar".
*   **Authority:** PLN (State-owned) and PDAM (Region-owned).
*   **Penalty:** Fines and eventual permanent removal of service.

---

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **The "Meter Smuggling" Myth:** Some people think bypassing the meter is a solution. Expert advice: DON'T. The fines for "P2TL" (electricity theft) can reach tens of millions of rupiah.
*   **Case Study:** A resident had 4 months of arrears (Rp 2M). Instead of waiting for disconnection, they went to the PLN office, paid Rp 600k (DP), and agreed to pay an extra Rp 150k on top of their monthly bill for a year. They kept their light on.
*   **Key Learning:** Early communication prevents the "dark house" scenario.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Scenario: "Tunggakan PLN 3 Bulan (Total Rp 1,2 Juta)"**
*   **Action Plan:**
    1.  Don't wait for the technician to come.
    2.  Visit Kantor PLN ULP.
    3.  Bring KTP and money for DP (Rp 400k).
    4.  Ask for "Skema Angsuran".
    5.  Request "Penyambungan Kembali" if already disconnected.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Bisa nego lewat aplikasi PLN Mobile."
*   **Reality:** No. Negotiations and installment agreements MUST be done in person at the office for verification.
*   **Myth:** "PDAM nggak bakal putus aliran kalau cuma nunggak sebulan."
*   **Reality:** Many PDAMs are becoming stricter; some disconnect after only 45 days.

---

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Check the Exact Amount:** Use PLN Mobile or E-commerce apps to see the total debt + fines.
2.  **Prepare the DP:** Save at least 30% of the total amount.
3.  **Visit the Office:** Go in the morning.
4.  **Speak to Customer Service:** Use the "Financial Hardship" angle (PHK, Medical bill).
5.  **Sign the Contract:** Get a copy of the installment schedule.
6.  **Switch to Pre-paid (Token):** After the debt is cleared, switch to tokens to prevent future arrears.
