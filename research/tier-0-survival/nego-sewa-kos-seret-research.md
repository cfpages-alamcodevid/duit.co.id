# Research: Cara Nego Sewa Kos Saat Seret

## Legal Framework (Indonesia)

### KUH Perdata (Kitab Undang-Undang Hukum Perdata)
- Pasal 1565-1567: Sewa adalah kontrak sewa menyewa untuk jangka waktu tertentu
- **Kewajiban utama**: Bayar tepat waktu sesuai perjanjian
- **Konsekuensi telat bayar**: Denda, evakuasi barang, atau gugatan pengusiran (tergantung perjanjian)

### Hak Penyewa
1. **Hak atas masa tenggang**: Bila perjanjian **tidak** tertulis, hukum mengakui masa tenggang wajar
2. **Hak atas barang pribadi**: Pemilik **tidak bisa** langsung membuang barang penyewa (Pasal 1266 KUH Perdata)
3. **Kewajiban alternatif**: Bila tidak mampu bayar tunai, bisa mengajukan kompensasi lain (bersihkan kamar, reparasi kecil) **jika disepakati bersama**

### Batas Waktu Telat Bayar
- **0-7 hari**: Status normal, biasanya pengingat saja
- **7-14 hari**: Status telat, denda bisa dikenakan (10-20% per bulan)
- **>14 hari**: Risiko tinggi pengusiran kecuali ada negosiasi

## Preparation Before Negotiation

### Step 1: Calculate Minimum Needs
```
Pendapatan bulan ini: Rp X
Kebutuhan makan/transport: Rp Y
Kebutuhan darurat: Rp Z
Sisa untuk kos: Rp S
```

### Step 2: Gather Proof of Limitations
- Slip gaji terakhir
- Surat keterangan perusahaan (cuti tidak dibayar/PHK)
- Rekening bank 3 bulan terakhir
- Bukti tagihan mendesak lain

### Step 3: Make Payment Proposal
**Option A: Installment Payment**
- Pay 50% today, 50% + small penalty in 7 days

**Option B: Deduct from Next Month's Rent**
- Delay this month Rp X
- Next month's rent deduct Rp X
- Pay normally month after

**Option C: Service Barter**
- Delay Rp X
- Clean house complex 2x/week for 1 month (value Rp X)
- Or: Wash clothes/admin tasks for owner

## Negotiation Scripts (Indonesian)

### Scenario 1: Direct Confrontation
> "Pak/Bu [Nama], selamat pagi/siang. Izin melapor, gaji saya bulan ini baru cair tanggal [tanggal] karena [alasan]. Total sewa yang belum saya transfer sekitar Rp [jumlah]. Boleh minta toleransi sampai tanggal [tanggal, maksimal 5 hari]?"

### Scenario 2: Offering Compensation
> "Karena keterlambatan ini, saya bermaksud membayar full bulan depan tanpa telat. Boleh kah kami sepakat bahwa sewa bulan [bulan depan] saya potong Rp [jumlah yang telat]?"

### Scenario 3: Requesting Installment
> "Saya benar-benar nggak sanggup bayar penuh hari ini. Boleh saya angsur dalam 2 kali? Rp [setengah] hari ini, Rp [setengah] besok lusa."

### Scenario 4: Emergency Move-Out
> "Maaf pak/bu, saya terpaksa harus pindah mendadak karena [alasan]. Saya minta maaf sebesar-besarnya. Saya bisa bayar sampai tanggal [tanggal] sebelum kunci disita?"

## Landlord Personality Types & Strategies

### Tipe 1: "Emotional/Olahraga" Landlord
- **Ciri**: Langsung marah, ancam buang barang
- **Strategy**: Don't retaliate emotionally. Offer tangible solution.

### Tipe 2: "Business Calculator" Landlord  
- **Ciri**: Calm, calculate profit/loss
- **Strategy**: Bring clear numbers. Offer long-term contract extension as compensation.

### Tipe 3: "Relaxed/Bookkeeper" Landlord
- **Ciri**: Rarely collect, but strict when they do
- **Strategy**: Remind mutual agreements. Use third party (roommate) as guarantor.

### Tipe 4: "Corporate/Big Kos Manager"
- **Ciri**: Systematic, written contracts, auto-eviction
- **Strategy**: Read contract for "Force Majeure" clause. Submit official letter from HRD. Request "Grace Period".

## When to Seek Legal Help (Red Flags)

### Emergency Indicators
1. **Barang dibuang tanpa peringatan** → Report to Polsek (theft/destruction)
2. **Ancaman pemukulan/intimidasi** → Save chat/voice records. Report to Polsek.
3. **Pemotongan gaji oleh pihak ketiga** → Illegal without written consent.
4. **Denda tidak masuk akal** (Rp 500rb denda for Rp 500rb delay) → Can sue in Pengadilan Negeri.
5. **Kekerasan psikis terus-menerus** → Report to Lurah/Polsek.

### Legal Aid Resources
1. **LBH (Lembaga Bantuan Hukum)** - Free for UMR workers
2. **Posbakum PN (Pos Bantuan Hukum Pengadilan Negeri)** - Free consultation
3. **LPSK (Lembaga Penyelesaian Sengketa Konsumen)** - Mediation without court
4. **Lurah/RT/RW** - Free initial mediation
5. **Polisi (110)** - Only for criminal cases

### Required Documents
- KTP photocopy
- Rental agreement (if any)
- Transfer/chat payment proofs
- Proof of delay (bank notification)
- Company letter (if work-related)
- Photos of threatened items

## Prevention Strategies

### Before Renting Kos
1. **Choose weekly/monthly flexible payment**
2. **Always request simple written agreement**
3. **Create separate kos bank account**
4. **Maintain good communication with owner**
5. **Know neighbors & complex rules**

### Emergency Protocol (Days 1-14)
**Days 1-3**: Calculate remaining money, prioritize: Food > Kos > Debt
**Days 4-7**: Take most realistic option (pay partial, sell items, borrow from close friends)
**Days 8-14**: Seek BPR/Koperasi loan, consider moving to cheaper kos

## Key Takeaways
1. **Honesty > Avoidance**: Owners angrier at disappearance than early notice
2. **Bring solutions, not just problems**: Offer deductions, installments, or services
3. **Systematic prevention**: Allocate kos money at month START, not end
4. **Know your legal rights**: KUH Perdata protects tenants from arbitrary eviction
