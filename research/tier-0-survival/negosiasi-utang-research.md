### 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Interest Rate Caps (2026):** Consumer loans capped at **0.1% per day**; Productive loans at **0.067% per day**. (Source: SEOJK 19/2023 & POJK 2024 updates).
- **Lock Cap Rule:** Total interest + fees + penalties **cannot exceed 100%** of the principal. (Source: AFPI/OJK).
- **Collection Hours:** Strictly **08.00 - 20.00** local time. (Source: POJK 22/2023).
- **SLIK Impact:** 90+ days delinquency results in "Kredit Macet" status, blocking future bank loans for years.

---

### 4. Market Research (Negotiation Landscape)
**Restructuring Options in 2026:**
- **Rescheduling:** Extending tenor (e.g., from 3 months to 12 months).
- **Reconditioning:** Removing late fees (denda) or lowering interest to focus on principal.
- **Haircut:** Lump-sum settlement of principal only (rare, usually for catastrophic cases).

**Creditor Behavior:**
- Creditors prefer *some* payment over *zero* payment.
- Legal P2P platforms are monitored by OJK for their NPL (Non-Performing Loan) ratios; they are incentivized to help you pay back.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Utang otomatis lunas setelah 90 hari."
- **Reality:** Only the *field collection* usually stops; the debt remains in SLIK OJK forever until paid.
- **Myth:** "Jasa Hapus Data Pinjol bisa bantu."
- **Reality:** 100% scam. Only the platform can update SLIK.
- **Myth:** "Polisi bisa menangkap karena tidak bayar utang."
- **Reality:** Debt is a civil (perdata) matter, not criminal, *unless* you used fake documents.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
**Step 1: Inventory & Verification**
- Check OJK status. List Pokok, Bunga, Denda.
**Step 2: Prepare the "Poverty Proof"**
- Collect PHK letters, medical records, or 3-month bank statements showing negative cashflow.
**Step 3: Draft the Proposal**
- Use the 3-Option Strategy: (A) Tenor extension, (B) Denda waiver, (C) Lump-sum discount.
**Step 4: Official Channel Communication**
- Use Email/CS ticket, not WhatsApp to a random DC.
**Step 5: Lock the Agreement**
- **Crucial:** Get a written Addendum or email confirmation before paying a single Rupiah.
