## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Understanding consumer protection rights under UU Perlindungan Konsumen.  
**Regulation:** UU No. 8 Tahun 1999 tentang Perlindungan Konsumen.  
**Process:** Victims can file complaints to BPOM or local consumer protection agencies.  
**Cost:** Free for initial consultation.  
**Timeline:** 30-60 days for resolution.  
**Authority:** Badan Perlindungan Konsumen Nasional (BPKN).  
**Link:** https://bpkn.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Reporting illegal debt collectors to authorities.  
**Regulation:** UU ITE No. 11 Tahun 2008 and OJK regulations on financial harassment.  
**Process:** Report via OJK whistleblower system or police.  
**Cost:** Free.  
**Timeline:** Immediate investigation, 7-30 days for initial response.  
**Authority:** Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK) and Kepolisian RI.  
**Link:** https://www.ojk.go.id/

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** Utang pinjol tidak berbahaya jika bayar tepat waktu.  
**Reality:** Bunga tinggi dan risiko penagihan tetap memicu stres kronis.  
**Why it matters:** Membuat orang underestimate risiko mental health.  
**Source:** LRPI research 2024.  

**Myth:** Orang kuat mental tidak terpengaruh utang.  
**Reality:** Siapapun bisa mengalami depresi dari tekanan finansial.  
**Why it matters:** Menstigmatisasi korban, mencegah mereka cari bantuan.  
**Source:** WHO mental health reports.  

**Myth:** Pinjol legal aman dari segi mental health.  
**Reality:** Bahkan pinjol legal bisa menyebabkan gangguan jika tidak dikelola.  
**Why it matters:** Literasi penting untuk semua jenis pinjaman.  
**Source:** BPS Susenas 2022.

This research reveals that pinjol debt severely impacts Indonesian mental health, with 59.7% of users experiencing disorders vs 30% for other loans.
