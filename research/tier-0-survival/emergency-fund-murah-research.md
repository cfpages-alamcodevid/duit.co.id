### 6. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Dana darurat harus jutaan baru berguna."
- **Reality:** Rp 100,000 is useful when your tire punctures at night.
- **Myth:** "Emas adalah dana darurat terbaik."
- **Reality:** Emas has a spread (selisih jual-beli). RDPU is better for short-term liquidity.
- **Myth:** "Simpan di bawah kasur lebih aman."
- **Reality:** Risk of theft, fire, and losing value to inflation (2.5% per year).
