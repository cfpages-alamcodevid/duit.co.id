### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "Ganti nomor HP otomatis bikin utang lunas."
- **Reality:** Debt remains in SLIK. Creditors may eventually find your new info or visit your home.
- **Myth:** "Utang pinjol lunas setelah 90 hari."
- **Reality:** Only the *active field collection* by the platform usually stops; debt is often sold to 3rd party collectors or stays in SLIK permanently.
- **Myth:** "Ada jasa joki pinjol yang bisa bantu hapus data."
- **Reality:** 100% Scam. They often steal your data to borrow more in your name.
