## 4. Market Research: Survival Protein Sources
1.  **Tempe/Tahu:** Best cost-to-protein ratio.
2.  **Telur (Eggs):** Most versatile and high quality.
3.  **Ikan Pindang/Cue:** Cheap omega-3 (Rp 5.000/basket).
4.  **Hati Ampela:** Cheap animal protein (Rp 2.000 - Rp 3.000 per set).
5.  **Kacang Hijau:** Good for snacks/fiber and protein.

## 5. Shopping Strategy (The "Duit.co.id" Way)
*   **Timing:** 5:00 AM - 6:00 AM at "Pasar Tradisional".
*   **Location:** "Pasar Kaget" or local "Tukang Sayur Keliling" (negotiate for bulk).
*   **Method:** **Bulk Buying vs Daily Eceran.** Buy rice by 5kg, eggs by 1/2kg, and tempe by the board. Never buy "per portion" at a Warteg if you are in Tier 0.
*   **The "Malam Hari" Hack:** Visit supermarkets (Superindo/Hypermart) at 9:00 PM for 50-70% discount on "Sayur Siap Saji" or wilting vegetables that are still edible.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Nutritionist Insight:** Cheap doesn't mean "only noodles". "Muntaber" (Malnutrisi) will cost more in medical bills than the money saved on food. Always include 1 fiber source and 1 protein source.
*   **Case Study:** A student survived 3 months on Rp 300k/month for food. He used "Food Prep" (cooking once for 3 days) to save on gas and prevent food waste.
*   **Key Learning:** "Bumbu Dasar" (Onion, Garlic, Chili) is the secret to making cheap food taste expensive.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Mie Instan adalah makanan termurah."
*   **Reality:** In terms of "kenyang" (fullness) and nutrition, rice + tempe is actually cheaper and healthier per serving. Mie instan makes you hungry faster.
*   **Myth:** "Makan sehat harus mahal."
*   **Reality:** Traditional Indonesian food (Gado-gado, Pecel, Sayur Asem) is the ultimate healthy-cheap food.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Inventory Your Pantry:** Check for leftover salt, oil, or sugar.
2.  **The "Pasar Subuh" Run:** Wake up early to get the best prices.
3.  **Food Prep:** Clean and cut all vegetables; marinate tempe immediately.
4.  **Cook Your Own Rice:** Rule #1 of survival.
5.  **Avoid Liquid Calories:** Drink only tap water (boiled) or plain tea. No boba, no sodas.
