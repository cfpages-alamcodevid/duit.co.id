## 2. Competitor Analysis

## 3. Key Data & Statistics
- **Trend:** Fraud via APK files (kedok undangan/resi) is the #1 way OTP is stolen in 2024-2025.
- **Fact:** OJK noted a significant rise in "Identitas Catut" (Identity theft) where victims only find out when they are rejected for a real bank loan.
- **Source:** Kominfo & OJK (2025 Reports).

## 4. Market Research (Attack Modus)
- **Modus 1: APK Phishing.** Sending a file like "Undangan_Nikah.apk". Once installed, it reads all SMS (including OTP) and sends them to the hacker.
- **Modus 2: Social Engineering.** Hacker calls pretending to be from Shopee/Gojek: "You won a prize, I just sent a 6-digit code, please read it back to me."
- **Modus 3: KTP Reuse.** Fraudsters take a KTP photo from a "Lowongan Kerja Palsu" and use it to register for Pinjol.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
- **Requirement:** UU Pelindungan Data Pribadi (UU PDP) is now fully enforceable (2025). Companies that leak data are liable.
- **Regulation:** UU ITE and UU PDP.
- **Authority:** Kominfo and OJK.

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
- **Expert Tip:** "Never share your KTP photo without a watermark. If a service refuses a watermarked KTP, it might be a scam or have poor security."
- **Case Study:** A victim in Jakarta found they had Rp 50 million in debt from 10 different Pinjols, despite never applying. The source? A photo of their KTP they sent for a "Data Entry" job on Facebook.

## 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**How to Watermark KTP (Kominfo Style):**
1. Open Photo Editor.
2. Add Text: "UNTUK VERIFIKASI [NAMA LAYANAN] TANGGAL [TANGGAL]".
3. Set opacity to 30-50%.
4. Place it on a blank area of the KTP (not covering face or ID number, but crossing the background).

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
- **Myth:** "If I delete the message with the OTP, it's safe."
- **Reality:** If you have a malicious APK installed, the hacker reads the SMS the moment it arrives. Deleting it does nothing.
- **Myth:** "Watermarking KTP makes it invalid for official use."
- **Reality:** Most official apps (Fintech/Banks) accept watermarks as long as the text is readable.

## 9. Action Steps Outline
- **Step 1: Check your "SLIK OJK" (iDebku).** Do this once every 6 months to ensure no "ghost loans" in your name.
- **Step 2: Two-Step Verification (2FA).** Enable this on WhatsApp, Gmail, and Instagram.
- **Step 3: Permission Audit.** Check your phone settings: "Which apps can read my SMS?"
- **Step 4: The "3-Second Rule."** Before clicking any link or sharing a code, wait 3 seconds and ask: "Did I request this?"
- **Step 5: Google Dark Web Report.** Check if your email/data is leaked.
