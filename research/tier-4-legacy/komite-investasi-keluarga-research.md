## 5. Legal &amp; Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Jika komite manage aset >Rp 100M untuk pihak ketiga, butuh lisensi Manajer Investasi OJK. Internal keluarga: Bebas asal tidak public offer
Regulation: UU No.8/1995 Pasar Modal, POJK No.77/POJK.04/2016 tentang PM
Process: Buat charter internal, appoint members, document decisions. No registration jika private
Cost: Rp 200-500 juta (legal drafting)
Timeline: 1-3 bulan setup
Authority: OJK (supervisi jika applicable), Notaris untuk charter jika formalized
Link: https://www.ojk.go.id/id/kanal/pasar-modal/regulasi/Pages/Legal-Docs.aspx

Requirement: Independent members harus disclosed conflict of interest
Regulation: Good Corporate Governance OJK untuk entitas terkait
Process: Annual disclosure form
Cost: Minimal
Timeline: Ongoing
Authority: Internal + OJK jika holding public
Link: POJK No. 21/2020 GCG
