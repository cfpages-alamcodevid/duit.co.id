### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Regulation: Common Reporting Standard (CRS) - UU Pajak Penghasilan Year 2000 amendments 2025
Process: Register financial institutions with Ditjen Pajak, file Form 1784-T annually
Cost: Rp 1-5 million for registration, plus advisor fees
Timeline: 6 months for full setup

Regulation: Economic Substance Requirements (ESR) for low-tax jurisdictions
Process: Annual filings proving actual business activity
Cost: Rp 2-10 million per jurisdiction

Regulation: CbC Reporting for global enterprises
Process: File OECD template if revenue >EUR 750 million
Authority: Ditjen Pajak + OECD
Timeline: Annual, due 12 months after tax year end

Requirement: Beneficial Ownership Register (UBO)
Regulation: UU Anti-Pencucian Uang amendments 2025
Process: Register in public registry if ownership >10%
Cost: Free but verification Rp 500K-1 million
Penalty for non-compliance: Rp 5 billion fine
