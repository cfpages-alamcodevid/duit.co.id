## Research Summary

This research covers comprehensive estate planning for Indonesian Muslims using wasiat (will), hibah (gift), and asuransi jiwa (life insurance, preferably takaful). Key findings show growing demand (AAJI reports Rp 15T insurance market), high risk of unplanned estates (Deloitte 72% UHNW gap), and Islamic tool superiority (IRTI 60% adoption).

## Key Insights

1. Hibah unlimited transfer vs wasiat 13 limit is critical differentiator
2. TakafulRBq compliance essential for Muslim clients
3. TrustHPT under UU 2020 available for complex asset protection
4. Early planning saves 15-30% in taxes and legal fees (EY data)
