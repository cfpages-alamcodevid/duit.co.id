### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: NIB as primary business license  
Regulation: UU 11/2020 Cipta Kerja, PP 5/2021, PP 28/2025  
Process: Register online via oss.go.id, input NIK/NPWP, select KBLI, submit  
Cost: Rp0 (free) for NIB issuance  
Timeline: 1-3 days for low-risk, instant for micro  
Authority: BKPM/Kemeninvest  
Link: https://oss.go.id  

Additional for high-risk: Izin teknis + sertifikat standar  
Regulation: PP 28/2025 Risk-Based Approach  
Process: OSS auto-assigns based on KBLI risk level  
Cost: Varies by permit, e.g. Rp500k for environmental docs  
Timeline: 7-14 days verification  
Authority: Relevant ministries (health, environment)

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: SIUP and TDP still required for trading businesses  
Reality: NIB replaces both since 2021, no renewal needed  
Why it matters: Avoids wasted time/money on obsolete permits  
Source: PP 5/2021  

Myth: High-risk businesses can't get licenses easily  
Reality: OSS risk-based system streamlines process  
Impact: Encourages formalization  
Source: BKPM data 2026
