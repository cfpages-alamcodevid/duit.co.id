### 9. Action Steps Outline
Provide structured action steps the writer will expand:
```
Step 1: Define Project Scope & Objectives
- Details needed: Business model choice (own brand vs franchise), location type (kiosk/mall/store)
- Example: "Budi ingin buka cabang gelato ice cream di mall Surabaya"
- Warning: Avoid overreach - start with 1 branch, not 3 simultaneously

Step 2: Market Research & Demand Analysis
- Details needed: Demographic data (population, income levels), competitor mapping, psychographic insights
- Example: Use Google Maps + BPS data for density, Shopee/Lazada for pricing benchmarks
- Warning: Don't rely on "feeling" - quantitative data beats assumption

Step 3: Operational Feasibility Assessment
- Details needed: Facility requirements, HR planning, supply chain logistics, daily operations timeline
- Example: Space 50m2, 3 employees shift, local supplier for fresh ingredients
- Warning: Include seasonality - dry season peak for laundry/food services

Step 4: Financial Projections & Budget Planning
- Details needed: Revenue forecasts (conservative/moderate/optimistic), cost breakdown, break-even analysis
- Example: Month 1 Rp 50jt revenue vs Rp 30jt costs = Rp 20jt loss, build to positive
- Warning: Account for delays - construction permits often take 4-6 weeks

Step 5: Risk Assessment & Mitigation Strategy
- Details needed: SWOT analysis, sensitivity testing, contingency plans for key risks
- Example: Risk "Lokasi banjir" - mitigation "Raised flooring + insurance"
- Warning: Don't ignore operational risks like employee turnover or supply disruption

Step 6: Implementation Timeline & Go-Live Plan
- Details needed: Phase-by-phase execution (prep/setup/opening), milestone tracking, launch marketing
- Example: Week 1-2: permits & design, Week 3-4: renovation, Week 5: staff hiring & training
- Warning: Buffer 2 weeks extra time - construction often overruns

Step 7: Monitoring & Evaluation Framework
- Details needed: KPIs (revenue, customer count, profit margin), review schedule (monthly first 6 months)
- Example: Dashboard tracking weekly sales, customer feedback aggregation
- Warning: Set realistic targets - first month typically 30-50% of full capacity
```
