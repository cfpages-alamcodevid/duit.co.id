### 9. Action Steps Outline
Provide structured action steps the writer will expand:
```
Step 1: Assess Business Readiness
- Evaluate current digital presence (website, Google My Business)
- Define target audience and location radius
- Set realistic budget and goals
- Example: Food business targeting 5km radius, Rp 1 million/month budget

Step 2: Set Up Google Ads Account
- Create account at ads.google.com
- Link Google Analytics and Google My Business
- Choose campaign type (Search, Display, Video)
- Warning: Avoid broad keywords initially

Step 3: Keyword Research and Targeting
- Use Google Keyword Planner
- Focus on long-tail, local keywords
- Set location targeting to specific areas
- Add negative keywords to save budget

Step 4: Create Compelling Ads
- Write clear headlines and descriptions
- Include call-to-action
- Add extensions (location, call, sitelink)
- Example: "Pesan Nasi Padang Delivery - Gratis Ongkir Jakarta Pusat"

Step 5: Set Budget and Bidding Strategy
- Start with daily budget Rp 50,000-200,000
- Use manual CPC or maximize clicks initially
- Monitor cost per acquisition

Step 6: Launch and Monitor Performance
- Run A/B tests on ad copy
- Track conversions, CTR, CPC
- Adjust based on data weekly
- Scale successful campaigns

Step 7: Advanced Optimization
- Implement remarketing campaigns
- Use audience insights for better targeting
- Consider video ads on YouTube
- Warning: Don't scale too fast without data
```
