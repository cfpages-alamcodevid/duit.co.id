## Legal & Regulatory Requirements

Family businesses are often unincorporated, but for saleable assets or employment, they may need formalization.

Requirement: If the business employes family members formally, comply with Labor Law (UU No. 13 Tahun 2003)  
Regulation: UU Ketenagakerjaan No. 13/2003  
Process: Register with BPJS Ketenagakerjaan, provide fair compensation  
Cost: BPJS contributions (4.24% of salary)  
Timeline: Immediate upon hiring  
Authority: Ministry of Manpower (Kemnaker RI)  
Link: https://www.kemnaker.go.id/

Requirement: For separation of roles and cash controls in PT-form businesses  
Regulation: KUH Perdata and UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 about Limited Liability Companies  
Process: Appoint directors and commissioners as required, implement approval hierarchies  
Cost: Notarization fees Rp 500,000-1,000,000  
Timeline: During company establishment or annual report  
Authority: Ministry of Law and Human Rights (Kemenkumham)  
Link: https://www.kemenkumham.go.id/

Requirement: Tax reporting for family employees  
Regulation: UU Pajak Penghasilan (UU 7/1983) and amendments  
Process: Issue withholding tax for salaries, no family favoritism in deductions  
Cost: Accounting service, Rp 2-5 million annually  
Timeline: Quarterly reporting  
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes (DGT)  
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

## Action Steps Outline

Step 1: Assess Current Controls  
- Evaluate existing processes for cash, approvals, roles  
- Tool: Self-assessment checklist  
- Warning: Be honest, involve non-family advisor  

Step 2: Define Roles and Boundaries  
- Create job descriptions for all, including family  
- Example: Separate family councils from operational meetings  

Step 3: Implement Cash Controls  
- Use POS systems, daily reconciliations  
- Details: Segregate handling from recording  

Step 4: Establish Approval Hierarchies  
- Define thresholds (e.g., Rp 5 million requires director approval)  
- Flaw to avoid: Family members bypassing processes  

Step 5: Monitor and Review  
- Quarterly audits, family feedback sessions
