## 9. Action Steps Outline

**Step 1:** Gather all financial data (bank statements, invoices, receipts)
- Details needed: Last month's transactions, outstanding receivables/payables
- Example: Use accounting software to auto-import bank data
- Warning: Don't rely on memory - use digital records

**Step 2:** Review income statement (revenue vs expenses)
- Details needed: Compare to budget, identify variances
- Example: Calculate gross margin, analyze top expense categories
- Warning: Don't ignore small variances - they compound

**Step 3:** Analyze cash flow (inflows vs outflows)
- Details needed: Operating, investing, financing cash flows
- Example: Track working capital changes
- Warning: Negative cash flow can kill business quickly

**Step 4:** Check key ratios and KPIs
- Details needed: Current ratio, profit margin, ROI on marketing
- Example: Set benchmarks from industry averages
- Warning: Don't compare to irrelevant businesses

**Step 5:** Prepare action plan and forecast
- Details needed: Next month's budget, corrective actions
- Example: Adjust spending based on review insights
- Warning: Implement changes immediately, don't delay
