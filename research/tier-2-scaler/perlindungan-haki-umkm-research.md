## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Merek registration for brand protection
Regulation: UU No 20/2016 tentang Merek dan Indikasi Geografis
Process: Online application via DJKI, document submission, examination (30-90 days)
Cost: Rp 500k-1M for UMKM (2026 rates)
Timeline: 30-90 days for approval
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual (DJKI)
Link: https://dgip.go.id

Requirement: Hak Cipta for original creative works
Regulation: UU No 28/2014 tentang Hak Cipta
Process: Online registration, automatic protection upon creation but certificate recommended
Cost: Rp 200k-400k
Timeline: 14 days for certificate
Authority: DJKI
Link: https://dgip.go.id

Requirement: Desain Industri for product appearance
Regulation: UU No 31/2000 tentang Desain Industri
Process: Application, examination, publication
Cost: Rp 1M-2M
Timeline: 60-90 days
Authority: DJKI
Link: https://dgip.go.id

Requirement: Paten for inventions
Regulation: UU No 13/2016 tentang Paten
Process: Application, substantive examination, grant
Cost: Rp 2M-4M (UMKM subsidized)
Timeline: 24-36 months

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
```
Case Study: Geprek Bensu - First to File Principle
Background: Benny Sujono vs Ruben Onsu in ayam geprek franchise dispute
Starting point: Benny registered "Bensu" merek in 2017, Ruben used similar name later
Result: Mahkamah Agung ruled for Benny, Ruben must change branding
Timeline: 2017-2022 legal battle
Key learning: Register HKI immediately upon business launch, don't wait for conflicts
Source: Putusan MA No. 57 PK/Pdt.Sus-HKI/2022

Case Study: MS Glow vs PS Glow - Brand Similarity Issues  
Background: Local beauty product companies in trademark dispute
Starting point: MS Glow registered first, PS Glow used similar name
Result: MS Glow won Rp 37.9M compensation, PS Glow must stop production
Timeline: 2022-2023 court process
Key learning: Even one letter difference can cause confusion if overall impression similar
Source: Putusan PN Surabaya 2023

Case Study: HAKUBAKU vs PT Tona Morawa - Bad Faith Registration
Background: Japanese company vs Indonesian firm over noodle brand
Starting point: HAKUBAKU had global rights, local company registered similar mark
Result: Indonesian registration cancelled, bad faith proven
Timeline: 2018-2026 legal process
Key learning: Cannot register marks known to belong to others with intent to free-ride
Source: Putusan PN Niaga Jakarta Pusat 2026

Expert Opinion: Ahmad Ridha Sabana (Presidential Envoy for UMKM)
Quote: "80% of HKI in Indonesia registered by foreign entities - we need local UMKM to protect domestic innovations"
Relevance: Urgency for UMKM to register before foreign companies claim local innovations
Source: Kompas.com 2025

Expert Opinion: Hermansyah Siregar (DJKI Director General)

Source: RRI.co.id 2026

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: HKI hanya untuk perusahaan besar
Reality: 11% UMKM sudah daftar, dengan tarif khusus Rp 500k
Why it matters: UMKM kecil justru lebih rentan tiruan tanpa HKI
Source: DJKI data 2026

Myth: Hak cipta otomatis tanpa daftar
Reality: Hak cipta timbul otomatis, tapi sertifikat dibutuhkan untuk bukti hukum
Why it matters: Tanpa sertifikat, sulit buktikan kepemilikan di pengadilan
Source: UU 28/2014

Myth: HKI mahal dan rumit untuk UMKM
Reality: Permenkum 5/2026 sederhanakan syarat, proses 30 hari
Why it matters: Biaya rendah tapi manfaat jangka panjang besar
Source: DJKI announcement 2026

Myth: Popularitas cukup lindungi brand
Reality: First to file principle - siapa daftar duluan yang dapat hak
Why it matters: Banyak UMKM kehilangan brand karena daftar terlambat
Source: Kasus Geprek Bensu 2022

Myth: Rahasia dagang tidak perlu HKI
Reality: Perlu NDA dan sistem internal protection, tapi bisa jadi HKI terkuat
Why it matters: Resep atau formula bisa dilindungi tanpa daftar publik
Source: UU 30/2000
```
