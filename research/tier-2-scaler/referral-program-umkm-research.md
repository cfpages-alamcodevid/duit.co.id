### 9. Action Steps Outline

- Details: Set clear objectives (e.g., 50 new customers/month), choose rewards
- Example: Diskon 15% untuk kedua belah pihak
- Warning: Ensure rewards affordable and sustainable

Step 2: Set up tracking system
- Details: Use apps like ReferralCandy or simple spreadsheets
- Example: WhatsApp bot for referral codes
- Warning: Track fraud (fake referrals)

Step 3: Promote to existing customers
- Details: Email, social media, in-store announcements
- Example: "Ajak teman dapat diskon 20%"

- Details: Track conversions, adjust incentives
- Example: If cashback works better than discounts, switch
- Warning: Regularly review ROI
