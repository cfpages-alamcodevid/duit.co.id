## 4. Market Research (For Business Idea Articles)
**Capital Requirements for Implementing Dual Source:**
Minimum capital to start dual sourcing: Rp 5-10 juta
Breakdown:
- Sample procurement from new vendor: Rp 2-3 juta (10-20% inventory normal)
- Transport & testing: Rp 1 juta
- Legal contract simple: Rp 500rb (notaris optional)
- Buffer stock transition: Rp 2 juta
Total: Rp 5.5 juta

Where to buy/find vendors:
- Platform: Tokopedia Bisnis, Alibaba (lokal agent), Indotrading.com
- Local: Pasar induk, kadin lokal
- Price comparison: Shopee B2B vs supplier langsung 10-15% lebih murah

**Revenue Potential:**
Average saving: 5-15% biaya bahan (negosiasi dual)
Profit margin increase: +5-10%
Monthly projection:
- Conservative: Hemat Rp 2 juta/bln (small disruption avoid)

- Optimistic: Rp 10 juta (full resilience)
Time to break-even: 1-2 bulan

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Dual source terlalu rumit untuk UMKM kecil
Reality: Mulai dengan 1 backup utama, test kecil Rp1-2jt
Why it matters: Hindari kerugian Rp10jt+ dari 1 delay
Source: Jurnal UMKM resilience

Myth: Biaya switching tinggi
Reality: 5-10% inventory awal, ROI cepat dari stability
Source: Case studies scholar

## 9. Action Steps Outline

- Details: Score current: Price(25%), Quality(30%), Delivery(25%), Service(20%)
