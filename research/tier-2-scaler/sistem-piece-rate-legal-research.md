### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: Piece rate boleh bayar sesuka hati, no minimum
Reality: Rata bulanan wajib >= UMK/UMP
Why it matters: Dispute PHI, denda besar
Source: UU 13/2003 Pasal 88(3)
```
```
Myth: Reject tidak dibayar = eksploitasi
Reality: Boleh jika SOP QC fair & training diberikan
Why it matters: Motivasi kualitas tinggi
Source: Putusan PHI kasus konveksi
```
