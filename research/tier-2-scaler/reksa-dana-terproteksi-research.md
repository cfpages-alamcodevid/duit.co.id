### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For investment in protected mutual funds, regulated under OJK.

Requirement: Must be registered at OJK, with published prospectus
Regulation: Peraturan OJK No. 23/POJK.04/2016 tentang Reksa Dana
Process: Register account via licensed application providers (e.g., stock brokers), complete KYC (euryurcher, NPWP), buy online
Cost: Generally no additional cost beyond fees; administrative fees Rp50,000 for new accounts
Timeline: Instant online, approval 1-3 days
Authority: Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK), https://portal.ojk.go.id/
Link: https://portal.ojk.go.id/pages/1405/reksa-dana

Requirement: Investor must be Indonesian citizen or permanent resident for domestic funds; foreigner rules differ
Regulation: UU Pasar Modal No. 8 Tahun 1995
Process: Provide identification, tax number, source of funds
Cost: None additional
Timeline: 1 day for domestic
Authority: OJK

Penalties: Misrepresentation of fund details: Revocation of license, fines up to Rp500 million

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Protected funds are 100% safe with zero risk
Reality: Protection caps at 90-100%, fees apply, market-dependent returns
Why it matters: Avoids unrealistic expectations and underestimating fees
Source: OJK investor education materials 2025

Myth: Returns always beat inflation (current 3.2% per BI 2025)
Reality: Average 6.2%; inflation protection not guaranteed
Why it matters: Educates on real vs nominal returns
Source: BSE Statistics 2025
