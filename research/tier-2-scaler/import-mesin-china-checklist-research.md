## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Angka Pengenal Importir (API) Umum/Penerima
Regulation: PMK 199/PMK.04/2019 (OSS integration)
Process: Daftar OSS.go.id dengan NIB, NPWP, SIUP/TDP
Cost: Gratis
Timeline: 1-3 hari
Authority: Kemendag
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Nomor Identitas Kepabeanan (NIK)
Regulation: PMK 191/2022
Process: Auto via INSW.beacukai.go.id post-API
Cost: Gratis
Timeline: Instan
Link: https://insw.beacukai.go.id/

Requirement: Pendaftaran PEBCAK (Pendaftaran Elektronik BC)
Regulation: PER-25/BC/2022
Process: CEISA.beacukai.go.id
Cost: Gratis

Requirement: Dokumen Impor: Invoice, PL, B/L, CO (ACFTA Form E for 0% BM)
Regulation: UU Pabean 17/2006
HS Code: Ch.84 (mesin) 5-7.5% BM rata2
Cost: BM+PPN+PPh ~20-25% nilai CIF
Timeline: 1-3 hari clearance
Authority: DJBC
Link: https://www.beacukai.go.id/panduan/impor-untuk-dipakai.html

Requirement: SNI compliance jika mandatory (mesin food contact)
Regulation: Permenperin 28/2017
Process: Test lab post-import or supplier cert
Penalty: Rp 2M/unit jika violate

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Validasi Kebutuhan Mesin
- Hitung ROI: current cost vs mesin (labor saving)
- Example: Packing manual Rp2k/unit -> mesin Rp500
- Warning: Overspec (beli pabrik besar untuk UMKM)

Step 2: Legal Prep (1 week)
- OSS NIB/API/NIK
- Example screenshots OSS
- Warning: No API = stuck

Step 3: Sourcing & Vet (2 weeks)
- Alibaba search HS match
- Video call supplier, sample request
- Warning: Fake supplier

Step 4: Order & Shipping
- Pay 30% DP, LC/escrow
- Choose forwarder LCL
- Warning: Insurance full

Step 5: Customs Clearance
- Upload PEB via broker
- Bayar BM/PPN
- Warning: Accurate invoice

Step 6: Post-Import
- Voltage adapter, SNI check
- Training operator
- Spareparts stock 6bln
