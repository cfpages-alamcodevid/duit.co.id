## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business/legal articles, research required licenses/permits.

Requirement: Business license (NIB) for training services
Regulation: UU No. 11 Tahun 2020 tentang Cipta Kerja
Process: Register online via OSS (Online Single Submission)
Cost: Free for basic NIB
Timeline: 1-3 days
Authority: Kemenko Perekonomian
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Tax registration (NPWP) if revenue exceeds threshold
Regulation: UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 tentang Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan
Process: Register at KPP or online
Cost: Free
Timeline: 1-7 days
Authority: DJP (Direktorat Jenderal Pajak)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Labor compliance for employee training
Regulation: UU No. 13 Tahun 2003 tentang Ketenagakerjaan
Process: Follow company training policies, document training records
Cost: None specific
Timeline: Ongoing
Authority: Kemenaker (Ministry of Manpower)
Link: https://kemnaker.go.id/

Requirement: Certification standards if issuing certificates
Regulation: SNI for training services
Process: Optional accreditation through BNSP
Cost: Rp 5-10 juta for accreditation
Timeline: 3-6 months
Authority: BNSP (Badan Nasional Sertifikasi Profesi)
Link: https://bnsp.go.id/
