```

Labor (2 operator):
- Gaji: 2 x Rp 5jt = Rp 10jt/bln
- Output: 200 pcs/hari x 22 = 4400 pcs/bln
- Cost/unit: Rp 2270

Machine (1 unit + 1 operator semi-skilled):
- Dep (5 thn/10k jam): Rp 2k/jam
- Listrik: Rp 1k/jam
- Maint: Rp 500/jam
- Operator: Rp 4jt/bln (partial)
- Total cost/jam: Rp 10k
- Output: 600 pcs/jam x 6 jam = 3600 pcs/hari? Wait adjust.
Assume 8 jam shift, output 5000 pcs/bln
Cost/unit: Rp 500

Break-even volume: 2000 pcs/bln (labor = machine cost)
Profit: Rp 1.5jt/bln saving x12 = Rp 18jt/tahun
```

Sensitivity: +10% wage = machine win sooner.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: Mesin selalu lebih murah dari hari 1
Reality: Hanya untung di volume &gt; break-even (1000-3000 unit/bln)
Why it matters: Beli mesin low volume = rugi cashflow
Source: BPS data low utilization UMKM
```

```
Myth: Operator bisa diganti 100% mesin
Reality: Hybrid model terbaik (1 operator control 3 mesin)
Why it matters: Downtime machine butuh human
Source: Case studies importer mesin
```
