### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Business verification for TikTok Ads account
Regulation: TikTok's advertising policies and Indonesian business registration
Process: Register business account with NPWP/company docs, verify via TikTok dashboard
Cost: Free (verification), but may need legal consultation Rp 500k
Timeline: 1-7 days
Authority: TikTok Indonesia + Kemenkominfo
Link: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/id/help/article/business-account-verification

Requirement: Data privacy compliance for user tracking
Regulation: UU PDP (Undang-Undang Perlindungan Data Pribadi) No. 27 Tahun 2022
Process: Implement consent mechanisms, secure data storage, privacy policy disclosure
Cost: Rp 1-2 juta for legal review/implementation
Timeline: Immediate for new campaigns
Authority: Kemenkominfo
Link: https://pdp.go.id/

Requirement: Advertising content standards
Regulation: Pedoman Periklanan Indonesia (PPI) by KPID
Process: Ensure ads are not misleading, comply with decency standards
Cost: Free (self-compliance), fines up to Rp 500 juta for violations
Timeline: Ongoing
Authority: KPID (Komisi Penyiaran Indonesia)
Link: https://www.kpi.go.id/
