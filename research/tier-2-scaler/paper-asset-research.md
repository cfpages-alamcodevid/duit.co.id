```
Minimum capital to start investing in paper assets:
- Stocks: Rp 100,000 (minimum transaction via online broker)
- Bonds: Rp 1,000,000 (minimum lot size for retail bonds)
- Mutual Funds: Rp 100,000 (minimum subscription)

Breakdown:
- Brokerage account opening: Rp 0-50,000 (some brokers free)
- Transaction fees: 0.15-0.35% for stocks, 0.1-0.2% for mutual funds
- Custody fees: Rp 15,000-25,000/year
```

**Revenue Potential:**
```
Average annual returns (historical, not guaranteed):

- Conservative (bonds/money market): Rp 40,000-50,000/month

Time to break-even: Immediate (but focus on long-term growth, not quick profits)
