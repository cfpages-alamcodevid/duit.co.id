### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Outsourcing is always cheaper than permanent employees
Reality: Depends on scale and vendor quality; small businesses may see minimal savings initially
Why it matters: Poor vendor choice can lead to hidden costs
Source: Industry analysis from 2025–2026

Myth: Outsourcing employees have fewer rights
Reality: Must follow same minimum standards (BPJS, THR)
Why it matters: Ensures fair treatment
Source: UU Cipta Kerja 2023
