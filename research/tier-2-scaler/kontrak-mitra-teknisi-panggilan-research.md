## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: Mitra teknisi = karyawan gratis, no kontrak formal
Reality: Disnaker bisa anggap PKWT jika subordinate control (jam kerja tetap, tools disediakan)
Why it matters: PHK cost + backpay BPJS
Source: Permenaker 7/2026

Myth: No need insurance, tanggung sendiri
Reality: UU PK No.8/1999, liability unlimited jika rusak property
Why it matters: Bangkrut 1 claim
Source: Hukumonline cases
```
