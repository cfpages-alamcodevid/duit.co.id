## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Izin P-IRT (Pangan Industri Rumah Tangga) untuk produksi <100kg/hari
Regulation: Permentan No. 13/2015, Permenkes No. 38/2017
Process: Ajukan ke Dinas Kesehatan Kab/Kota, inspeksi lokasi
Cost: Rp 300.000 - 500.000
Timeline: 14-30 hari
Authority: Dinkes Kab/Kota
Link: https://p-irt.dinkes.go.id/

Requirement: Sertifikasi BPOM ML (untuk mass production >P-IRT)
Regulation: UU Pangan No.18/2012
Process: Online SIPEL via bpom.go.id
Cost: Rp 1-5jt (lab test)
Timeline: 3 bulan
Authority: BPOM
Link: https://e-reg.bpom.go.id/

Requirement: Sertifikat Halal MUI untuk kemasan minuman
Regulation: UU Jaminan Produk Halal 33/2014
Process: Ajukan via halalmui.org
Cost: Rp 500.000 - 2jt
Timeline: 30 hari
Authority: MUI
Link: https://halalmui.org/

Requirement: K3 Mesin (Keselamatan Kerja)
Regulation: Permenaker No.5/2020
Process: Training operator, label safety
Cost: Rp 1jt (kursus)
Timeline: Instan
Authority: Disnaker
Link: https://kemnaker.go.id/
```
