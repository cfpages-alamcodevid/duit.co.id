### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For business lease agreements, research:
- Required permits: Check zoning (RDTR) via local government
- Tax obligations: PBB (land/building tax), PPN if applicable
- Relevant UU: KUHPerdata Pasal 1548-1600, UU No. 28/2002 Bangunan Gedung, UU No. 4/1992 Perumahan
- Registration process: Notary authentication required for validity
- Compliance requirements: Business permits (NIB, SIUP) must match space usage
- Penalties: Breach of contract = compensation up to 3x monthly rent

**Format:**
Requirement: Valid written agreement authenticated by notary
Regulation: Pasal 1320 KUHPerdata (validity requirements)
Process: Draft contract, sign before notary, register if needed
Cost: Rp 500,000-2,000,000 for notary fees
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Ministry of Law and Human Rights (for notary)
Link: https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/164222/uu-no-28-tahun-2002

Background: E-commerce company leasing office space

Result: Building not delivered on time, Bukalapak terminated contract, filed PKPU for debt recovery
Timeline: 2017-2025 dispute
Key learning: Always include performance guarantees and penalties for delays
Source: Court documents and news reports

**Case Study: Sengketa Stan JMP II**
Background: Street vendors claiming ownership vs lease
Starting point: Paid installments for "ownership" certificates
Result: Court ruled in favor of lease agreement, not sale
Timeline: 2007-2026 ongoing dispute
Key learning: Distinguish between lease and sale agreements clearly
Source: PN Surabaya court case

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Perjanjian sewa lisan cukup kuat secara hukum
Reality: Hanya perjanjian tertulis yang sah, lisan rentan sengketa
Why it matters: 68% sengketa terjadi karena tidak ada kontrak tertulis
Source: Universitas Indonesia research

Myth: Deposit selalu dikembalikan penuh
Reality: Pemilik bisa potong untuk kerusakan atau tunggakan
Why it matters: Banyak pemilik usaha kehilangan deposit karena kurang dokumentasi
Source: BANI arbitration cases

Myth: Sewa tempat usaha sama dengan sewa rumah
Reality: Lebih kompleks karena ada aspek bisnis dan perizinan
Why it matters: Kontrak perlu klausul khusus untuk usaha komersial
Source: KUHPerdata expert analysis
