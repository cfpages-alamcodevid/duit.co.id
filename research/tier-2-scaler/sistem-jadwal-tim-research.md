### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Work-rest period regulation for roster systems
Regulation: Peraturan Menteri Tenaga Kerja No. Per-15/Men/VII/2005
Process: Maximum 10 weeks work + 2 weeks rest, 12 hours/day with 1 hour break
Cost: Compliance audits Rp 1-5 million
Timeline: Immediate implementation required
Authority: Ministry of Manpower
Link: https://peraturan.go.id/permenaker-no-15-tahun-2005

Requirement: General labor law compliance for shift work
Regulation: UU No. 13 Tahun 2003 tentang Ketenagakerjaan
Process: Include roster terms in company regulations or collective bargaining agreement
Cost: Legal consultation Rp 500.000-2.000.000
Timeline: Before implementation
Authority: Ministry of Manpower
Link: https://peraturan.go.id/uu-no-13-tahun-2003
