## Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** PMK 69/PMK.010/2024 on Tax Holiday Facilities  
**Regulation:** Ministry of Finance Regulation  
**Process:** Apply via OSS system, submit business plan, get MoF approval  
**Cost:** Free application, but consultancy fees IDR 50-100 million  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months approval process  
**Authority:** Ministry of Finance & BKPM  
**Link:** https://www.kemenkeu.go.id/publikasi/peraturan/detail/pmk-no-69pmk0102024  
**Requirement:** Global Minimum Tax compliance for MNEs  
**Regulation:** OECD Pillar Two, implemented via PMK 136/2024  
**Process:** Automatic top-up tax if ETR <15%, QDMTT for domestic collection  
**Cost:** Additional 15% tax liability if not compliant  
**Timeline:** Effective 2026 for in-scope companies  
**Authority:** Directorate General of Taxes  
**Link:** https://www.pajak.go.id/en/global-minimum-tax  
**Requirement:** Pioneer Industry Certification  
**Regulation:** BKPM Regulation on Pioneer Industries  
**Process:** Self-assessment via OSS, or quantitative scoring  
**Cost:** IDR 10-25 million for verification  
**Timeline:** 1-2 months  
**Authority:** BKPM  
**Link:** https://www.bkpm.go.id/id/regulasi/pioneer-industries
