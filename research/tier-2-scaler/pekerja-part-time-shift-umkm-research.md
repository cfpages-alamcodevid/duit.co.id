## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** PKWT untuk part-time <3 bulan awal, bisa perpanjang max 5 tahun
- Regulation: UU No. 6 Tahun 2023 tentang Ketenagakerjaan, PP No. 35 Tahun 2021
- Process: Buat surat perjanjian tertulis, tanda tangan, salin ke Disnaker jika >1 bulan
- Cost: Gratis (template online)
- Timeline: 1 hari
- Authority: Disnaker setempat
- Link: https://kemnaker.go.id/ - Permenaker No. 2/2022

**Requirement:** BPJS Ketenagakerjaan wajib jika kerja >10 hari berturut2/bln, pro-rata iuran
- Regulation: UU SJSN No.40/2004, Permenaker No.2/2015
- Process: Daftar online BPJSTK, potong gaji karyawan 2%, employer 3.7%
- Cost: Rp 70rb/bln per pekerja (pro rata)
- Timeline: 30 hari setelah mulai kerja
- Authority: BPJS Ketenagakerjaan
- Link: https://www.bpjsketenagakerjaan.go.id/

**Requirement:** Upah min pro-rata UMK/UMP, lembur 1.5x jam normal

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
**Case Study: Cafe Kopi Nusantara (Jakarta)**
- Background: Owner tier 2, 2 outlet, revenue Rp 80jt/bln
- Starting point: Full time night shift mahal Rp 5jt/bln
- Result: Ganti 3 part time mahasiswa, cost Rp 3jt/bln, jam operasi +4 jam, revenue +35%
- Timeline: 2 bulan adaptasi
- Key learning: Shift rotation 4 hari on 3 off, app attendance, bonus attendance
- Source: Testimoni FB Group UMKM F&B Indonesia (2025)

**Case Study: Retail Clothing Bandung**
- Background: Owner Sari, 35th, 1 toko Rp 40jt revenue
- Starting point: Kesulitan shift malam
- Result: Hire 2 part time ibu RT, cost Rp 2.2jt/bln, sales malam +25%
- Timeline: 1 bulan
- Key learning: PKWT 3 bulan trial, training 1 minggu, no BPJS karena <21 hari/bln
- Source: Kaskus thread \"Strategi Part Time Retail\" (2026)

Forum: Reddit r/indonesia, Kaskus - common challenge turnover tinggi, solusi bonus & fleksibel jadwal

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: Part time tidak perlu kontrak tertulis
Reality: Harus PKWT tertulis max 5 tahun, jika lisan bisa dianggap PKWTT (hak full)
Why it matters: Risiko tuntutan pesangon, BPJS full
Source: Permenaker No.11/2015

Myth: Part time <21 hari bebas BPJS
Reality: >10 hari berturut BPJS TK wajib, Kesehatan jika >1 bln
Why it matters: Denda 1% gaji per hari telat
Source: BPJS TK aturan 2023

Myth: Lembur part time tidak bayar
Reality: 1.5x upah/jam normal, max 4 jam/hari
Why it matters: Tuntutan Disnaker
Source: UU Ketenagakerjaan
```
