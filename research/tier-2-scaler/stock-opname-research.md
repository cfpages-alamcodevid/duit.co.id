### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Stock opname hanya perlu dilakukan sekali setahun
Reality: Modern businesses do cycle counting weekly/monthly for accuracy
Why it matters: Prevents stockouts and overstock that hurt scaling
Source: Inventory management best practices

Myth: Digital stock opname mahal dan rumit untuk UMKM
Reality: Free trials available, simple apps like Zoho start at Rp 99k/month
Why it matters: Many miss efficiency gains due to cost assumptions
Source: Software vendor data

Myth: Stock opname hanya untuk retail, bukan manufaktur
Reality: Critical for raw materials, WIP, finished goods tracking
Why it matters: Production planning depends on accurate inventory
Source: Manufacturing consultants
