## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Perjanjian konsinyasi (MoU) tertulis
**Regulation:** KUHPerdata Buku III Bab XVI Pasal 1525-1530 (penitipan barang untuk dijual kembali)
**Process:** 1. Draft MoU (template sederhana), 2. TTD kedua pihak, 3. Stock opname awal, 4. Rutin weekly/monthly reconciliation
**Cost:** Rp 0 (self), Rp 500rb-1,5 juta (notaris legalisasi)
**Timeline:** 1-3 hari
**Authority:** Swasta (perdata), notaris if formalized
**Link:** https://jdih.setneg.go.id/PUUdoc/17348/kuhperdata.pdf

**Requirement:** Pajak penjualan normal (PPN 11% if >4.8M/bln)
**Regulation:** UU HPP 2021, PMK 65/2023
**Process:** Laporan SPT Masa PPN
**Cost:** Normal
**Timeline:** Bulanan

Penalties: Sengketa perdata, tuntutan ganti rugi if breach

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
```
Myth: Konsinyasi 100% gratis dan zero risk
Reality: Bayar komisi 5-10%, tanggung shrinkage 2-5%, return rusak
Why it matters: Bisa rugi Rp 1-2 juta/bln if no control, putus relation supplier
Source: Survey UMKM Zahir 2024

Myth: Supplier konsinyasi susah dicari
Reality: Ribuan grosir Bandung/Tangerang siap (cari 'grosir konsinyasi Shopee')
Why it matters: Mulai langsung tanpa survey panjang
Source: Shopee search data
```
