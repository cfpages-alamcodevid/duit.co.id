### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Data collection and processing must comply with UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 (PDP Law)
Regulation: Undang-Undang Pelindungan Data Pribadi
Process: Obtain consent for personal data, implement data security measures, report breaches
Cost: Minimal, but potential fines for non-compliance (up to Rp 5 billion)
Timeline: Immediate for existing data, 1-2 years for full compliance
Authority: Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika (Kominfo)
Link: https://jdih.kominfo.go.id/produk-hukum/uu-no-27-tahun-2022

Requirement: Financial data handling via OJK regulations for MSMEs
Regulation: Peraturan OJK No. 1/POJK.07/2013
Process: Secure customer financial data, annual reporting
Cost: Compliance audit costs (Rp 1-5 million/year)
Timeline: Ongoing
Authority: Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK)
Link: https://ojk.go.id/id/aturan/Peraturan-OJK/Pages/POJK-No-1-POJK-07-2013.aspx
