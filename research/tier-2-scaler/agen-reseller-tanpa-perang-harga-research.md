# 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

```
Requirement: Tidak boleh fix harga jual (RPM/Resale Price Maintenance) via perjanjian, dilarang UU No.5/1999 Persaingan Usaha
Regulation: Peraturan KPPU No.3/2010 tentang Perjanjian Vertikal; HET hanya untuk barang esensial (UU 7/2014)
Process: Gunakan HJR (Harga Jual Rekomendasi) atau MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) tanpa sanksi pemutusan
Cost: Notaris kontrak Rp1-2jt
Timeline: 1 minggu
Authority: KPPU
Link: https://kppu.go.id/regulasi/

Requirement: Territory exclusivity ok jika reasonable (tidak kartel)
Regulation: Pasal 15 UU Persaingan Usaha
Process: Tentukan zona kota/kecamatan di kontrak
Cost: 0
Timeline: Instant
Authority: KPPU
Link: https://kppu.go.id/

Requirement: Pajak PPh 23 untuk komisi agen >Rp4.8jt/tahun
Regulation: PMK 141/2015
Process: Potong 2% atau lapor eSPT
Cost: Admin software Rp500rb/year
Timeline: Bulanan
Authority: DJP
Link: pajak.go.id
```

# 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

```
Myth: MAP/HJR sama dengan kartel harga, pasti kena KPPU
Reality: Legal jika voluntary & no enforcement (hanya rekomendasi + incentive)
Why it matters: Banyak UMKM takut, stuck single channel
Source: KPPU guideline 2020

Myth: Territory exclusivity blokir kompetisi
Reality: Allowed jika pro-competitive (efisiensi distribusi)
Why it matters: Hindari overlap sales, tingkatkan loyalty
Source: UU Persaingan Usaha Pasal 15
```
