### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Influencer disclosure and transparency
Regulation: Undang-Undang Nomor 8 Tahun 1999 tentang Perlindungan Konsumen (UU Perlindungan Konsumen)
Process: Influencers must disclose sponsored content with clear hashtags like #Ad or #Sponsored
Cost: No direct cost, but non-compliance can lead to fines
Timeline: Immediate for all campaigns
Authority: Badan Perlindungan Konsumen Nasional (BPKN)
Link: https://www.kemendag.go.id/uu-perlindungan-konsumen

Requirement: Content authenticity and truth in advertising
Regulation: Undang-Undang Nomor 32 Tahun 2002 tentang Penyiaran (Broadcasting Law)
Process: Register content with KPI if broadcast, but social media falls under self-regulation
Cost: Free
Timeline: Ongoing compliance
Authority: Komisi Penyiaran Indonesia (KPI)
Link: https://kpi.go.id/

Requirement: Protection against misleading information
Regulation: Undang-Undang Nomor 11 Tahun 2008 tentang Informasi dan Transaksi Elektronik (UU ITE)
Process: Influencers liable for false claims under Article 28
Cost: Potential fines up to Rp 50 million
Timeline: Post-campaign monitoring
Authority: Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika
Link: https://kominfo.go.id/

Requirement: Intellectual property rights
Regulation: Undang-Undang Nomor 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta
Process: Written agreements for content usage rights
Cost: Notary fees for contracts (Rp 100.000-200.000)
Timeline: Before campaign start
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual
Link: https://dgip.go.id/
