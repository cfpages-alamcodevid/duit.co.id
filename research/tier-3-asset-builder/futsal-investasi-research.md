## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Business License (Izin Usaha Industri Olahraga)
Regulation: UU No. 3/2022 tentang Sistem Keolahragaan Nasional
Process: Apply to local sports department, submit facility specifications, safety compliance
Cost: Rp 5-15 juta
Timeline: 2-3 months
Authority: KONI (Komite Olahraga Nasional Indonesia) & Local Government
Link: https://koni.or.id

**Requirement:** Building Construction Permit (IMB)
Regulation: Permen PU No. 26/PRT/M/2020 tentang Bangunan Gedung
Process: Submit architectural plans, structural calculations, safety compliance
Cost: Rp 10-25 juta (depending on city and building size)
Timeline: 1-2 months
Authority: Dinas Pekerjaan Umum dan Perumahan Rakyat
Link: https://pu.go.id

**Requirement:** Business Operational License (SIUP)
Regulation: UU No. 5/1999 tentang Larangan Praktik Monopoli dan Persaingan Usaha Tidak Sehat
Process: Register at local trade office, tax registration
Cost: Rp 2-5 juta
Timeline: 2-4 weeks
Authority: Dinas Perizinan dan Penanaman Modal
Link: https://www.sip.go.id

**Requirement:** Safety Standards Compliance
Regulation: SNI 03-7063-2005 tentang Keamanan Pusat Olahraga
Process: Safety audit, emergency procedures, equipment certification
Cost: Rp 3-8 juta for certification
Timeline: 1 month
Authority: National Standardization Agency (BSN)
Link: https://bsn.go.id

**Requirement:** Insurance Requirements
Regulation: OJK Regulation No. 77/POJK.03/2016
Process: Public liability insurance, property insurance, work accident insurance
Cost: Rp 12-25 juta annually
Timeline: 2-3 weeks
Authority: OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan)
Link: https://www.ojk.go.id
