### 3. Key Data & Statistics
*   **Statistic:** Rata-rata Gap Harga "Ask" vs Harga "Deal" 2026: 8% - 15% (Secondary Market).
*   **Statistic:** Waktu Rata-rata Properti Terjual (Days on Market) 2026: 120 - 180 hari.
*   **Statistic:** Komisi Agent Standard 2026: 2-3% (Dibayar Penjual).
*   **Statistic:** Biaya Appraisal Independen: Rp 2jt - 5jt.

### 4. Market Research
**Negotiation Levers 2026:**
1.  **Speed of Cash:** Cash keras usually gets 5-10% more discount than KPR.
2.  **Tax Incentives:** If the seller can't provide PPN DTP (secondary), use that as a 10% price drop argument.
3.  **Appraisal Deficit:** If the bank appraisal is lower than the price, the seller must drop the price or the deal dies.
4.  **Renovation Costs:** Detailed quote for repairs to be deducted from the price.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Surat Penawaran Formal (Letter of Intent).
*   **Requirement:** PPJB (Perjanjian Pengikatan Jual Beli) with clear "price and payment terms".
*   **Warning:** Never pay "Booking Fee" without a signed document stating the fee is refundable if the KPR is rejected or negotiation fails.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Case Study:** "Dina, 29." Target house 1.2M. Agent said "Nett price is 1.15M". Dina did an independent appraisal (result: 1.05M) and showed the seller the 2026 market data for similar units. Seller agreed to 1.07M. Savings: Rp 80jt.
*   **Expert Quote:** "The one who cares the least about the deal wins the negotiation. In 2026, there are more sellers than buyers in certain areas. Walk away, and they will call you back." - Property Negotiator.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "Flinch" Technique:**
- Seller: "Harga nett 2 Miliar."
- Buyer: (Silent for 5 seconds, looks at the ceiling) "Oof... 2 Miliar? (Deep breath) I saw a similar unit for 1.7M down the road, and yours needs a new roof. My max is 1.6M cash today."
- *Result:* Usually triggers a counter-offer between 1.7M - 1.8M.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Menawar terlalu rendah itu tidak sopan."
*   **Reality:** Business is business. As long as you have data (appraisal/market comparison), no price is too low to start.
*   **Myth:** "Agen properti ada di pihak pembeli."
*   **Reality:** Agents only get paid when the deal closes. They want the highest price (higher commission) and the fastest deal. Don't reveal your max budget to the agent.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Market Research.** Find 5 similar properties nearby and their "sold" prices.
2.  **Step 2: Get a Pre-Approval.** Show the seller you are a "Serious Buyer" with bank backing.
3.  **Step 3: The Inspection.** Find every flaw (leaks, cracks, damp) to build a "Repair List".
4.  **Step 4: The Low-Ball with Data.** Start 20% lower than your target price, backed by the Repair List and Appraisal.
