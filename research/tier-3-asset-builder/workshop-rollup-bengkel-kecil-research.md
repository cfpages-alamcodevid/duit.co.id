## 4. Market Research (Business Idea Article)

**Capital Requirements:**
```
Minimum capital to start: Rp 500 juta (1-2 bengkel pilot + setup central).
Breakdown:
- Equipment/tools upgrade: Rp 50 juta (lift, diagnostic tools - Tokopedia/Shopee Rp 20-30 jt/unit)
- Initial inventory (parts): Rp 100 juta (oli, filter, ban - central buy from distributor seperti Sumber Jaya Parts)
- Marketing budget (branding chain): Rp 50 juta (first 3 months, Google Ads/FB)
- Operating buffer: Rp 150 juta (3 months salaries 10 teknisi Rp 5-7 jt/bln)
- Acquisition deposit/legal: Rp 150 juta
Total: Rp 500 juta

Where to buy:
- Acquisitions: OLX.co.id (search \"jual bengkel\" - e.g. Jakarta Rp 200-400 jt, Bandung Rp 100-250 jt)
- Parts: Tokopedia/Alibabaindonesia (bulk 20% discount), distributor lokal (Astra, Nippon)
- Equipment: Shopee/Tokopedia (scanner OBD Rp 1 jt, compressor Rp 15 jt)
- Price comparison: OLX vs FB Marketplace (nego 10-20%)
```

```
Average selling price: Service Rp 500rb-2 jt/job (ganti oli Rp 200rb, tune-up Rp 1 jt)
Cost per unit: Rp 300-600rb (parts+labor)
Profit margin: 25-35% small, 40% post-central procurement.

Monthly projection:
- Conservative: Rp 300 jt revenue (5 shops x Rp 60 jt), profit Rp 90 jt
- Moderate: Rp 500 jt (8 shops), profit Rp 175 jt
- Optimistic: Rp 800 jt (12 shops), profit Rp 320 jt

Time to break-even: 8-12 months (acquisition multiple 1.5x annual profit).
```

**Market Demand:**

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** SIUP Mikro/Kecil untuk setiap lokasi, TDP pusat, NPWP konsolidasi.
Regulation: UU 20/2008 UMKM, Permen 17/2021 OSS.
Process: Daftar OSS.go.id (online, 1 hari), upload Akta CV/PT.
Cost: Rp 0-1 jt (gratis OSS).
Timeline: 1-3 hari.
Authority: Kemenkop UKM / BKPM.
Link: https://oss.go.id/

**Requirement:** Izin lingkungan (UKL-UPL if waste oil >1 ton/bln).
Regulation: PP 22/2021 Pengelolaan Lingkungan.
Process: DLH provinsi.
Cost: Rp 5-10 jt.
Timeline: 14 hari.
Link: https://menlhk.go.id/

Penalties: Denda Rp 100 jt+ non-compliance.
