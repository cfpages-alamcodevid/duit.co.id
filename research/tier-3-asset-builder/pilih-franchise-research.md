### 4. Market Research
**Capital Requirements:**
*   Range: Rp 10 Juta - Rp 1 Miliar+.
*   Common inclusions: Equipment, Initial ingredients, Training, License fee (5 years).
*   Hidden costs: Rental deposit, Legal permits (PBG/SLF), Employee recruitment fees.

**Revenue Potential:**
*   Margin F&B: 20-40% (high operational effort).
*   Margin Services (Laundry): 25-35% (lower labor intensity).
*   Margin Retail (Indomaret): 10-15% (high stability, high volume).

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** STPW (Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba).
*   **Regulation:** PP No. 42 Tahun 2007 and its updates.
*   **Authority:** Ministry of Trade (Kemendagri).
*   **Warning:** Without STPW, a business is legally a "Business Opportunity" (BO), not a "Franchise", offering less protection.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Case Study:** "Budi, 40, Surabaya." Invested in a 'Vending Machine' franchise for laundry detergent. Modal 45jt. Autopilot. 2026 trend. Passive income Rp 3jt/month. BEP 15 months.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "Three-Bucket" Selection Strategy:**
1.  **Bucket 1 (Cash Cow):** Low margin, high stability (Indomaret).
2.  **Bucket 2 (Hype/Trend):** High margin, higher risk (Viral F&B like Gacoan/Mixue).
3.  **Bucket 3 (Service/Niche):** Steady demand, low labor (Self-service Laundry).

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Franchise pasti untung."
*   **Reality:** 70% depends on location. A great brand in a bad location will fail.
*   **Myth:** "Franchisor akan mengurus semuanya."
*   **Reality:** Unless it's "Full Autopilot", you still need to manage the staff and local marketing.
