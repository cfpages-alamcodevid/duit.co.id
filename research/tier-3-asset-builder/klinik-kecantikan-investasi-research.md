## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### Requirement: BPOM Registration
**Regulation:** UU No. 8 Tahun 1999 tentang Pengawas Obat dan Makanan
**Process:** Submit product documentation, testing reports, facility inspection
**Cost:** Rp 5-10 juta
**Timeline:** 3-6 months
**Authority:** BPRI (Badan Pengawas Obat dan Makanan)
**Link:** https://bpom.go.id

### Requirement: Izin Usaha Industri Kecil (Izin Usaha)
**Regulation:** PP No. 78 Tahun 2020 tentang Perizinan Usaha Industri
**Process:** Apply to local DPMPTSP, submit business plan, facility compliance
**Cost:** Rp 2-5 juta
**Timeline:** 2-4 months
**Authority:** Local government (DPMPTSP)
**Link:** https://www.dpmptsp.go.id

### Requirement: Izin Kesehatan Lingkungan (Izin BK)
**Regulation:** Permenkes No. 98 Tahun 2020 tentang Kesehatan Lingkungan
**Process:** Environmental health inspection, waste management compliance
**Cost:** Rp 1-3 juta
**Timeline:** 1-2 months
**Authority:** Dinas Kesehatan
**Link:** https://keswan.kemkes.go.id

### Requirement: Sertifikasi Tenaga Ahli
**Regulation:** Permenaker No. 5 Tahun 2021 tentang Kompetensi Tenaga Kesehatan
**Process:** Staff certification, training records
**Cost:** Rp 500,000 - 2,000,000 per staff
**Timeline:** Ongoing
**Authority:** Kemkes (Kementerian Kesehatan)
**Link:** https://www.kemkes.go.id

### Requirement: BPJS Tenaga Kerja
**Regulation:** UU No. 24 Tahun 2011 tentang BPJS Tenaga Kerja
**Process:** Register employees, monthly contributions
**Cost:** 3-4% of employee salary
**Timeline:** Monthly
**Authority:** BPJS Ketenagakerjaan
**Link:** https://www.bpjsketenagakerjaan.co.id

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

### Myth: Beauty clinics require medical license to operate
**Reality:** Non-medical beauty treatments (facials, skincare, body treatments) don't require medical license, only esthetician certification
**Why it matters:** Reduces entry barriers for non-medical entrepreneurs
**Source:** BPOM & Kemkes regulations

### Myth: Equipment depreciates quickly like salon furniture
**Reality:** Professional beauty equipment depreciates only 15-25% over 5 years vs 50-70% for furniture
**Why it matters:** Equipment maintains value as collateral and appreciates with technology
**Source:** Equipment valuation data

### Myth: Beauty business is highly seasonal and inconsistent
**Reality:** While holidays are peak, regular treatments create predictable recurring revenue
**Why it matters:** Business model supports stable cash flow and financing
**Source:** Industry financial statements

### Myth: Beauty clinics require very large spaces to be profitable
**Reality:** 40-60 sqm premium spaces can be highly profitable with right location and services
**Why it matters:** Lower real estate investment, higher ROI potential
**Source:** Multiple clinic financial case studies
