## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Kontrak Makloon (perjanjian produksi)
Regulation: KUH Perdata Pasal 1233-1253, UU No. 30/2000 Rahasia Dagang (formula/IP)
Process: Draft notaris, tanda tangan, BPJS TK/ BPJS Kesehatan wajib
Cost: Rp 2-5 juta (notaris + materai)
Timeline: 1 minggu
Authority: Disnaker, Kemenkumham
Link: https://jdih.kemenkumham.go.id/

Requirement: Sertifikasi Halal (untuk makanan/kosmetik)
Regulation: UU Jaminan Produk Halal 33/2014
Process: Daftar MUI
Cost: Rp 1-3 juta
Timeline: 1-3 bulan
Link: https://halalmui.org/
```
