## Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** Valid land title (Hak Milik for citizens, Hak Pakai/HGB for foreigners via PT PMA).  
**Regulation:** UU No. 5/1960 Agrarian Law, PP 18/2021.  
**Process:** Register via BPN, obtain Hak Pakai (30 years + extensions).  
**Cost:** IDR 50-200M for legal and registration.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months.  
**Authority:** National Land Agency (BPN).  
**Link:** https://www.bpn.go.id/  

**Requirement:** Zoning compliance (RTRW).  
**Regulation:** Regional spatial plans.  
**Process:** Verify with local government.  
**Cost:** IDR 10-50M.  
**Timeline:** 1 month.
