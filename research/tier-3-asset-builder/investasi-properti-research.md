### 4. Market Research
**Capital Requirements:**
*   Minimum capital: Rp 50 - 100 Juta (as DP for KPR).
*   Breakdown:
    *   DP 10-15%: Rp 50.000.000 (for 500jt house).
    *   Biaya Notaris & Admin: Rp 15.000.000.
    *   Pajak (BPHTB): Rp 0 (if MBR/First home in certain regions) or ~Rp 20.000.000.
*   Total: Rp 85.000.000.

**Revenue Potential:**
*   Capital Appreciation: 5-12% per year (Location dependent).
*   Rental Yield: 3-15% per year.
*   Time to break-even: 7-12 years (Rental only) or 3-5 years (Flip).

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** Sertifikat Hak Milik (SHM) or HGB.
*   **Regulation:** UU No. 1/2022 (HKPD) for taxes.
*   **Process:** Notary check, BPHTB payment, AJB signing.
*   **Authority:** BPN (Badan Pertanahan Nasional).
*   **2026 Update:** Permendagri No. 14/2025 bans PBB rate hikes in 2026 to maintain purchasing power.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Investasi properti harus punya uang milyaran."
*   **Reality:** With KPR and government incentives (PPN DTP), 100jt is enough for a starting asset.
*   **Myth:** "Beli properti pasti untung."
*   **Reality:** Location and occupancy management determine the profit, especially in high-supply areas like certain apartment clusters.
