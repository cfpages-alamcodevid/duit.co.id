## 4. Market Research (Business Model)

**Capital Requirements:**
Minimum capital to start: Rp 300.000.000 (untuk setup sistem, bukan per outlet)
Breakdown:
- SOP/training manual development (konsultan): Rp 100.000.000
- Legal contract (KSO/revenue share template, notaris): Rp 50.000.000
- Marketing rekrut partner (FB ads, expo UMKM): Rp 100.000.000
- Software POS remote audit (Moka/Zapier): Rp 50.000.000 (setup + 1yr)
Total: Rp 300.000.000

Where to buy:
- Konsultan SOP: Upwork/Fiverr Indonesian Rp 20jt, or lokal agency Rp 50jt+
- Lawyer: Hukumonline partners Rp 20-30jt/template
- POS: moka.co.id Rp 299rb/bln/outlet
- Expo: Franchise Expo Jakarta tiket Rp 5jt/booth

**Revenue Potential:**
Average selling price: Revenue share 25-35% dari gross sales outlet
Cost per unit: Biaya support 10% dari share (training, marketing pusat)
Profit margin: 70-80%

Monthly projection:
- Conservative: 3 outlets x Rp 300jt sales x 25% share x 70% margin = Rp 157.5jt net
- Moderate: 5 x Rp 300jt x 30% x 75% = Rp 337.5jt net
- Optimistic: 10 x Rp 400jt x 35% x 80% = Rp 1.12M net

Time to break-even: 4-6 bulan (dengan 2-3 partner pertama)

**Market Demand:**
Search volume: &quot;franchise murah&quot; 50k/bln, &quot;kemitraan bisnis&quot; 20k/bln (est. Google Keyword Planner)
Trend: Increasing 20% YoY (Google Trends)

## 5. Legal &amp; Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Kontrak Kerjasama Operasi (KSO) atau Lisensi Merek + Fee Royalty/Revenue Share
Regulation: KUH Perdata Buku III Bab Kerjasama, UU Merek No 20/2016, jika &gt;franchise-like: Permenkumham 71/2019
Process: 1. Daftar merek HKI 2. Buat perjanjian notaris 3. (Opsional) Daftar franchise Kemenkumham jika full model
Cost: Rp 20jt (merek) + Rp 30jt (notaris)
Timeline: 2-4 minggu
Authority: Dirjen HKI (merek), Notaris, Kemenkumham (franchise)
Link: https://jdih.kemenkumham.go.id (Permen 71/2019), https://www.hukumonline.com/klinik (KSO guide)

Requirement: Audit Clause - Hak inspect buku &amp; stock
Regulation: Kontrak swasta
Penalties: Termination + ganti rugi jika fraud

Requirement: Termination - Notice 30-90 hari
Regulation: Kontrak, KUH 1446 (pembatalan)
Penalties: Forfeit deposit Rp 50-100jt

## 8. Common Myths &amp; Misconceptions
Myth: Partner outlet = zero capex &amp; zero risk
Reality: Capex awal sistem Rp 300jt+, risiko partner underperform/moral hazard
Why it matters: Overestimate profit, no audit = loss control
Source: Forum UMKM complaints

Myth: Kontrak sederhana cukup
Reality: Butuh clause detail audit, IP protection, non-compete
Why it matters: Sengketa mahal Rp 100jt+
Source: Hukumonline KSO guides

## 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Validasi kesiapan bisnis (brand strength, SOP siap?)
- Details: Checklist 10 poin (unique menu, supply chain)
- Example: Cafe Andi test SOP di cabang owned
- Warning: Weak brand = partner gagal

Step 2: Bangun legal framework (kontrak KSO)
- Details: Clause revenue share, audit quarterly, termination 60 hari
- Example: Template from lawyer Rp 30jt
- Warning: Skip notaris = tidak enforceable

Step 3: Rekrut &amp; pilot partner (1-2 lokasi)
- Details: Criteria (lokasi, pengalaman, deposit Rp 50jt)
- Example: Ads FB, expo UMKM
- Warning: Screening ketat hindari fraud

Step 4: Launch, monitor, scale
- Details: Training 2 minggu, POS connect real-time
- Example: Audit bulanan sales/stock variance &lt;5%
- Warning: Early termination jika KPI miss

Step 5: Optimize &amp; exit clause
- Details: Annual review, renegotiate share
- Warning: Succession plan jika owner pensiun
