### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For land certificate due diligence in Indonesia.

**Requirement: Certificate Authenticity Check**
Regulation: UUPA No. 5/1960, PP No. 24/1997.
Process: Physical inspection (watermark, seal) + BPN verification via Sentuh Tanahku or office visit.
Cost: Rp 50,000 - Rp 150,000.
Timeline: 1-2 days online, 1 week office.
Authority: Badan Pertanahan Nasional (BPN).
Link: https://www.atrbpn.go.id/

**Requirement: Boundary and Dispute Verification**
Regulation: SE ATR/BPN No. 1/2026.
Process: Survey mapping, check Buku Tanah for encumbrances.
Cost: Rp 1,000,000+ for surveyor.
Timeline: 2-4 weeks.
Authority: BPN and licensed surveyors.

**Requirement: Electronic Certificate Conversion (2026 Update)**
Regulation: Permen ATR/BPN No. 16/2021 updated 2026.
Process: Submit old certificate, pay PNBP Rp 150,000, get digital version.
Cost: Rp 150,000 + admin.
Timeline: 1-3 days.
Authority: BPN.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
**Case Study: Tanah Abang Dispute**
Background: Ahli waris vs Kementerian PKP over 34,690 m² land.
Starting point: Old Verponding document claim.
Result: Ongoing sengketa, government project delayed.
Timeline: Since 2025, unresolved.
Key learning: Always verify against BPN records, old documents like Verponding invalid in modern system.

Starting point: 1974 Gran document, but claimed by others.
Result: Court case ongoing, estimated loss Rp 160 million in crops.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: "Girik documents are still valid proof of ownership."
Reality: Girik no longer recognized as full proof since 2026 digital system; must convert to SHM.
Why it matters: Leads to invalid claims, disputes.
Source: ATR/BPN regulations 2026.

Myth: "Online apps like Sentuh Tanahku are sufficient for due diligence."
Reality: Apps provide basic info; full verification requires BPN office and PPAT.
Why it matters: Misses hidden encumbrances.
Source: BPN guidelines.
