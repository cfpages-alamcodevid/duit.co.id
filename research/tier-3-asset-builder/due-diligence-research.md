### 4. Market Research
**The 4 Pillars of Indonesian Due Diligence:**
1.  **Financial (Keuangan):** Checking bank mutations vs. POS data. Analyzing tax reports (SPT) vs. actual sales.
2.  **Legal (Hukum):** NIB, Izin Lokasi, HAKI (Brand), and labor contracts. Checking for active lawsuits in PN (Pengadilan Negeri).
3.  **Operational (Operasional):** Supplier reliability, equipment condition, and "Key Person Risk" (does the business die if the owner leaves?).
4.  **Commercial (Komersial):** Customer reviews, secret shopping, and competitor density in the area.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha) via OSS RBA.
*   **Regulation:** UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 (UU PT) for corporate entities.
*   **Process:** Background check via AHU Online (Kemenkumham) for company structure and history.
*   **Compliance:** Check for BPJS Ketenagakerjaan compliance (hidden liabilities).

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Red Flag Checklist for Indonesia:**
*   **The "Shadow" Owner:** Business is registered under a cousin/employee's name (Risk of legal dispute).
*   **The "Lease Trap":** Business is for sale because the 5-year lease is ending and the landlord is doubling the rent.
*   **The "Fake Crowd":** Seller hires friends to sit in the cafe during the buyer's visit. (Verification: Check Wi-Fi logs or POS timestamp frequency).

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Cukup lihat laporan laba rugi (P&L)."
*   **Reality:** Cash Flow is more important. Profit is an opinion, Cash is a fact.
*   **Myth:** "Kalau sudah ada izin resmi pasti aman."
*   **Reality:** Permits can be fake or expired. Always check via official government portals (OSS, AHU, DJP).

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Initial Gut Check.** Visit the location unannounced.
2.  **Step 2: Financial Deep Dive.** Request 12 months of bank statements and compare with POS records.
3.  **Step 3: Legal Audit.** Hire a notary or use AHU Online to check company standing.
4.  **Step 4: The "Interrogation".** Ask why they are *really* selling.
5.  **Step 5: Valuation Modeling.** Use conservative multipliers (2x-4x annual profit).
