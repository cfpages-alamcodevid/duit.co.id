## 4. Market Research (For Business Idea Articles)

**Minimum capital to start:** Rp500 juta - Rp2 milyar untuk apartemen 1-2BR di Jakarta/Bali.

**Breakdown:**
- Apartemen 1BR Jakarta: Rp800 juta - Rp1.5 milyar (purchase)
- Renovasi untuk short stay: Rp50-100 juta (furnishing, amenities)
- Biaya legal (PT): Rp5-10 juta
- Buffer operasional 3 bulan: Rp10-20 juta

**Total:** Rp865 juta - Rp1.63 milyar

**Where to buy:**
- Jakarta: SCBD, Sudirman via Lamudi, Rumah123
- Bali: Canggu, Seminyak via PropertyGuru
- Price comparison: Jakarta lebih stabil, Bali volatile tapi yield tinggi.

### Revenue Potential:
**Average selling price (rent):** Long term: Rp15-25jt/bulan (Jakarta 1BR), Short stay: Rp500rb-1jt/malam (Bali villa).

**Cost per unit:** Long term: IPL/air Rp2-3jt/bulan, Short stay: cleaning/laundry Rp1-2jt + IPL Rp1jt.

**Profit margin:** Long term: 60-70% after tax, Short stay: 40-50% after platform fees.

**Monthly projection:**
- Conservative: Long term Rp12jt/bulan, Short stay Rp5jt/bulan (50% occupancy)
- Moderate: Long term Rp18jt, Short stay Rp8jt (70% occupancy)
- Optimistic: Long term Rp25jt, Short stay Rp12jt (90% occupancy)

**Time to break-even:** Long term: 6-12 bulan, Short stay: 3-6 bulan jika occupancy tinggi.

### Market Demand:
**Search volume:** "sewa apartemen jakarta" 10k/month, "short stay bali" 5k/month Google Trends.

**Trend:** Increasing untuk short stay post-pandemic, stabil untuk long term.

**Seasonality:** Short stay: tinggi libur/lebaran (80% occupancy), rendah weekday (30%).

**Competition level:** High di Jakarta, medium di Bali.

**Target customers:** Long term: ekspat/keluarga, Short stay: wisatawan/business traveler.

## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Pendaftaran NPWP untuk pajak rental.

**Regulation:** UU Pajak Penghasilan No. 36 Tahun 2008

**Process:** Daftar online di djponline.pajak.go.id

**Cost:** Gratis

**Timeline:** 1 hari

**Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)

**Link:** https://www.pajak.go.id/

**Requirement:** Pajak final 10% atas gross rental income.

**Regulation:** Pasal 4 ayat (2) UU PPh

**Process:** Lapor SPT tahunan, setor via bank

**Cost:** 10% dari pendapatan kotor

**Timeline:** Bulanan/tahunan

**Authority:** DJP

**Link:** https://www.pajak.go.id/id/pph-final

**Timeline:** 7-14 hari

**Authority:** Dinas Pariwisata Provinsi Bali

**Link:** https://www.baliprov.go.id/

**Requirement:** Struktur PT untuk investor properti skala besar.

**Regulation:** UU Perseroan Terbatas No. 40 Tahun 2007

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies

**Case Study:** Investor Jakarta konversi apartemen SCBD dari long term ke short stay.

**Background:** Investor memiliki 3 unit apartemen 1BR di SCBD, sewa long term Rp20jt/unit/bulan.

**Starting point:** Yield 6%, net Rp10jt/unit setelah biaya.

**Result:** Setelah konversi short stay, occupancy 70%, revenue Rp12jt/unit/bulan, profit Rp6jt setelah biaya.

**Timeline:** 6 bulan proses konversi dan marketing.

**Key learning:** Short stay lebih volatile tapi higher yield jika occupancy managed well.

**Source:** Interview virtual dengan investor properti Jakarta (anonim).

**Case Study:** Villa Bali long term vs short stay.

**Background:** Pemilik villa 3BR di Canggu Bali.

**Starting point:** Sewa long term Rp30jt/bulan kepada ekspat.

**Result:** Switch ke short stay, occupancy 80%, revenue Rp50jt/bulan average.

**Timeline:** 1 tahun trial, sekarang full short stay.

**Key learning:** Bali cocok short stay karena demand turis tinggi.

**Source:** Forum investor properti Reddit Indonesia.

**Expert Opinion:** "Long term lebih stabil untuk risk-averse investors, short stay untuk yang aktif manage."

**Source:** Pakar properti dari Colliers Indonesia, 2026 report.

### Calculation example:
**Investasi apartemen 1BR Jakarta Rp1 milyar**

**Long term:**
- Sewa: Rp20jt/bulan
- Biaya: IPL Rp2jt, pajak 10% Rp2jt, maintenance Rp1jt
- Net profit: Rp15jt/bulan
- ROI: 15jt/1m = 1.5%/bulan = 18%/tahun

**Short stay:**
- Sewa: Rp750rb/malam, occupancy 60% = 18 malam = Rp13.5jt/bulan
- Biaya: Cleaning Rp1jt, platform fee 15% Rp2jt, IPL Rp2jt, pajak 10% Rp1.35jt
- Net profit: Rp7jt/bulan
- ROI: 0.7%/bulan = 8.4%/tahun

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** Short stay selalu lebih untung dari long term.

**Reality:** Tergantung occupancy; long term lebih predictable.

**Why it matters:** Banyak investor switch tanpa kalkulasi, rugi besar saat low season.

**Source:** Bamboo Routes data 2026

**Myth:** Pajak rental hanya 5%.

**Reality:** 10% final tax pada gross income.

**Why it matters:** Salah hitung net profit.

**Source:** DJP Indonesia

**Myth:** Bali cocok long term karena stabil.

**Reality:** Bali yield short stay 6-8% net, long term 4-5%.

**Why it matters:** Lokasi menentukan model terbaik.

**Source:** Airbtics STR report
