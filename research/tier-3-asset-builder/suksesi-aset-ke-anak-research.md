## Legal & Regulatory Requirements
**Requirement:** Inheritance Law Application  
**Regulation:** KUHPerdata (Civil Law) for non-Muslims, Kompilasi Hukum Islam (KHI) for Muslims  
**Process:** Follow respective inheritance divisions, obtain Surat Keterangan Waris  
**Cost:** Rp 500rb-2jt for legal consultation  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months for full process  
**Authority:** Pengadilan Negeri/Agama, Notary  
**Link:** https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/ (for KUHPerdata), https://kemenag.go.id/ (for KHI)  

**Requirement:** Property Transfer Tax (BPHTB Waris)  
**Regulation:** UU No. 28 Tahun 2009 about Regional Taxes and Levies  
**Process:** Calculate 2.5% of (NJOP - NPOPTKP), pay at BPN  
**Cost:** 2.5% of property value above NPOPTKP (minimum Rp300jt)  
**Timeline:** During property transfer process  
**Authority:** Badan Pertanahan Nasional (BPN)  
**Link:** https://www.bpn.go.id/  

**Requirement:** No Inheritance Tax in Indonesia  
**Regulation:** UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 about Income Tax (no inheritance tax provision)  
**Process:** Assets transferred without inheritance tax  
**Cost:** None for inheritance tax  
**Timeline:** N/A  
**Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)  
**Link:** https://www.djp.go.id/

## Common Myths & Misconceptions

### Myth: Inheritance tax applies in Indonesia
**Reality:** No inheritance tax exists in Indonesia  
**Why it matters:** Prevents unnecessary tax planning fears  
**Source:** UU No. 36/2008 Income Tax Law  

### Myth: Only written wills are valid
**Reality:** Oral wills valid in certain circumstances, but written recommended  
**Why it matters:** Ensures legal validity  
**Source:** KUHPerdata Pasal 875  

### Myth: Business shares can't be inherited
**Reality:** PT shares transferable to heirs under UUPT  
**Why it matters:** Allows business continuity  
**Source:** UU No. 40/2007 PT Law
