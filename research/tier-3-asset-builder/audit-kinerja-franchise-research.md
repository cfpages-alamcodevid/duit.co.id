### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For franchise audits, compliance with business regulations and audit standards.

**Requirement: Audited Financial Statements**
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas
Process: Companies with assets > Rp50 billion or public companies must have audited financials
Cost: Rp 50-200 juta per audit depending on company size
Timeline: Annual audit, monthly internal reviews
Authority: Public accounting firms registered with IAPI
Link: https://www.ojk.go.id/sustainable-finance/id/peraturan/undang-undang/Documents/5.%20UU-40-2007%20PERSEROAN%20TERBATAS.pdf

**Requirement: Franchise Registration (STPW)**
Regulation: PP No. 42 Tahun 2007 and Permendag 71/2019
Process: Register through OSS system, provide audited financials showing 3+ years profitability
Cost: Rp 1-5 juta administrative fees
Timeline: Initial registration, annual renewals
Authority: Ministry of Trade through OSS
Link: https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Details/39965

**Requirement: Tax Compliance Audits**
Regulation: UU No. 6 Tahun 1983 tentang Ketentuan Umum dan Tata Cara Perpajakan
Process: Submit SPT Tahunan with audited financials for companies > certain thresholds
Cost: Included in general audit costs
Timeline: Annual tax audits
Authority: Directorate General of Taxes
Link: Official tax regulation documents

**Requirement: Business License Compliance**
Regulation: UU No. 3 Tahun 1982 tentang Wajib Daftar Perusahaan
Process: Maintain NIB and business licenses for all franchise outlets
Cost: Rp 500,000-2 juta per renewal
Timeline: Annual renewals
Authority: Ministry of Investment/BKPM
Link: OSS system documentation
