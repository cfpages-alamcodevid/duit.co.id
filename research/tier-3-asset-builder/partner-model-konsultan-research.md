### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
For consulting partnerships in Indonesia (relevant to UU No. 40/2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas, PP No. 43/2011):

Requirement: Partnership Agreement (Anggaran Dasar) registration
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 pasal 7 tentang PT Pembentukan dan pasal 32 tentang kemitraan
Process: Register with Ministry of Law and Human Rights (Kemenkumham) via OSS system, obtain NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)
Cost: Rp 1-2 million (registration fees, notary)
Timeline: 7-14 working days
Authority: Kemenkumham online portal (oss.go.id)
Link: https://oss.go.id/
Relevance: Foundation for formal partnership structure

Requirement: Tax registration as partnership entity
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan, Pasal 26 tentang kemitraan
Process: Register NPWP for partnership at KPP (Kantor Pajak Pratama), choose between CV or PT structure
Cost: Free initial registration, annual compliance fees Rp 1-2 million
Timeline: 3-7 working days approval
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (DJP)
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/
Relevance: Critical for revenue sharing and tax optimization

Requirement: Intellectual Property ownership documentation
Regulation: UU No. 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta, specifically pasal 28 tentang hak paten dan merek
Process: Register IP claims in partnership agreement, optionally at DGIP (Direktorat Jenderal Kekayaan Intelektual)
Cost: Rp 500k-2 million per IP type
Timeline: Partnership agreement immediate, government registration 30-60 days
Authority: Kemenkumham DGIP
Link: https://dgip.kemenkumham.go.id/
Relevance: Prevents disputes in client ownership and deliverables

Requirement: Professional liability insurance
Regulation: Not mandatory but recommended under financial services best practices (OJK Circular No. 55/POJK.05/2019 for consulting partnerships)
Process: Apply through AXA/Mandiri Insurance with partnership coverage
Cost: Rp 5-15 million annual premium
Timeline: Policy issuance 7-14 days
Authority: OJK licensed insurers
Link: https://www.ojk.go.id/
Relevance: Protects against quality control failures and client disputes
