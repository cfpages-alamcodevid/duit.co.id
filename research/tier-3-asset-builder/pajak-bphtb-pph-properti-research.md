### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: BPHTB payment before AJB signing.
Regulation: UU 28/2009, Perda regional.
Process: Calculate via e-BPHTB, pay to local treasury, validate.
Cost: 5% of taxable value.
Timeline: Must be paid before PPJB/AJB.
Authority: Bapenda local.
Link: https://www.djp.go.id/

Requirement: PPh final payment on gross value.
Regulation: PP 34/2016.
Process: Submit SSP to KPP, validate before AJB.
Cost: 2.5% of transaction value.
Timeline: Before AJB.
Authority: DJP.
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

Requirement: Notary deed registration.
Regulation: UU 30/2004.
Process: PPAT creates AJB, registers at BPN.
Cost: 0.5-1% of value.
Timeline: Within 30 days.
Authority: BPN.
Link: https://bpn.go.id/

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: PPh is calculated on profit, not gross value.
Reality: It's final tax on gross transaction value.
Why it matters: Misunderstanding leads to underestimation.
Source: DJP regulations.

Myth: BPHTB can be paid after AJB.
Reality: Must be paid before to avoid delays.
Why it matters: Causes transaction failures.
Source: Property transaction cases.

Myth: No taxes when buying from family.
Reality: Full taxes apply unless inheritance/hibah.
Why it matters: Legal non-compliance risks.
Source: UU 28/2009.
