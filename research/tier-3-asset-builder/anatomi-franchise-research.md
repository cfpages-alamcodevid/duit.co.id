### 4. Market Research
**The "Healthy" Pillars:**
1.  **Proven Track Record:** At least 3-5 years of operation and 10+ stable outlets.
2.  **Unit Economics:** Gross Margin >60% (for F&B) or >40% (for Services). Net Margin 15-25%.
3.  **Support System:** Robust supply chain, centralized marketing, and regular staff training.
4.  **IP Protection:** Brand registered at HAKI (DJKI) and official STPW (Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba).

**Winning Sectors 2026:**
*   **Daily Services:** Laundry (Self-service), Pet Care, Maintenance (AC/Deep Cleaning).
*   **Niche F&B:** Healthy food, specialty coffee (not just sugar-based), and local heritage food with modern branding.
*   **Education/Skill:** Coding for kids, specialized adult vocational training.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** STPW (Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba).
*   **Regulation:** PP No. 42 Tahun 2007 tentang Waralaba.
*   **Process:** Franchisor must provide a "Prospektus Penawaran Waralaba" to the franchisee at least 2 weeks before signing.
*   **DJKI Check:** Always check the brand's trademark status to avoid legal disputes.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Quote:** "Don't buy a franchise because you like the product. Buy it because you like the system. If there is no manual for every tiny detail, it's not a franchise; it's just a partnership." - Franchise Consultant.
*   **Case Study:** "Mixue vs. Local Boba Trend." Mixue succeeded because of its massive supply chain and low pricing power, while local boba trends failed due to lack of operational consistency and high ingredient costs.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "Viral Trap" Scenario:**
*   Brand "Es Krim Pelangi" is viral on TikTok.
*   Franchise fee: Rp 150jt.
*   Reality: No centralized supply chain (buy ingredients locally), no SOP for staff, and the brand is not registered.
*   Result: 6 months later, the trend dies, and the investor is left with expensive equipment and no support.

**The "Healthy" Scenario:**
*   Brand "Laundry Clean" (5 years exp, 50 outlets).
*   Franchise fee: Rp 250jt.
*   System: Centralized app for tracking, machine maintenance included, and brand is well-known.
*   Result: Slow but steady 20% ROI annually.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Franchise itu pasti sukses karena sudah ada nama."
*   **Reality:** Location and local management still determine 50% of success.
*   **Myth:** "Cukup bayar, semua diurus pusat."
*   **Reality:** Even in "Autopilot" models, you must audit the reports and visit the site.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Request the Prospectus.** Look for the list of current franchisees and call them.
2.  **Step 2: Visit the "Weakest" Outlet.** Don't just visit the flagship. See how the worst-performing outlet is managed.
3.  **Step 3: Financial Audit.** Ask for 2 years of P&L from 3 different locations.
4.  **Step 4: Legal Check.** Verify STPW and HAKI status.
5.  **Step 5: Negotiations.** Check for territorial protection (radius) so another outlet doesn't open right next to you.
