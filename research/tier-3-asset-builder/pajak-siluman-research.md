### 4. Market Research
**Inflation Hedges Performance 2025-2026:**
*   **Gold (Emas):** Traditional hedge, yields 0% but preserves value. Average return 2024-2025 ~15-20% due to global uncertainty.
*   **Stocks (Saham):** Companies with pricing power (Consumer Goods/Banks) can pass inflation to customers.
*   **Property:** Rents usually rise with inflation, plus capital appreciation.
*   **Bitcoin/Crypto:** Increasingly viewed as "Digital Gold" by Tier 3 investors.

**The "Hidden Tax" Calculation:**
*   Saving Rp 1 Billion in 0.5% interest bank account.
*   Inflation 3.5%.
*   Real purchasing power loss: ~3% or Rp 30 Million per year.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

*   **Taxation:** PPh Final 20% on bank interest makes saving even less attractive compared to 10% dividend tax or 0.1% stock sale tax.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Uang di bank itu aman."
*   **Reality:** It's safe from theft, but not from value erosion. It's "guaranteed to lose" purchasing power.
*   **Myth:** "Inflasi itu sama untuk semua orang."
*   **Reality:** Personal inflation rates vary. If you buy luxury goods or travel, your inflation is likely higher than the CPI.
