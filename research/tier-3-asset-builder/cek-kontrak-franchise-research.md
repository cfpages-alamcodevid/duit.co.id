## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Obtain Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba (STPW) before operating.  
**Regulation:** PP No. 35 Tahun 2024 tentang Waralaba.  
**Process:** Register prospectus and agreement via OSS system.  
**Cost:** Rp1-5M (registration fees).  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Trade.  
**Link:** https://peraturan.go.id/id/pp-no-35-tahun-2024

**Process:** Include mandatory clauses (hak/kewajiban, IP, termination).

## 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies

**Case Study:** PT Mysalon International vs Ratnasari Lukitaningrum (2020 Supreme Court).  
**Background:** Franchisee sued franchisor for unilateral termination.  
**Result:** Court ruled franchisor liable for compensation.  
**Key learning:** Contracts must specify termination grounds clearly.  
**Source:** Supreme Court decision 1064 K/Pdt/2020.  

**Case Study:** Geprek Bensu trademark dispute (2019 Commercial Court).  
**Background:** Franchisee Ruben Onsu sued by Benny Sujono over "Bensu" use.  
**Result:** Court awarded damages for IP violation.  
**Key learning:** Verify IP rights before signing.  
**Source:** PN Jakarta Pusat decision.  

**Expert Opinion:** "Contracts often favor franchisors; always negotiate compensation clauses." - Arif Gaffar, Gaffar & Co. Law.

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** Franchise contracts are standard and fair.  
**Reality:** Often imbalanced, with franchisors holding power.  
**Why it matters:** Leads to disputes and losses.  
**Source:** BPS 2024 report on business conflicts.  

**Myth:** Foreign franchises are risk-free.  
**Reality:** Must comply with local content rules.  
**Why it matters:** Regulatory fines.  
**Source:** GR 35/2024.
