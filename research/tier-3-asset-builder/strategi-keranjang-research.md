### 4. Market Research
**The Three Buckets Model:**
1.  **Bucket 1 (Liquidity & Peace of Mind):**
    *   Goal: Cash for 1-2 years of lifestyle.
    *   Instruments: Cash, RDPU, Gold (partial).
    *   Risk: Minimal.
2.  **Bucket 2 (Income & Stability):**
    *   Goal: Generate steady cash flow.
    *   Instruments: SBN, FR (Fixed Rate bonds), Blue Chip Dividends, Rental Property.
    *   Risk: Low to Medium.
3.  **Bucket 3 (Growth & Legacy):**
    *   Goal: Wealth multiplication.
    *   Instruments: Growth Stocks, Private Equity/Small Business, Global ETFs, Crypto.
    *   Risk: High.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Persona: "The 2 Billion Portfolio"**
*   **Bucket 1 (Cash/RDPU):** Rp 300 Juta (Lifestyle Rp 25jt/mo x 12).
*   **Bucket 2 (SBN/Dividends):** Rp 1 Miliar (Yielding 6% = Rp 60jt/year).
*   **Bucket 3 (Stocks/Global):** Rp 700 Juta (Target 15% growth).
*   **Annual Rebalancing:** At the end of year, profits from Bucket 3 are "poured" back into Bucket 1 & 2 to maintain the levels.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Harus diversifikasi ke 20 jenis instrumen."
*   **Reality:** Over-diversification leads to mediocre returns. 3 Buckets focus on *purpose* rather than just number of assets.
*   **Myth:** "Bucket 1 itu buang-buang uang karena return rendah."
*   **Reality:** Bucket 1 is "Portfolio Insurance." It allows you to take bigger risks in Bucket 3.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Determine Monthly Burn Rate.** Know exactly how much you need for 12 months.
2.  **Step 2: Fill Bucket 1.** Priority number one.
3.  **Step 3: Build the Income Floor (Bucket 2).** Focus on SBN/FR for tax efficiency.
4.  **Step 4: Aggressive Growth (Bucket 3).** Use the remainder for high-conviction plays.
5.  **Step 5: Annual Refill.** Move profits from growth to security.
