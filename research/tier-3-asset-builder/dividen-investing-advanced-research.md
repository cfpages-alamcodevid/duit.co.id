## Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: High dividend yields always mean good investments.
Reality: High yields can indicate "dividend trap" where falling stock prices artificially inflate yield.
Why it matters: Investors chase yield without checking fundamentals, leading to capital losses.
Source: Historical analysis of Indonesian dividend traps (e.g., some mining stocks during downturns).

Myth: Dividends are guaranteed forever.
Reality: Companies can cut or suspend dividends during financial distress.
Why it matters: Over-reliance on dividends without diversification leads to income shock.
Source: COVID-19 dividend cuts in 2020.

Myth: Global dividends are too complicated for Indonesian investors.
Reality: Via ETFs and proper tax treaties, global dividends are accessible and tax-efficient.
Why it matters: Limits diversification opportunities.
Source: DJP tax treaty guidelines.
