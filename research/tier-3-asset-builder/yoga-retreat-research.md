## 6. Case Studies

### Case Study 1: The Yoga Barn, Ubud
- **Founded:** 2007 by Charley Patton (co-founder)
- **Scale:** Largest yoga studio in Bali with 8 studios, 180+ classes/week
- **Facilities:** On-site accommodation (Nest + Guesthouse), Garden Kafe (organic cafe), Refresh Spa, Yoga Shop
- **Capacity:** Dozens of rooms through various accommodation tiers
- **Revenue Model:** Drop-in classes, class cards, retreat packages, teacher trainings, workshops, spa, F&B
- **Key Achievement:** Built a complete wellness ecosystem — guests can stay, eat, practice, and receive treatments without leaving the property
- **Lesson:** Diversifikasi revenue stream; jangan hanya bergantung pada kelas yoga

### Case Study 2: Fivelements Retreat, Mambal (Abiansemal, Bali)
- **Founded:** ~2010
- **Scale:** 19 rooms, award-winning eco-conscious wellness retreat
- **Location:** Tepi Sungai Ayung, di luar Ubud (lebih tenang)
- **Features:** Plant-based cuisine (Sakti Dining Room), Balinese healing, sacred arts
- **Price Point:** Premium/luxury — significantly higher than average Ubud rates
- **Awards:** Multiple international wellness awards
- **Lesson:** Differentiation melalui konsep eco-luxury + Balinese authenticity; bukan sekadar yoga

### Case Study 3: Floating Leaf Eco-Luxury Retreat, Sukawati (Sanur area)
- **Founded:** ~2015
- **Scale:** 14 rooms, "Best Retreat Center in Asia" multiple years
- **Features:** Two spas, organic permaculture gardens, yoga sanctuary with ocean + mountain views
- **Business Model:** Fokus pada retreat leader hosting — mereka menyediakan venue + support untuk retreat leader eksternal
- **Success Metric:** Retreat leaders report sold-out events, high repeat rate
- **Lesson:** Model "retreat center as venue" lebih scalable; Anda tidak harus mengisi sendiri setiap retreat

## 7. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### Business Licenses
| Requirement | Regulation | Process | Cost Est. | Timeline |
|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|----------|
| PT/CV Establishment | UU Cipta Kerja | Notaris, AHU | 5-15 jt | 2-4 minggu |
| IMB (Izin Mendirikan Bangunan) | UU Bangunan Gedung | Pemda setempat | 1-5% dari nilai bangunan | 2-6 bulan |
| SIUP / NIB | OSS RBA | Online via OSS | 0 (NIB gratis) | 1-3 hari |
| Tanda Daftar Usaha Pariwisata (TDUP) | UU Kepariwisataan | Dinas Pariwisata | 500K-2 jt | 1-2 bulan |
| Izin Usaha Akomodasi | Permenparekraf | Dinas Pariwisata | 1-5 jt | 1-3 bulan |
| Izin Restoran | Pemda | Dinas terkait | 500K-2 jt | 1-2 bulan |
| Izin Spa/Kesehatan Tradisional | Dinas Kesehatan | Puskesmas/Dinkes | 500K-1 jt | 1-2 bulan |
| IMTA (untuk staff asing) | UU Ketenagakerjaan | Kemnaker | ~1.2 jt/ tahun | 2-4 minggu |

### Tax Obligations
- **PPh Badan:** 22% (tarif final untuk UMKM tertentu bisa 0.5%)
- **PPN:** 11% untuk jasa tertentu
- **PPh 21:** Karyawan
- **PPh 23:** Sewa, jasa tertentu
- **PBB:** Properti

### Foreign Ownership Notes
- **Tanah:** WNA tidak bisa memiliki tanah Hak Milik (SHM) di Indonesia
- **Hak Guna Bangunan (HGB):** WNA bisa melalui PT PMA, maksimal 30 tahun + perpanjangan
- **Rekomendasi:** Untuk investor WNA, struktur PT PMA dengan nominee arrangement yang legal, atau fokus pada operasional
- **Investor WNI:** Jauh lebih mudah; SHM untuk tanah, HGB untuk bangunan

### Investment Thesis
- **Tanah sebagai hedge:** Inflasi Indonesia 3-5%/tahun, properti di destinasi wisata premium mengalahkan inflasi
- **Bangunan sebagai income-producing asset:** Retreat center menghasilkan 15-30% ROI operasional + apresiasi tanah 10-15% = total return 25-45%/tahun
- **Diversifikasi:** Bukan all-in di satu properti; retreat center bisa jadi bagian dari portfolio properti yang lebih luas

## 11. Competition & Market Saturation Analysis

### Ubud — Saturated but Premium
- **Reality:** Ubud sudah sangat padat dengan retreat center. Lebih dari 50 retreat centers terdaftar di BaliSpirit
- **Advantage:** Established wellness ecosystem, easier to attract guests
- **Disadvantage:** High land prices, traffic, competition, noise in central areas
- **Strategy for Ubud:** Go niche (luxury detox, corporate retreat, specialized YTT)
- **Harga tanah:** Rp 5-15 jt/m2 (pusat), Rp 3-8 jt/m2 (pinggir)

### Tabanan — Emerging Opportunity
- **Reality:** Tabanan (15-30 menit dari Canggu/Ubud) masih banyak sawah
- **Advantage:** Land 40-60% cheaper, quieter, authentic Balinese village feel
- **Disadvantage:** Less infrastructure, longer guest travel time
- **Existing centers:** Prana Dewi, Sava Eco Retreat, Bali Silent Retreat, Bali Lush
- **Harga tanah:** Rp 800K-2.5 jt/m2

### Lombok — Blue Ocean (Still)
- **Reality:** Wellness tourism di Lombok masih sangat early stage
- **Advantage:** Very cheap land, beautiful beaches, Mandalika development
- **Disadvantage:** Limited talent pool, longer flight access, less awareness
- **Strategy:** First-mover advantage in Mandalika area; target domestic corporate retreat

### Yogyakarta — Culture + Wellness
- **Advantage:** Rich Javanese culture, cheap land, Borobudur proximity, large talent pool
- **Disadvantage:** Less recognition as wellness destination vs Bali
- **Strategy:** Combine yoga with Javanese meditation, jamu traditions, cultural immersion
- **Emerging area:** Kaliurang, Borobudur surrounding villages

### Key Competitive Advantage for New Entrants
1. **Corporate wellness niche** — Companies willing to pay premium for retreat sewa exclusive
2. **Detox/specialized programs** — Higher margins than general yoga
3. **Bali alternative locations** — Tabanan, Amed, North Bali offer better value
4. **Hybrid retreat + resort model** — Open to public + retreat groups

## 13. Action Steps Outline

### Step 1: Feasibility & Location Scouting
- **Riset lokasi:** Kunjungi 3-5 lokasi potensial (Tabanan, Ubud pinggir, Lombok)
- **Cek akses:** Jarak dari bandara, kondisi jalan, ketersediaan air bersih
- **Cek zoning:** Pastikan tanah di zonasi pariwisata/perdagangan (bukan sawah lindung)
- **Contoh:** Bandingkan harga tanah di Desa Kerta (Payangan) vs Desa Sibang Kaja (Abiansemal)

### Step 2: Legal & Business Structure
- **Pendirian PT:** Untuk operasional & perizinan
- **SHM/HGB:** Pastikan status tanah jelas
- **IMB:** Sesuaikan dengan desain bangunan
- **NIB + TDUP:** Dapatkan dari OSS RBA
- **Warning:** Jangan mulai bangun sebelum IMB selesai — risiko pembongkaran tinggi di Bali

### Step 3: Design & Construction
- **Arsitek spesialis retreat center:** Cari yang pengalaman dengan eco-bamboo structure
- **Design principles:** Open air, natural materials, seamless indoor-outdoor
- **Yoga shala:** Orientasi timur untuk morning practice, atap tinggi, ventilasi alami
- **Villa:** Minimalis namun nyaman; AC, bathroom en-suite, kualitas tempat tidur tinggi

### Step 4: Program Development & Pricing
- **Develop 3 signature retreat packages:** 3-day, 5-day, 7-day
- **Tentukan pricing:** Benchmark dengan The Yoga Barn (1-day 1.1M, 3-day 2.25M, 7-day 9.375M)
- **Recruitment:** Cari 2-3 yoga teacher inti + guest teacher network
- **Sertifikasi:** Yoga Alliance registration untuk teacher training program

### Step 5: Marketing & Booking Setup
- **Website:** Booking engine integrated (e.g., Bedful, Little Hotelier)
- **Channels:** Booking.com, Expedia (untuk akomodasi), Retreat booking platforms
- **Social media:** Instagram-heavy, behind-the-scenes content, teacher highlights
- **Partnership:** Corporate HR departments, travel agents, wellness influencers

### Step 6: Soft Opening & Operations
- **Trial operations:** Undang 2-3 influencer/media untuk complimentary stay
- **Feedback loop:** Perfect SOP for 3 months before full launch
- **Staff training:** Hospitality standard for all non-teaching staff
