## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** STPW (Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba) for franchise operations.  
**Regulation:** PP 35/2024 tentang Waralaba.  
**Process:** Apply via OSS system with prospectus and agreement; no validity limit.  
**Cost:** Administrative fees Rp 500K - Rp 2M.  
**Timeline:** 7-14 days.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Trade.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id  

**Requirement:** NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha) for business registration.  
**Regulation:** UU Cipta Kerja (2020).  
**Process:** Online application via OSS based on risk level.  
**Cost:** Free for low-risk, Rp 50K for medium.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 days.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Investment.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id  

**Requirement:** IMB/PBG for property development/modification.  
**Regulation:** UU 28/2002 tentang Bangunan Gedung.  
**Process:** Submit plans to local DPMPTSP; inspection required.  
**Cost:** Rp 5M - Rp 20M.  
**Timeline:** 14-30 days.  
**Authority:** Local government (DPMPTSP).  
**Link:** Local building permits portal.  

**Requirement:** Legal entity for asset holding (PT recommended for property protection).  
**Regulation:** UU 40/2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas.  
**Process:** Notary establishment, NPWP, NIB.  
**Cost:** Rp 10M - Rp 20M (including notary fees).  
**Timeline:** 7-14 days.  
**Authority:** Ministry of Law and Human Rights.  
**Link:** https://kemenkumham.go.id

## 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** Combining property and franchise is too complex legally.  
**Reality:** With proper entity (PT) and OSS-based registrations, it's streamlined under UU Cipta Kerja.  
**Why it matters:** Simplifies compliance for asset builders.  
**Source:** PP 35/2024 and UU Cipta Kerja.  

**Myth:** Property ownership in franchise increases tax burden significantly.  
**Reality:** PPh final rates and BPHTB exemptions can optimize; PT structure allows deductions.  
**Why it matters:** Misunderstanding leads to missed optimization opportunities.  
**Source:** DJP guidelines and tax consultant articles.  

**Myth:** Franchise agreements don't protect property-integrated businesses.  
**Reality:** PP 35/2024 requires clauses for asset protection and termination compensation.  
**Why it matters:** Ensures business continuity for investors.  
**Source:** PP 35/2024 clauses.
