### 4. Market Research
**The OPM Hierarchy:**
1.  **Level 1: Low-Interest Debt (KUR/KPR Promo):** Best for building initial assets.
2.  **Level 2: Private Lending / Angel Investors:** For scaling businesses with high growth potential.
3.  **Level 3: Strategic Partnerships / Equity:** No interest, but you give away ownership.

**Hutang Baik (Good Debt) vs. Hutang Buruk (Bad Debt):**
*   **Hutang Baik:** Interest < ROI of Asset. (e.g., KPR for a high-yield boarding house).
*   **Hutang Buruk:** Interest > ROI (or no ROI). (e.g., Credit card for lifestyle/clothes).

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Collateral:** SHM (Property), BPKB (Vehicle), or Cash Collateral (Deposit).
*   **SLIK OJK:** Maintaining a "Kol 1" (current) status is essential for Tier 3 investors to keep accessing OPM.
*   **Legal:** SPK (Surat Perjanjian Kredit) review. Watch out for "Personal Guarantee" clauses in business loans.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Quote:** "Leverage is a double-edged sword. It makes a good deal great, but it makes a bad deal a disaster." - Finance Professor.
*   **Case Study:** "Pak Roy, Entrepreneur." Borrowed 1B from bank at 10% (KMK). Used it to buy stock for his wholesale business which has a 30% margin and 4x turnover. The 1B generated 1.2B in profit. After interest (100jt), he made 1.1B profit using "Other People's Money".

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "KPR Arbitrage" Scenario:**
*   Buy House: 1B.
*   KPR Interest: 8% (Rp 80jt/year).
*   Convert to Kos-kosan: Yield 12% (Rp 120jt/year).
*   Capital Appreciation: 5% (Rp 50jt/year).
*   **Total ROI:** 17%.
*   **Spread:** 17% - 8% = 9% Profit on money you didn't even own.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Hutang itu haram/jahat."
*   **Reality:** Debt is a tool. Like a hammer, it can build a house or break a thumb. Successful "Asset Builders" use debt to acquire assets.
*   **Myth:** "Harus lunas hutang dulu baru bisa investasi."
*   **Reality:** If you can borrow at 9% and earn 15%, paying off the debt is actually a "loss" of the 6% spread.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Fix Your SLIK.** Ensure no late payments.
2.  **Step 2: Calculate Your "Cost of Capital".** (Interest rate + fees).
3.  **Step 3: Identify High-Yield Assets.** (ROI must be at least 3-5% higher than interest).
4.  **Step 4: Use "Cheap" Debt First.** (KUR, KPR Promo, Fixed Rate Bonds).
5.  **Step 5: Have a Backup Plan.** Ensure cash flow from other sources can cover the loan if the asset fails temporarily.
