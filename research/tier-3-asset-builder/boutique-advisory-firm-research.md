### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
Requirement: Register as CV or PT for business entity
Regulation: UU No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan Terbatas, UU No. 25 Tahun 1992 tentang Perkoperasian (for CV)
Process: Create akta at notaris, register via OSS for NIB, get NPWP
Cost: Rp 3-5 juta for setup
Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Authority: Kemenkumham, OSS
Link: https://oss.go.id/

Requirement: Tax registration and compliance
Regulation: UU No. 36 Tahun 2008 tentang Pajak Penghasilan
Process: Register NPWP, file taxes quarterly/annually
Cost: Free for NPWP, annual tax fees vary
Timeline: Immediate after entity registration
Authority: Direktorat Jenderal Pajak
Link: https://www.pajak.go.id/

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
Myth: Butuh modal besar (Rp 100 juta+) untuk mulai boutique advisory firm
Reality: Bisa mulai dengan Rp 10-20 juta, fokus digital marketing dan networking
Why it matters: Membuat calon founders ragu, padahal barrier masuk rendah
Source: Mordor Intelligence market data, startup costs analysis

Myth: Boutique firm kalah saing dengan big consulting seperti Deloitte
Reality: Boutique unggul di niche spesifik, personalized service, lebih agile
Why it matters: Membuat founders overinvest di branding besar, padahal differentiation via expertise
Source: Competitor analysis from Talu, Astha websites

Myth: Sulit dapat klien pertama tanpa track record
Reality: Mulai dari jaringan kampus, LinkedIn, webinar gratis, case studies kecil
Why it matters: Founders menunda launch, padahal client acquisition bisa organik
Source: Monica & Co. case studies

### 9. Action Steps Outline
Step 1: Assess expertise and identify niche
- Details: Map keahlian kampus, survey demand via LinkedIn/komunitas
- Example: Jika ahli finance, fokus corporate advisory untuk SMEs
- Warning: Jangan overgeneralize, spesialisasi penting

Step 2: Legal and operational setup
- Details: Register CV/PT, setup NPWP, buka rekening bisnis
- Example: Gunakan OSS online untuk NIB dalam 1 hari
- Warning: Pastikan compliance pajak dari awal

Step 3: Build team and infrastructure
- Details: Hire 1-2 junior advisors, setup office virtual
- Example: Rekrut mahasiswa akhir sebagai asisten
- Warning: Mulai kecil, scale berdasarkan revenue

Step 4: Marketing and client acquisition
- Details: Website, LinkedIn, networking events, free webinars
- Example: Tawarkan konsultasi gratis untuk testimonials
- Warning: Fokus quality leads, bukan quantity

Step 5: Deliver services and scale
- Details: Standardize processes, track metrics, expand services
- Example: Dari 1-on-1 ke group workshops
- Warning: Maintain work-life balance, hindari burnout
