### 4. Market Research
**Valuation Model: The Multiplier Method**
*   **Value = Adjusted EBITDA x Multiplier**
*   **Adjusted EBITDA:** Net Profit + Interest + Taxes + Depreciation + Amortization + *Owner's Discretionary Earnings* (lifestyle costs hidden in the books).
*   **Multiplier Factors:**
    *   3x: High owner dependency, poor systems.
    *   4x-5x: Stable, good staff, decent brand.
    *   6x+: Market leader, tech-enabled, fully autopilot.

**Where to Find Deals:**
*   **Direct Outreach:** Contacting businesses that look well-run but the owner seems tired.
*   **Brokers:** Local business brokers or property agents who handle "over-kontrak".
*   **Forums:** Facebook Groups ("Jual Beli Bisnis"), OLX, or specialized platforms like *StartFranchise*.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Asset Purchase vs. Share Purchase:**
    *   **Asset Purchase:** Buying the equipment, brand, and lease. (Safer, no hidden debts).
    *   **Share Purchase:** Buying the PT (Legal Entity). (Easier for permits, but risk of hidden liabilities/taxes).
*   **Contracts:** Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA), Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), and Non-Compete Clause (essential to stop the old owner from opening a new shop next door).
*   **Labor:** Handling employee transitions under UU Cipta Kerja (Masa kerja/pesangon issues).

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Quote:** "You are not buying the past; you are buying the future cash flow. If the owner is the main attraction, you are buying a job, not a business." - M&A Advisor.
*   **Case Study:** "Dina, Investor." Bought a local beauty salon for Rp 400jt (3x EBITDA). She spent Rp 50jt on digital marketing (which the old owner never did). Within 1 year, profit doubled. Effective purchase price: 1.5x EBITDA.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Normalizing the P&L (The "Clean up"):**
*   Owner's Report shows 10jt profit.
*   But owner paid for his car, his family's meals, and his personal electricity from the business account (total 15jt).
*   **True EBITDA:** 10jt + 15jt = 25jt.
*   **Valuation (4x):** 25jt x 4 = 100jt (Fair Price).

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Harus punya uang tunai milyaran."
*   **Reality:** Many deals use "Seller Financing" or "Earn-outs" (pay 70% now, 30% from future profits).
*   **Myth:** "Beli bisnis yang rugi biar murah."
*   **Reality:** Fixing a broken business is 10x harder than scaling a healthy one. Buy "Healthy but Under-managed".
