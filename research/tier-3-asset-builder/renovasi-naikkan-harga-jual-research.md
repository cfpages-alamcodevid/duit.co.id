## Legal & Regulatory Requirements

Requirement: Building permit (IMB) for major renovations
Regulation: UU No. 28 Tahun 2002 tentang Bangunan Gedung
Process: Apply through local DPMPTSP with architectural drawings
Cost: Rp 500k-2 million depending on complexity
Timeline: 7-14 days
Authority: Local government (DPMPTSP)
Link: https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/58779

Requirement: Zoning compliance check
Regulation: Peraturan Daerah tentang RTRW (Rencana Tata Ruang Wilayah)
Process: Consult local planning department before renovation
Cost: Free consultation, paid verification Rp 1-5 million
Timeline: 3-7 days
Authority: Bappeda local
Link: Check local Bappeda website

Requirement: Environmental impact assessment for large renovations
Regulation: PP No. 27 Tahun 2012 tentang AMDAL
Process: Required if renovation >500m² or in protected areas
Cost: Rp 50-200 million for assessment
Timeline: 30-90 days
Authority: KLHK (Ministry of Environment)
Link: https://ppid.menlhk.go.id/

Requirement: Contractor licensing verification
Regulation: UU No. 2 Tahun 2017 tentang Jasa Konstruksi
Process: Verify SIUJK certificate
Cost: N/A (verification only)
Timeline: Immediate online check
Authority: PUPR Ministry
Link: https://sijaka.pu.go.id/
