## Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** PT PMA untuk foreign ownership properti  
**Regulation:** Law No. 40/2007 jo UU No. 6/2023 (Cipta Kerja)  
**Process:** Daftar OSS-RBA, akta notaris, pengesahan Kemenkumham dalam 14 hari.  
**Cost:** IDR 10-20 juta.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 bulan.  
**Authority:** BKPM & Kemenkumham.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id  

**Requirement:** Holding company untuk ring-fencing  
**Regulation:** UU PT, tidak spesifik tapi praktik umum.  
**Process:** Dirikan PT induk, transfer saham PT operasional.  
**Cost:** IDR 5-10 juta per PT.  
**Timeline:** 2-4 bulan.  
**Authority:** Kemenkumham.  
**Link:** https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Details/46102  

**Requirement:** Tax clearance untuk transfer aset  
**Regulation:** MoF Reg 81/2024  
**Process:** Apply business purpose test ke DGT.  
**Cost:** Gratis, tapi audit biaya.  
**Timeline:** 30-60 hari.  
**Authority:** Direktorat Jenderal Pajak.  
**Link:** https://www.pwc.com/id/en/pocket-tax-book/english/pocket-tax-book-2026.pdf  

## Expert Opinions & Case Studies

**Case Study:** PT Lippo Karawaci Tbk merger ke healthcare  
**Background:** Developer properti terbesar Indonesia, market cap IDR 30 triliun.  
**Starting point:** Fokus properti volatile, risiko 2020 COVID.  
**Result:** Acquire Siloam Hospitals, diversify 25% revenue ke healthcare, EVA positive.  
**Timeline:** 2013-2025, 12 tahun.  
**Key learning:** Ring-fencing via subsidiary PT untuk protect core assets.  
**Source:** Harvard case study "PT Lippo Karawaci Value Creation"  

**Case Study:** PT Bank Syariah Indonesia merger  
**Background:** 3 bank syariah BUMN, merger selama COVID untuk efisiensi.  
**Starting point:** Market share kecil vs konvensional.  
**Result:** Bank terbesar syariah Indonesia, asset IDR 300 triliun.  
**Timeline:** 2020-2023.  
**Key learning:** Asset transfer book value, tax neutral, protect dari risiko individual bank.  
**Source:** Conventus Law "Indonesia Transfer Assets Book Value"  

**Case Study:** Postnuptial agreement PT PMA shares  
**Background:** Investor asing properti Bali, marriage breakdown.  
**Starting point:** Shares di PT PMA tanpa prenup.  
**Result:** Court order compensation IDR 5 miliar, bukan transfer shares (sectoral limit).  
**Timeline:** 2024-2026 litigation.  
**Key learning:** Separate PT untuk bisnis properti sebelum marriage/divorce.  
**Source:** Jarji Zaidan Partners case law

## Common Myths & Misconceptions

**Myth:** CV lebih murah dari PT untuk properti bisnis.  
**Reality:** CV unlimited liability, no capital min, tapi foreign restricted & no asset protection.  
**Why it matters:** Risiko personal bankruptcy jika bisnis gagal.  
**Source:** Law No. 40/2007.  

**Myth:** UD cukup untuk small properti investment.  
**Reality:** UD unlimited liability, simple setup, tapi no corporate veil & tax disadvantages.  
**Source:** UU Perdagangan.  

**Myth:** Transfer aset ke PT tax-free.  
**Reality:** Market value jika penjualan, book value jika reorganisasi dengan approval.  
**Why it matters:** Tax bill besar jika salah struktur.  
**Source:** MoF Reg 81/2024.

- Warning: Penalty jika miss report.
