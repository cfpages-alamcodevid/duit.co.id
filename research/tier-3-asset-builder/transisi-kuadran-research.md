### 4. Market Research
**Transition Paths:**
1.  **S to B to I:** Build a system (Business) first, then invest profits.
2.  **S to I (Direct):** High-income professionals investing directly into paper assets/property.

**Investment Landscape for Tier 3:**
*   Min. Capital for "I" status: Rp 1 - 5 Miliar (Liquid assets to generate meaningful cash flow).
*   Instruments: Dividen Saham Blue Chip, SBN (ORI/SR), Properti Sewaan, Angel Investing in local startups.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Taxation:**
    *   S-Quadrant: PPh 21 (Progressive up to 35%) or PPh Final 0.5% (for UMKM <4.8M).
    *   I-Quadrant: PPh Final Dividen 10% (or 0% with reinvestment), PPh Final Saham 0.1% (Sales).
*   **Core Tax System (2025):** Automated monitoring by DJP makes transparent reporting mandatory for high-income earners.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Case Study:** "Dr. Herman, 45, Surgeon." Income 200jt/month. 15 years as 'S'. Transitioned by investing 50% of income into Ruko and Dividen Saham. By 2026, passive income covers 100% of lifestyle costs.
*   **Expert Insight:** "The biggest hurdle isn't the money, it's the 'Expert Syndrome'. Professionals in S-quadrant find it hard to trust systems or other people's management." - Financial Mentor.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Persona: "Sari, 38, Independent Consultant"**
*   Situation: Earns 1M/year but works 60 hours/week.
*   Transition Plan:
    *   Year 1-2: Shift to 'B' by hiring a junior consultant and automating lead gen (Systematization).
    *   Year 3: Allocate 'B' profits to 'I' (Dividen Stocks).
    *   Result: Work reduced to 10 hours/week; income remains stable from dividends + business profits.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Jadi investor itu pasif total."
*   **Reality:** Active management is required during the transition, especially in due diligence.
*   **Myth:** "Harus punya modal milyaran dulu baru bisa jadi I."
*   **Reality:** You start being an 'I' the moment you prioritize ROI over hourly rates.
