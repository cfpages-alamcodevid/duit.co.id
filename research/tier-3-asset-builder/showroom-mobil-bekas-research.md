- Buy trade-in at 5-10% below market

#### F. After-Sales & Maintenance

- Periodic maintenance packages (3-6 month intervals)
- Yearly coating maintenance
- Referral to partner workshops

## 6. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### Business Licenses

**Requirement:** NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha) via OSS-RBA
**Regulation:** PP No. 5/2021 (Permendag 47/2021)
**Process:**
1. Daftar akun OSS (oss.go.id)
2. Isi data usaha (KBLI 45101 - Perdagangan Mobil Bekas)
3. TTD elektronik
4. NIB + sertifikat standar terbit otomatis
**KBLI Codes Needed:**
- 45101: Perdagangan Besar Mobil Bekas
- 45201: Reparasi dan Perawatan Mobil (untuk bengkel/detailing)
- 47797: Perdagangan Eceran Bahan Kimia (jika jual chemical detailing)
**Cost:** Rp 0 (gratis via OSS)
**Timeline:** 1-3 hari kerja
**Authority:** BKPM/DPMPTSP daerah setempat

**Requirement:** Izin Lokasi / PBG (Persetujuan Bangunan Gedung)
**Regulation:** UU Cipta Kerja, PP No. 16/2021
**Process:** Perizinan bangunan untuk showroom (khusus jika bangun baru atau renovasi struktur)
**Cost:** Variable (retribusi daerah, konsultan ~Rp 5-15 jt)
**Timeline:** 1-3 bulan
**Authority:** Dinas PUPR / DPMPTSP

**Requirement:** Tanda Daftar Perusahaan (TDP) — sudah terintegrasi dengan NIB
**Regulation:** UU No. 3/1982 tentang Wajib Daftar Perusahaan

**Requirement:** NPWP Perusahaan
**Regulation:** UU PPh
**Process:** Daftar online via ereg.pajak.go.id

### Tax Obligations

| Tax Type | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| PPh Final UMKM (PP 55/2022) | 0,5% dari omzet | Untuk omzet < Rp 4,8 M/tahun, 4 tahun pertama |
| PPh Badan (UU HPP) | 22% | Jika omzet > Rp 4,8 M/tahun |
| PPN | 12% (2025-2026) | Per 2026, PPN 12% untuk pengusaha kena pajak |
| PPh 22 (Penjualan Mobil) | 0,45% x DPP (harga jual) × 20 unit/tahun threshold | PNBP Balai Lelang jika via lelang |
| PPh Final Pasal 4(2) Pengalihan | 1-2,5% | Jika transaksi properti showroom |
| Pajak Bumi dan Bangunan (PBB) | 0,5% x NJKP x NJOP | Untuk properti showroom milik sendiri |

**Important:** Showroom mobil bekas adalah PKP (Pengusaha Kena Pajak) jika omzet > Rp 4,8 M/tahun. Wajib memungut PPN 12% atas penjualan mobil.

### Consumer Protection

**Regulation:** UU No. 8/1999 tentang Perlindungan Konsumen
- Wajib memberikan informasi benar tentang kondisi mobil
- Dilarang melakukan manipulasi odometer
- Wajib memberikan garansi (minimal standar showroom) — mesin & transmisi 3-6 bulan adalah standar industri
- Jika menjual mobil ex-tabrak/banjir, wajib diungkapkan

### Odometer Fraud Risks (Pasal 378 KUHP)

Penipuan pemutaran odometer dapat dikenakan:
- Pasal 378 KUHP: Ancaman pidana 4 tahun penjara
- UU No. 8/1999: Sanksi administratif dan pidana
- Risiko reputasi: Bisa menghancurkan bisnis jika terbongkar

### BPKB/STNK Requirements

- Periksa keaslian BPKB ke Samsat/Samsat keliling
- Cek BBN KB (Bea Balik Nama) belum dibayar
- Pastikan STNK tidak mati >2 tahun
- Pastikan tidak dalam status blokir leasing
- Gunakan aplikasi Signal/Crowd for car history check

## 7. Case Studies

### Case Study 1: Rapih Motor — MGK Kemayoran, Jakarta
**Background:** Salah satu showroom mobil bekas tertua dan terbesar di MGK Kemayoran, Jakarta Pusat
**Category:** General used cars (not exclusively premium, but carries many premium units)
**Starting point:** From small kiosk to large showroom complex in MGK
**Scale:** Dozens of units displayed across multiple floors
**Model:** Mixed inventory (regular + premium), no in-house detailing
**Revenue streams:** Car sales, trade-ins, financing commissions
**Key Learning:** MGK Kemayoran is the largest used car hub in Southeast Asia. Being in a cluster increases foot traffic but also competition. Premium cars (Alphard, BMW, Merc) are displayed on upper floors.
**Source:** Kompas.com (June 2025), direct observation

### Case Study 2: Concept Car — Alam Sutera, Tangerang
**Background:** Premium used car showroom targeting upper-middle segment in Alam Sutera, Tangerang
**Specialty:** Luxury brands — BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Lexus, Porsche
**Starting point:** Owner started as car enthusiast, built network through community
**Model:** Low-volume, high-margin (targets 1-3 premium cars/month)
**Differentiator:** Each car undergoes detailed inspection & reconditioning; offer 6-month engine/transmission warranty
**Revenue streams:** Car sales (70%), consignment (20%), detailing (10%)
**Key Learning:** Premium car buyers trust reputation over price. Brand positioning (showroom design, lighting, service) is critical. Each unit needs professional photos and detailed CarFax-like report.
**Source:** Industry observation

### Case Study 3: RB Garasi — Premium Pre-Owned Specialist (Jakarta)
**Background:** Specialist premium pre-owned car dealer in Jakarta
**Specialty:** Toyota Alphard, Lexus, BMW, Mercedes — models 3-5 years old
**Model:** Full service — sourcing, reconditioning, detailing, warranty
**Revenue Detail:**
- Buys via auction (BCA Finance, Adira) and direct trade-ins
- Reconditions thoroughly (mechanical + cosmetic)
- Detailing package (ceramic coating) included in price (or upsell)
- Margin per unit: Rp 50-150 jt depending on model
**Key Learning:** To succeed in premium: (1) Workshop-quality inspection, (2) Transparent history, (3) Factory-grade detailing, (4) After-sales service, (5) Financing partnerships
**Source:** Industry observation

### Case Study 4: Otopia Coating & Detailing
**Background:** Fastest-growing detailing chain in Indonesia — 90+ outlets nationally
**Category:** Detailing-only (not showroom), but relevant as detailing profit center
**Revenue model:** Coating packages Rp 2-13,75 jt, detailing Rp 1-10 jt
**Scale:** 90+ outlets across Indonesia (per 2026)
**Key Learning:** Detailing as a stand-alone business is scalable. For showroom, in-house detailing adds Rp 30-60 jt/month revenue and increases car desirability.
**Source:** Scuto.co.id, Otopiacoating.id

### Case Study 5: Hyundai Promise (OEM Certified Pre-Owned)
**Background:** Hyundai Indonesia's certified pre-owned program
**Category:** Manufacturer-backed used car program
**Model:** Curated, inspected, certified used cars with manufacturer warranty
**Key Learning:** OEM certification increases buyer confidence dramatically. Premium independent showrooms can adopt similar certification/warranty model.
**Source:** KatadataOTO (October 2025) — Quote from Wahyu Seto, GM Hyundai Solusi Mobilitas

- Unlike rental showrooms, owned showroom = asset that appreciates
- Kombinasi "tanah + bisnis" meningkatkan LTV (Loan to Value) di bank
- Some banks accept 50-65% LTV for commercial property + business cash flow

### Inventory (Cars) as Asset — Liquid but Depreciating

**Advantages:**
- Highly liquid compared to many business assets
- Can be converted to cash in 7-30 days (at discount)
- Can be used as collateral for inventory financing (though limited)

**Disadvantages:**
- Depreciates 5-15% per year (depends on model)
- Holding cost: 1-3% per month in lost value + storage
- Insurance cost (comprehensive) ~2,5-5% of car value/year
- Risk of price fluctuation (market sentiment, new model launches)

**Inventory Financing Options:**
| Option | Rate (p.a.) | LTV | Notes |
|--------|------------|-----|-------|
| Kredit Investasi (Bank) | 8-12% | 60-75% of property | For building, not cars |
| Kredit Modal Kerja (Bank) | 9-14% | 50-70% of inventory | Need track record |
| Pinjaman Leasing (Adira/BFIF) | 12-24% | 70-80% of car value | For retail financing, not showroom stock |
| Peer-to-Peer Lending (Modalmu, Koinworks) | 14-24% | Variable | For working capital |
| Self-Financed | 0% | 100% | Best but slowest growth |

**Key Rule:** Never finance 100% of inventory from debt. Maintain 30-50% equity position.

### Inspection Checklist (Pre-Sale)

1. **Engine & Transmission:**
   - Compression test
   - Leak-down test
   - Transmission shift quality
   - Check for oil leaks (engine, transmission, differential)
   - Coolant condition & level

2. **Body & Paint:**
   - Paint thickness gauge reading (all panels)
   - Check for accident damage (panel gaps, overspray, weld marks)
   - Check for flood damage (mud residue, rust in unusual places, electrical issues)
   - Check undercarriage condition

3. **Interior:**
   - Electronics (all buttons, infotainment, AC, seat adjustment)
   - Smell check (cigarette, mold, flood)
   - Upholstery condition (tears, stains, wear)
   - Odometer verification (compare with service records)

4. **Chassis & Suspension:**
   - Frame alignment check
   - Suspension bushing condition
   - Steering rack leaks
   - Brake pad/rotor thickness

5. **Documents:**
   - BPKB (verify physical vs. database)
   - STNK (check pajak status)
   - Service history book
   - Faktur (for newer cars)
   - Cek blokir leasing via aplikasi Samsat online

### Fraud Prevention SOP

| Red Flag | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Odometer doesn't match service records | Decline or adjust price Rp 20-50 jt |
| Paint thickness varies >100 microns across panels | Suspect accident repair; check frame |
| No service history | Discount 10-15% from market price |
| BPKB looks suspicious (font, watermark) | Verify at Samsat/Signal app |
| Seller wants cash only, no transfer | High risk; verify KTP/SKCK |
| Price too good (30%+ below market) | Almost certainly problematic |
| Multiple owners in short time | Check for title washing |
| Flood damage signs (mud, rust, musty smell) | Decline unless heavily discounted & disclosed |

## 10. Risk Management

### Inventory Holding Costs

| Cost Item | Monthly Cost per Car |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Financing cost (if loan, 12% p.a.) | Rp 3-10 jt (on Rp 300-1000 jt) |
| Insurance comprehensive | Rp 800rb-2,5 jt |
| Parking/storage in showroom | Rp 500rb-2 jt (allocated rent) |
| Opportunity cost of capital | Rp 3-8 jt (12% return foregone) |
| Depreciation | Rp 5-15 jt (5-15% annual / 12) |
| **Total Holding Cost** | **~Rp 12-38 jt per car per month** |

**Rule of Thumb:** Premium cars should turn in 30-60 days. Beyond 90 days, the margin is eroded by holding costs.

### Price Fluctuation Risk

**Mitigation:**
- Diversify inventory across brands (don't bet on 1 model)
- Monitor market trends (new model launches affect used prices)
- Set floor prices with automatic discount triggers
- Maintain cash reserve for market downturns
- Focus on models with proven resale value (Alphard, Land Cruiser, Porsche 911)

### Insurance

| Insurance Type | Coverage | Annual Premium (Estimate) |
|---------------|----------|--------------------------|
| Comprehensive Inventory | All cars in showroom (fire, theft, natural disaster) | 0,5-1% of total inventory value |
| Building & Property | Fire, earthquake, flood | 0,2-0,5% of property value |
| Public Liability | Customer injury on premises | Rp 5-15 jt |
| Business Interruption | Loss of income if disaster strikes | 0,5-1% of annual revenue |
| Employees (BPJS TK) | Mandatory work accident & death | 1-4% of salary (employer portion) |
| Test Drive Insurance | Accident during test drive | Included in comprehensive or separate ~Rp 3-8 jt/year |

**Recommended Partners:** Lippo Insurance, ACA Asuransi, Garda Oto (for cars), and conventional property insurers for building.

### Theft & Fraud Risk

- Install CCTV 24/7 (with cloud backup)
- Test drive policy: accompany with staff, hold ID and keys
- Key management: double-lock safe for BPKB and keys
- Background check on employees (especially those handling money)
- Bank transfer only for large transactions (no cash > Rp 100 jt)
- Set up PPAT/notaris for sales documents

### Market Downside Protection

- Keep 15-20% of inventory value as cash reserve
- Maintain relationships with 3+ auction houses for quick liquidation
- Have a "clearance price" for each car (cost - 10%)
- Cross-trade with other showrooms (buy-sell between dealers)
- Hold period alert: cars >60 days get aggressive marketing push

## 13. Common Myths & Misconceptions

### Myth 1: "Bisnis mobil bekas cuma cocok untuk yang paham mesin"
**Reality:** Untuk showroom premium, yang lebih penting adalah jaringan, reputasi, dan manajemen keuangan. Mekanik bisa di-outsource atau di-hire.
**Why it matters:** Banyak calon pengusaha berkualitas mundur karena merasa tidak punya latar belakang otomotif.

### Myth 2: "Margin showroom mobil bekas kecil, cuma 3-5%"
**Reality:** Untuk mobil biasa, ya. Tapi untuk premium, margin 5-15% adalah standar. Dengan harga Rp 500 jt-1 M per unit, margin absolutnya Rp 50-150 jt.
**Why it matters:** Premium segment justifies the higher setup cost.

### Myth 3: "Showroom mobil bekas butuh modal Rp 10 M+"
**Reality:** Modal Rp 500 jt-2 M cukup untuk skala kecil dengan strategi consignment-first. Beli properti memang butuh Rp 5 M+, tapi sewa bisa mulai Rp 80-150 jt/tahun.
**Why it matters:** Jangan takut memulai dengan skala yang tepat.

### Myth 4: "Detailer bisa digaji UMR, nggak perlu spesialis"
**Reality:** Premium car detailing membutuhkan certified detailers. Kesalahan coating bisa merusak cat mobil Rp 500 jt+. Detailer premium digaji Rp 6-12 jt/bulan di Jakarta.
**Why it matters:** Kualitas detailing adalah brand differentiator.

### Myth 5: "Mobil bekas premium nggak laku karena orang kaya beli baru"
**Reality:** Banyak eksekutif dan pengusaha membeli Alphard/BMW bekas 3-5 tahun. Harganya 40-60% dari baru, masih terawat, dan garansi bisa diperpanjang.
**Why it matters:** Premium used car segment is growing faster than budget segment.
