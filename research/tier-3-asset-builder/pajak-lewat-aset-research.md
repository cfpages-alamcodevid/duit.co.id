### 4. Market Research
**Tax Optimization Strategies:**
1.  **Penyusutan (Depreciation):** Buying equipment/vehicles for business use allows you to deduct the cost over several years, lowering taxable profit.
2.  **Property PPN DTP:** Leveraging government incentives for property purchases to save 5-11% on tax immediately.
3.  **Corporate Expenses vs. Personal:** Shifting personal costs (mobile, internet, car) to business expenses if used for income generation.
4.  **Dividen Tax Free:** Reinvesting dividends into other domestic assets (stocks/SBN) to pay 0% tax (UU HPP).

**High-Level Strategy:**
"Asset Rich, Cash Poor" (on paper). Reinvesting profits into assets (Capex) to reduce EBT (Earnings Before Tax).

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Regulation:** UU No. 7 Tahun 2021 (Harmonisasi Peraturan Perpajakan).
*   **Compliance:** All assets must be reported in the SPT (Annual Tax Return).
*   **GAAR (General Anti-Avoidance Rule):** Tax planning must have "Economic Substance". You can't just buy a yacht and call it a "business asset" if your business is a laundry.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Quote:** "Tax avoidance is not a crime; it's a right. The government provides incentives to encourage certain behaviors (like buying property or investing in R&D). It's your job to take them." - Tax Consultant.
*   **Case Study:** "CV Maju Jaya." Had 500jt profit. Tax would be ~110jt (Progressive). Instead, they bought a delivery van for 200jt. Depreciation Year 1: 50jt. New Taxable Profit: 450jt. They also utilized Super Tax Deduction for training. Effective tax dropped by 30%.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "Corporate Car" Math:**
*   Buy a car personally: Pay using "Post-Tax" income. No tax benefit.
*   Buy a car via PT (Business): Pay using "Pre-Tax" revenue. Deduct depreciation. Deduct fuel/maintenance.
*   **Result:** Personal wealth increases (via the company asset) while personal tax liability decreases.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Tax planning itu sama dengan pengemplangan pajak (Tax Evasion)."
*   **Reality:** Evasion is illegal (lying/hiding). Planning is legal (using the rules to your advantage).
*   **Myth:** "Hanya untuk perusahaan besar."
*   **Reality:** Individuals and UMKM can use depreciation and dividend reinvestment rules just as effectively.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Audit Your Current Tax Bracket.** Are you paying 30-35%?
2.  **Step 2: Review Your Capex Plan.** What assets does your business need in the next 2 years? (Buy now to lower this year's tax).
3.  **Step 3: Setup a PT/CV.** Shifting from individual to corporate can often open more deduction opportunities.
4.  **Step 4: Reinvest Dividends.** Don't withdraw cash; move it to Bucket 2 (SBN) or Bucket 3 (Stocks) to trigger the 0% tax rule.
5.  **Step 5: Consult a Pro.** A good tax lawyer/accountant pays for themselves in savings.
