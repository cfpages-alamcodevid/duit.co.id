### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Diversifikasi artinya beli banyak saham."
*   **Reality:** If all your stocks are in the same sector (e.g., all banks), you aren't diversified. You need different asset classes (Gold vs Stocks).
*   **Myth:** "Diversifikasi bikin untung kecil."
*   **Reality:** It caps your downside. In a 2026 volatile market, surviving is the first step to winning.
