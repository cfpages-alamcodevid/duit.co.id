**Comparison Matrix 2026:**
| Feature | REITs (DIRE) | Properti Fisik |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Control** | None (Managed by Fund Manager) | 100% (You decide rent/renovation) |
| **Leverage** | No (Usually cash-based for retail) | Yes (KPR/KPA up to 90%) |
| **Maintenance** | Passive | Active (Leaking, taxes, tenants) |
| **Liquidity** | High (Sell on IDX) | Low (Takes months) |
| **Tax** | 0% on Dividend (if reinvested) | 10% on Rent, 2.5% on Sale |

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **REITs:** Regulated by OJK (POJK No. 64/2017). Buying via Securities account.
*   **Fisik:** Regulated by BPN and Notary AJB system.
*   **2026 Note:** New "Fractional Property" regulations allow people to buy 1% of a villa in Bali via blockchain-based platforms—a hybrid between REITs and Physical.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "DIRE itu sama dengan saham properti (e.g. BSDE, PWON)."
*   **Reality:** No. Property stocks are development companies (sell houses). DIREs are holding companies (collect rent). DIREs are legally required to distribute 90% of profit as dividends.
*   **Myth:** "Beli properti fisik selalu lebih untung karena harga tanah naik."
*   **Reality:** Land prices rise, but physical property depreciates (building gets old). DIREs often have the capital to renovate assets continuously.
