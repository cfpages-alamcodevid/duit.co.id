### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Requirement:** SHM (Sertifikat Hak Milik). AVOID Girik/AJB only for long-term investment.
*   **Requirement:** Cek Zonasasi (RTRW/RDTR). Don't buy "Green Zone" (Hutan Lindung/Pertanian) if you want to build.
*   **Process:** Check certificate authenticity at BPN (Sentuh Tanahku app).
*   **Authority:** Kementerian ATR/BPN.

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies

*   **Expert Quote:** "Land doesn't rot, but it can be stolen (serobotan). In 2026, the biggest risk is overlapping certificates. Always use a trusted Notary and verify with BPN's digital system." - Land Law Specialist.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**Strategy: "Land Banking" vs "Land Flipping"**
- **Banking:** Buy and hold for 10 years in the path of progress (Infrastructure).
- **Flipping:** Buy raw land, split into smaller kavelings (plots), add basic infrastructure (roads, drains), and sell at 50-80% markup within 1-2 years.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Beli tanah pasti untung."
*   **Reality:** If the area doesn't develop or is in a flood/conflict zone, prices can stagnate for decades.
*   **Myth:** "AJB sudah cukup kuat."
*   **Reality:** AJB is just a sales deed. You need SHM to be legally secure in 2026.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Trend Analysis.** Follow government infrastructure plans (PUPR).
2.  **Step 2: Physical Survey.** Check for signs of dispute, flooding, and access.
3.  **Step 3: Legal Due Diligence.** Certificate check, Tax (PBB) clearance, Zoning check.
4.  **Step 4: Boundary Marking.** Build a fence or markers to prevent land grabbing.
