### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
```
Requirement: Business license (Izin Usaha Industri Kecil)
Regulation: UU No. 5/2017 tentang Pembentukan Peraturan Pelaksana
Process: Apply to local DPMPTSP (Dinas Penanaman Modal dan Pelayanan Terpadu Satu Pintu)
Cost: Rp 500,000 - 2,000,000 (depending on location)
Timeline: 2-4 weeks
Authority: Local government

Requirement: Water safety certification
Regulation: SNI 03-7063-2005 tentang Keselamatan Kolam Renang
Process: Inspection by local health department (Dinas Kesehatan)
Cost: Rp 2,000,000 - 5,000,000
Timeline: 1-2 months
Authority: Local health department

Requirement: Environmental permit (BPPT)
Regulation: UU No. 32/2009 tentang Perlindungan dan Pengelolaan Lingkungan Hidup
Process: Environmental impact assessment (AMDAL)
Cost: Rp 10,000,000 - 25,000,000
Timeline: 3-6 months
Authority: Local environmental agency

Requirement: Building construction permit (IMB)
Regulation: Peraturan Daerah setempat
Process: Submit architectural plans to local building department
Cost: Rp 3,000,000 - 8,000,000
Timeline: 4-8 weeks
Authority: Local building department

Requirement: Business operational permit (SIUP)
Regulation: UU No. 8/1999 tentang Perlindungan Konsumen
Process: Registration with local trade department
Cost: Rp 1,000,000 - 3,000,000
Timeline: 2-3 weeks
Authority: Local trade department
```

```
Why it matters: Biaya maintenance bisa dikelola dengan budget yang terencana (15-20% dari pendapatan)

Reality: Lokasi perumahan dengan penduduk padat bisa lebih menguntungkan karena demand lokal
```
