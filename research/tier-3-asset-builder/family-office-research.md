### 4. Market Research
**Family Office Models 2026:**
1.  **Single-Family Office (SFO):** Manages wealth for one family only. High privacy, high cost.
2.  **Multi-Family Office (MFO):** Manages multiple families. Shares costs, access to institutional deals.
3.  **Virtual Family Office:** Using a network of external advisors instead of full-time staff.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Regulation:** Rencana Peraturan Pemerintah (PP) tentang Family Office (In progress 2025/2026).
*   **Process:** Registration in the Special Economic Zone (KEK).
*   **Note:** Potential use of "Common Law" style arbitration for legal disputes in the FO hub.
*   **Authority:** OJK and Coordinating Ministry for Maritime and Investment Affairs.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Family Office itu cuma buat orang super kaya (Triliuner)."
*   **Reality:** While it started that way, Virtual/Multi-Family Offices are now accessible for those with wealth in the US$ 5M - 10M range.
*   **Myth:** "Family Office cuma cara buat hindari pajak."
*   **Reality:** It's about 'Wealth Preservation' and 'Efficiency', including legal protection and professional management, not just tax avoidance.
