### 4. Market Research
**Model 1: Owner-Operator (Self Managed)**
*   **Pros:** 100% of profit stays with owner; full control over staff and quality.
*   **Cons:** High time commitment (20-40 hours/week); requires skill in hiring/firing and inventory management.
*   **Best for:** Retired individuals, career changers, or those with family members to help.

**Model 2: Auto-Pilot (Franchisor Managed)**
*   **Pros:** Truly passive; managed by pros; system-driven.
*   **Cons:** Shared profits; "Blind" investment (rely on franchisor's reports); risk of franchisor neglecting your outlet if they have 100+ others.
*   **Best for:** High-earning professionals, doctors, executives, or multi-asset investors.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements
*   **Contract Focus:** 
    *   **Audit Rights:** Clause allowing franchisee to perform independent audits.
    *   **Transparency:** Clause requiring real-time POS (Point of Sale) access.
    *   **Management Liability:** Who pays if the manager steals or if there's a fine?
*   **Taxation:** PPh 23 (for management fees) vs PPh Final (on revenue/dividends).

### 6. Expert Opinions & Case Studies
*   **Expert Quote:** "Autopilot is only as good as the reporting system. If you can't see the sales in real-time from your phone, it's not autopilot—it's a black hole." - Retail Consultant.
*   **Case Study:** "Bambang, Manager." Invested in an autopilot coffee shop. Profit sharing 50/50. He didn't check the POS for 6 months. Found out the manager was "forgetting" to input cash sales. Net loss of Rp 100jt. Lesson: Trust, but verify.

### 7. Practical Examples & Scenarios
**The "Time-Value" Calculation:**
*   **Scenario A (Operator):** Profit 30jt/mo, but requires 100 hours of your time. Your hourly value is 200rb/hr. "Cost of Time" = 20jt. Real Profit = 10jt.
*   **Scenario B (Autopilot):** Profit 15jt/mo, requires 2 hours of your time. Your "Cost of Time" = 400rb. Real Profit = 14.6jt.
*   **Conclusion:** If you are a high earner, Autopilot is mathematically better even with lower nominal returns.

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Autopilot itu 100% pasif."
*   **Reality:** You still need to manage the manager. You are the CEO of your own investment.
*   **Myth:** "Operator itu capek tapi pasti lebih untung."
*   **Reality:** Poor management by an owner-operator can lead to higher waste/theft than the management fee of an autopilot system.
