### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "ROI = Harga Sewa / Harga Beli."
*   **Reality:** This is Gross ROI. Real ROI must include "All-in" costs including taxes, notary, and vacancy periods.
*   **Myth:** "Properti tidak pernah rugi."
*   **Reality:** High vacancy + high maintenance = negative cash flow.

### 9. Action Steps Outline
1.  **Step 1: Calculate Acquisition Cost.** Purchase price + Tax (BPHTB) + Notary + Renovation.
2.  **Step 2: Estimate Operating Expenses.** PBB, Insurance, Maintenance, Management Fee.
3.  **Step 3: Factor in Vacancy Rate.** Assume 10-15% (1.5 months/year).
4.  **Step 4: Calculate Net ROI.** (Annual Rent - Expenses) / Total Acquisition Cost.
