### 4. Market Research
**The Essential Formulas:**
1.  **Gross Rental Yield:** (Annual Rent / Purchase Price) x 100%.
2.  **Cap Rate (Net):** (NOI - Net Operating Income / Purchase Price) x 100%.
    *   NOI = Gross Rent - (Maintenance + Tax + Vacancy + Management).
3.  **Cash-on-Cash Return:** (Annual Cash Flow / Actual Cash Invested) x 100%.
    *   Includes KPR leverage.

**2026 Benchmark Cap Rates:**
*   **Jakarta CBD (Office/Apartment):** 3-4.5%.
*   **Tangerang/BSD (Residential):** 2-4%.
*   **Bali (Canggu/Uluwatu Villa):** 8-12%.
*   **Surabaya (Ruko):** 5-7%.

### 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### 8. Common Myths & Misconceptions
*   **Myth:** "Bunga KPR cuma 4% (Promo)."
*   **Reality:** Promo is for 1-2 years. You must calculate ROI based on the *floating* rate (10-12%) for a realistic long-term view.
*   **Myth:** "Yield 5% itu sudah untung."
*   **Reality:** If inflation is 3.5% and maintenance is 1%, your real return is 0.5%.
