## 5. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

**Requirement:** Business licenses (NIB, SIUP) and professional certifications.  
**Regulation:** UU No. 40/2007 on PT, BKPM regulations for foreign-owned practices.  
**Process:** Register via OSS, obtain certifications from IAI/HKI.  
**Cost:** Rp 5-20 juta for licenses.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months.  
**Authority:** BKPM, IAI.  
**Link:** https://oss.go.id/

**Requirement:** Tax compliance and clearances.  
**Regulation:** UU Pajak, DJP guidelines.  
**Process:** Audit past 5 years, obtain tax clearance.  
**Cost:** Rp 20-50 juta for audits.
