## 6. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

### A. Persetujuan Bangunan Gedung (PBG)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Requirement** | PBG (replaces IMB) — mandatory before construction |
| **Regulation** | PP No. 16 Tahun 2021 (derived from UU Cipta Kerja) |
| **Documents needed** | Technical plans (architectural, structural, utility); cost estimation |
| **Cost** | Rp 10–30 million (varies by region and building size) |
| **Timeline** | 2–4 weeks after complete submission |
| **Authority** | Dinas Cipta Karya, Tata Ruang, dan Pertanahan (Citata) DKI or equivalent in each region |

**Critical Alert (Feb 2026):**
- 185 out of 397 padel courts in Jakarta **do not have PBG**
- Gubernur DKI Pramono Anung has ordered **penghentian operasional, pembongkaran, and pencabutan izin usaha** for violators
- A venue in Puri Indah, West Jakarta, was **permanently sealed** for lacking PBG + building on RTH (Ruang Terbuka Hijau)
- New padel courts in Jakarta: **BANNED from residential zones**, must be in commercial zones
- New requirement: **Izin Teknis Awal from Dispora DKI** before PBG application

## 7. Asset Analysis — Land + Building as Hedge

### The "Duit.co.id Asset Builder" Thesis

The padel investment thesis for **Tier 3 (Asset Builder)** is not merely about court rental cash flow — it is about **land + building as an inflation hedge**.

1. **Land appreciation** — Padel venues in strategic locations benefit from the underlying land value increase. In Jakarta, South Jakarta land prices have appreciated 8–12% annually (2020–2025).

2. **Repurposing potential** — Padel court buildings (especially steel structures) are not single-purpose:
   - Steel frame structures can be **disassembled and moved** to another site (portable asset, not sunk cost)
   - The building can be converted into: warehouse, showroom, indoor sports hall, event space, coworking space
   - A 1,500–2,000 m² covered space with 12m ceiling height has **significant conversion value**

3. **Construction as value-add** — Building on underutilised land increases the property's NOI (Net Operating Income), which directly increases its valuation.

4. **Depreciation schedule:**

| Component | Useful Life | Salvage Value |
|---|---|---|
| Land | Infinite | Appreciates |
| Steel structure (hot-dip galvanized) | 15–20 years | High — can be resold as scrap or relocated |
| Concrete foundation | 20–30 years | Low if demolished, but adds to land value |
| Tempered glass walls | 8–12 years | Minimal |
| Synthetic turf | 6–8 years | Replacement cost Rp 55–75M |
| LED lighting | 5–7 years | Modular replacement |

5. **Risk comparison:** Padel venue on owned land is significantly less risky than on leased land. **Leasehold risk** is the #1 threat — if the lease is not renewed, all above-ground improvements could be lost (unless steel structure is relocated).

### Resale/Exit Scenarios

| Scenario | Outcome |
|---|---|
| **Sell to another operator** | Multiples of 3–5× annual EBITDA typical for sports facilities |
| **Sell land with improvements** | Land value + residual building value |
| **Repurpose building** | Convert to warehouse, event hall, logistics hub |
| **Relocate steel structure** | 30–40% of original structure cost recoverable |

*Source: JLL Indonesia (Kompas), PT Bumiayu Citra Raya, various*

## 17. Summary

### Key Insights for the Article Writer

1. **Padel is a genuine asset-backed business**, not a fad. It offers land + building as an inflation hedge, strong cash yield (25–35% annual ROI per Liga.Tennis data), and clear repurposing pathways.

2. **The gold rush phase is ending in Jakarta** but **just beginning in secondary cities**. The best opportunities for new investors in 2026 are in Banten, Bandung, Surabaya, and Makassar — not South Jakarta.

3. **Regulatory risk is the #1 blind spot.** 185 of 397 courts in Jakarta operate without PBG. New rules ban residential-zone padel courts. PBJT 10% tax must be factored into pricing. **Any venue without proper permits risks demolition.**

4. **The financial model works best at 4+ courts** with diversified revenue (coaching + F&B + membership). Single-court venues struggle to achieve positive unit economics.

5. **Construction quality determines ROI.** Steel structures (vs concrete) preserve asset value, allow relocation, and speed up construction by 30–50%.

6. **The market is growing 74% annually** but is not immune to correction. Differentiation through community building, coaching programs, and lifestyle integration is essential.
